
# Title 19— CUSTOMS DUTIES
## SUBTITLE III— ADMINISTRATIVE PROVISIONS
#### § 1558. No remission or refund after release of merchandise
##### (b) Payment of duties required notwithstanding export or destruction of articles; exception

When articles are exported or destroyed under customs supervision after once having been released from customs custody, as provided for in subsection (h)  1 of section 1304 of this title , such exportation or destruction shall not exempt such articles from the payment of duties other than the marking duty provided for in such subsection (h).
