
# Title 19— CUSTOMS DUTIES
## SUBTITLE VI— SOFTWOOD LUMBER
### CHAPTER 28— TRADE FACILITATION AND TRADE ENFORCEMENT
#### § 4316. Commercial Customs Operations Advisory Committee
##### (d) Meetings

Notwithstanding section 1009(f) of title 5 , the Advisory Committee shall meet at the call of the Secretary of the Treasury and the Secretary of Homeland Security, or at the call of not less than ⅔ of the membership of the Advisory Committee. The Advisory Committee shall meet at least 4 times each calendar year.
