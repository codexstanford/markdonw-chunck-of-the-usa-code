
# Title 19— CUSTOMS DUTIES
## SUBTITLE VI— SOFTWOOD LUMBER
### CHAPTER 10— CUSTOMS SERVICE
#### § 2072. Officers and employees
##### (b) Absence or disability of Commissioner

The Secretary of the Treasury is authorized to designate an officer of the United States Customs Service to act as Commissioner of Customs, 1 during the absence or disability of the Commissioner of Customs, 1 or in the event that there is no Commissioner of Customs. 1
