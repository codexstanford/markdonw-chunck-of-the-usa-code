
# Title 43— PUBLIC LANDS
### CHAPTER 35— FEDERAL LAND POLICY AND MANAGEMENT
#### § 1735. Forfeitures and deposits
##### (b) Expenditure of moneys collected administering Oregon and California Railroad and Coos Bay Wagon Road Grant lands

Any moneys collected under this Act in connection with lands administered under the Act of August 28, 1937 ( 50 Stat. 874 ; 43 U.S.C. 1181a–118 1j), 1 shall be expended for the benefit of such land only.
