
# Title 43— PUBLIC LANDS
### CHAPTER 31— DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR
#### § 1471h. Availability of appropriations for uniforms or allowances

Appropriations available to the Department of the Interior for salaries and expenses shall on and after December 8, 2004 , be available for uniforms or allowances therefor, as authorized by law ( 5 U.S.C. 5901–590 2 and D.C. Code 4–204).
