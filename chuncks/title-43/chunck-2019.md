
# Title 43— PUBLIC LANDS
### CHAPTER 35— FEDERAL LAND POLICY AND MANAGEMENT
#### § 1770. Applicability of provisions to other Federal laws
##### (c) Application of antitrust laws

(1) Nothing in this subchapter shall be construed as exempting any holder of a right-of-way issued under this subchapter from any provision of the antitrust laws of the United States.

(2) For the purposes of this subsection, the term “antitrust laws” includes the Act of July 2, 1890 (26 Stat. 1 15 U.S.C. 1 et seq.); the Act of October 15, 1914 ( 38 Stat. 730 , 15 U.S.C. 12 et seq.); the Federal Trade Commission Act ( 38 Stat. 717 ; 15 U.S.C. 41 et seq.); and sections 73 and 74 of the Act of August 27, 1894 [ 15 U.S.C. 8 , 9].
