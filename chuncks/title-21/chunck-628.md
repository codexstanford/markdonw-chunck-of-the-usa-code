
# Title 21— FOOD AND DRUGS
### CHAPTER 9— FEDERAL FOOD, DRUG, AND COSMETIC ACT
#### § 356i. Prompt reports of marketing status
##### (d) Failure to meet requirements

If a holder of an approved application fails to submit the information required under subsection (a), (b), or (c), the Secretary may move the application holder’s drugs from the active section of the list published under subsection  2 355(j)(7)(A) of this title to the discontinued section of the list, except that the Secretary shall remove from the list in accordance with subsection  2 355(j)(7)(C) of this title drugs the Secretary determines have been withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety of  3 effectiveness.
