
# Title 28— JUDICIARY AND JUDICIAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 97— JURISDICTIONAL IMMUNITIES OF FOREIGN STATES
#### § 1605A. Terrorism exception to the jurisdictional immunity of a foreign state
###### (b) Limitations .

An action may be brought or maintained under this section if the action is commenced, or a related action was commenced under section 1605(a)(7) (before the date of the enactment of this section) or section 589 of the Foreign Operations, Export Financing, and Related Programs Appropriations Act, 1997 (as contained in section 101(c) of division A of Public Law 104–208 ) not later than the latter of

(1) 10 years after April 24, 1996 ; or

(2) 10 years after the date on which the cause of action arose.
