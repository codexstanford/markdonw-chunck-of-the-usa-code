
# Title 28— JUDICIARY AND JUDICIAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 7— UNITED STATES COURT OF FEDERAL CLAIMS
#### § 178. Retirement of judges of the Court of Federal Claims

(a) A judge of the United States Court of Federal Claims who retires from office after attaining the age and meeting the service requirements, whether continuously or otherwise, of this subsection shall, subject to subsection (f), be entitled to receive, during the remainder of the judge’s lifetime, an annuity equal to the salary payable to Court of Federal Claims judges in regular active service.
