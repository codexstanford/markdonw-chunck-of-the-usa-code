
# Title 28— JUDICIARY AND JUDICIAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 180— ASSUMPTION OF CERTAIN CONTRACTUAL OBLIGATIONS
#### § 4001. Assumption of contractual obligations related to transfers of rights in motion pictures
###### (c) Exclusion for Grants of Security Interests .

Subsection (a) shall not apply to

(1) a transfer of copyright ownership consisting solely of a mortgage, hypothecation, or other security interest; or

(2) a subsequent transfer of the copyright ownership secured by the security interest described in paragraph (1) by or under the authority of the secured party, including a transfer through the exercise of the secured party’s rights or remedies as a secured party, or by a subsequent transferee. The exclusion under this subsection shall not affect any rights or remedies under law or contract.
