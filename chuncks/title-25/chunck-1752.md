
# Title 25— INDIANS
### CHAPTER 31— NATIVE AMERICAN LANGUAGES
#### § 2907. Survey on Native American languages
##### (a) In general

Not later than 18 months after January 5, 2023 , and every 5 years thereafter, the Secretary of Health and Human Services, acting through the Commissioner of the Administration for Native Americans (referred to in this section as the “Secretary”), shall undertake a survey of the use of all Native American languages in the United States.
