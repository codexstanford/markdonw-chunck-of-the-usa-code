
# Title 25— INDIANS
### CHAPTER 33— NATIONAL INDIAN FOREST RESOURCES MANAGEMENT
#### § 3105. Forest management deduction
##### (b) Amount of deduction

Deductions made pursuant to subsection (a) shall not exceed the lesser amount of

(1) 10 percent of gross proceeds, or

(2) the percentage of gross proceeds collected on November 28, 1990 , as forest management deductions by the Secretary on such sales of Indian forest products, unless the appropriate Indian tribe consents to an increase in the deductions.
