
# Title 25— INDIANS
### CHAPTER 27— TRIBALLY CONTROLLED SCHOOL GRANTS
#### § 2502. Grants authorized
##### (d) No requirement to accept grants

Nothing in this chapter may be construed

(1) to require a tribe or tribal organization to apply for or accept; or

(2) to allow any person to coerce any tribe or tribal organization to apply for, or accept, a grant under this chapter to plan, conduct, and administer all of, or any portion of, any Bureau program. Such applications and the timing of such applications shall be strictly voluntary. Nothing in this chapter may be construed as allowing or requiring any grant with any entity other than the entity to which the grant is provided.
