
# Title 29— LABOR
## Subtitle B— Regulatory Provisions
#### § 1142. Advisory Council on Employee Welfare and Pension Benefit Plans
##### (b) Duties and functions

It shall be the duty of the Council to advise the Secretary with respect to the carrying out of his functions under this chapter and to submit to the Secretary recommendations with respect thereto. The Council shall meet at least four times each year and at such other times as the Secretary requests. In his annual report submitted pursuant to section 1143(b)  1 of this title, the Secretary shall include each recommendation which he has received from the Council during the preceding calendar year.
