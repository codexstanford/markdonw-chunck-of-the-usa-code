
# Title 29— LABOR
## Subtitle B— Regulatory Provisions
#### § 1053. Minimum vesting standards
##### (b) Computation of period of service
######### (i) In the case of each individual who is absent from work for any period

(I) by reason of the pregnancy of the individual,

(II) by reason of the birth of a child of the individual,

(III) by reason of the placement of a child with the individual in connection with the adoption of such child by such individual, or

(IV) for purposes of caring for such child for a period beginning immediately following such birth or placement, the plan shall treat as hours of service, solely for purposes of determining under this paragraph whether a 1-year break in service has occurred, the hours described in clause (ii).
