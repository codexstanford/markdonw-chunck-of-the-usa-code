
# Title 29— LABOR
## Subtitle B— Regulatory Provisions
#### § 1169. Additional standards for group health plans
##### (d) Continued coverage of costs of a pediatric vaccine under group health plans

A group health plan may not reduce its coverage of the costs of pediatric vaccines (as defined under section 1928(h)(6) of the Social Security Act [ 42 U.S.C. 1396s(h)(6) ] as amended by section 13830  2 of the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act of 1993) below the coverage it provided as of May 1, 1993 .
