
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 7— SOCIAL SECURITY
#### § 403. Reduction of insurance benefits
##### (e) Occurrence of more than one event

If more than one of the events specified in subsections (c) and (d) and section 422(b)  1 of this title occurs in any one month which would occasion deductions equal to a benefit for such month, only an amount equal to such benefit shall be deducted.
