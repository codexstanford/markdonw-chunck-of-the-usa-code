
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 164— SPEAK OUT ACT
#### § 19403. Limitation on judicial enforceability of nondisclosure and nondisparagement contract clauses relating to sexual assault disputes and sexual harassment disputes
##### (c) Continued applicability of Federal, State, and Tribal Law

This chapter shall not be construed to supersede a provision of Federal, State, or Tribal Law that governs the use of pseudonyms in the filing of claims involving sexual assault or sexual harassment disputes.
