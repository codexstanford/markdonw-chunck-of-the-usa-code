
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 85— AIR POLLUTION PREVENTION AND CONTROL
#### § 7437. Greenhouse gas air pollution plans and implementation grants
##### (d) Definitions

In this section:

(1) Eligible entity

The term “eligible entity” means

(A) a State;

(B) an air pollution control agency;

(C) a municipality;

(D) an Indian tribe; and

(E) a group of one or more entities listed in subparagraphs (A) through (D).

(2) Greenhouse gas

The term “greenhouse gas” means the air pollutants carbon dioxide, hydrofluorocarbons, methane, nitrous oxide, perfluorocarbons, and sulfur hexafluoride.
