
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 6A— PUBLIC HEALTH SERVICE
#### § 210–1. Annual and sick leave
##### (a) Regulations

In accordance with regulations of the President, commissioned officers of the Regular Corps and officers of the Ready Reserve Corps on active duty may be granted annual leave and sick leave without any deductions from their pay and allowances: Provided , That such regulations shall not authorize annual leave to be accumulated in excess of sixty days.
