
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 66— DOMESTIC VOLUNTEER SERVICES
#### § 5001. Grants and contracts for volunteer service projects
##### (e) Duration of grant or contract; competitive process

(1) Beginning with fiscal year 2013 and for each fiscal year thereafter, each grant or contract awarded under this section, for such a year, shall be

(A) awarded for a period of 3 years, with an option for a grant renewal of 3 years if the grantee meets the performances  1 measures established under subsection (g); and

(B) awarded through a competitive process described in paragraph (2).

(2)

(A) The Corporation shall promulgate regulations establishing the competitive process required under paragraph (1)(B), and make such regulations available to the public, not later than 18 months after April 21, 2009 . The Corporation shall consult with the directors of programs receiving grants under this section during the development and implementation of the competitive process.
