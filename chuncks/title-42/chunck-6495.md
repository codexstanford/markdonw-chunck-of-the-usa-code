
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 7— SOCIAL SECURITY
#### § 1320a. Uniform reporting systems for health services facilities and organizations
##### (c) Availability of information to appropriate agencies and organizations

The Secretary shall provide information obtained through use of the uniform reporting systems described in subsection (a) in a useful manner and format to appropriate agencies and organizations, including health systems agencies (designated under section 300 l –4  1 of this title) and State health planning and development agencies (designated under section 300m  1 of this title), as may be necessary to carry out such agencies’ and organizations’ functions.
