
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 7— SOCIAL SECURITY
#### § 804. Coronavirus capital projects fund
##### (d) Definitions

In this section:

(1) Secretary

The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Treasury.

(2) State

The term “State” means each of the 50 States, the District of Columbia, and Puerto Rico.

(3) Tribal government

The term “Tribal government” has the meaning given such term in section 802(g) of this title .
