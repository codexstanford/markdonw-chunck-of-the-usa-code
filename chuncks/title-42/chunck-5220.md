
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 6A— PUBLIC HEALTH SERVICE
#### § 300jj–33. State grants to promote health information technology
##### (c) Implementation grants

The Secretary may award a grant to a State or qualified State designated  1 entity that

(1) has submitted, and the Secretary has approved, a plan described in subsection (e) (regardless of whether such plan was prepared using amounts awarded under subsection (b);  2 and

(2) submits an application at such time, in such manner, and containing such information as the Secretary may specify.
