
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 148— WINDSTORM IMPACT REDUCTION
#### § 15704. National Advisory Committee on Windstorm Impact Reduction
##### (e) Charter

Notwithstanding section 1013(b)(2) of title 5 , the Advisory Committee shall not be required to file a charter subsequent to its initial charter, filed under section 1008(c) of title 5 , before the termination date specified in subsection (f) of this section.
