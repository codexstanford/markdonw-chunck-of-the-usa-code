
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 8— LOW-INCOME HOUSING
#### § 1437g. Public housing Capital and Operating Funds
##### (a) Merger into Capital Fund

Except as otherwise provided in the Quality Housing and Work Responsibility Act of 1998, any assistance made available for public housing under section 1437 l of this title before October 1, 1999 , shall be merged into the Capital Fund established under subsection (d).
