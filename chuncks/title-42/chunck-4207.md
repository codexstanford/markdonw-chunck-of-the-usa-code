
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 6A— PUBLIC HEALTH SERVICE
#### § 300w–2. Payments under allotments to States

(a)

(1) For each fiscal year, the Secretary shall make payments, as provided by section 6503(a) of title 31 , to each State from its allotment under section 300w–1 of this title (other than any amount reserved under section 300w–1(d) of this title ) from amounts appropriated for that fiscal year.

(2) Any amount paid to a State for a fiscal year and remaining unobligated at the end of such year shall remain available for the next fiscal year to such State for the purposes for which it was made.
