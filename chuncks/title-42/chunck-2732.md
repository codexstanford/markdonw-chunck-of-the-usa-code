
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 6A— PUBLIC HEALTH SERVICE
#### § 290ii–2. Regulations and enforcement
##### (a) Training

Not later than 1 year after October 17, 2000 , the Secretary, after consultation with appropriate State and local protection and advocacy organizations, physicians, facilities, and other health care professionals and patients, shall promulgate regulations that require facilities to which the Protection and Advocacy for Mentally Ill Individuals Act of 1986  1 ( 42 U.S.C. 10801 et seq.) applies, to meet the requirements of subsection (b).
