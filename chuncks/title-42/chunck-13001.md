
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 55— NATIONAL ENVIRONMENTAL POLICY
#### § 4370m–8. Funding for governance, oversight, and processing of environmental reviews and permits
##### (b) Reasonable costs

As used in this section, the term “reasonable costs” shall include costs to implement the requirements and authorities required under sections 4370m–1 through 4370m–7 of this title, including the costs to agencies and the costs of operating the Council.
