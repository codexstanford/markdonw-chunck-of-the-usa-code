
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 7— SOCIAL SECURITY
#### § 1395lll. Standardized post-acute care (PAC) assessment data for quality, payment, and discharge planning
##### (l) Limitation

There shall be no administrative or judicial review under sections 1395ff and 1395 oo of this title or otherwise of the specification of standardized patient assessment data required, the determination of measures, and the systems to report such standardized data under this section.
