
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 161— DEPARTMENT OF ENERGY RESEARCH AND INNOVATION
#### § 18612. Restoration of laboratory directed research and development program
##### (b) Exception for national security laboratories

This section shall not apply to the national security laboratories with respect to which section 3119  1 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2017 ( Public Law 114–328 ) applies.
