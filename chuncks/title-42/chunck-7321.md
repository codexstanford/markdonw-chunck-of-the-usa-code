
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 7— SOCIAL SECURITY
#### § 1395p. Enrollment periods
##### (f) Individuals deemed enrolled in medical insurance program

Any individual

(1) who is eligible under section 1395 o (a) of this title to enroll in the medical insurance program by reason of entitlement to hospital insurance benefits as described in paragraph (1) of such section, and

(2) whose initial enrollment period under subsection (d) begins after March 31, 1973 , and

(3) who is residing in the United States, exclusive of Puerto Rico, shall be deemed to have enrolled in the medical insurance program established by this part.
