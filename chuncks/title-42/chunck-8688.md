
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 7— SOCIAL SECURITY
#### § 1396w–3. Enrollment simplification and coordination with State health insurance exchanges
##### (a) Condition for participation in Medicaid

As a condition of the State plan under this subchapter and receipt of any Federal financial assistance under section 1396b(a) of this title for calendar quarters beginning after January 1, 2014 , a State shall ensure that the requirements of subsection (b) is  1 met.
