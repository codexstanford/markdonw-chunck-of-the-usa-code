
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 6A— PUBLIC HEALTH SERVICE
#### § 286. National Library of Medicine
##### (g) “Medicine” and “medical” defined

For purposes of this part, the terms “medicine” and “medical”, except when used in section 286a of this title , include preventive and therapeutic medicine, dentistry, pharmacy, hospitalization, nursing, public health, and the fundamental sciences related thereto, and other related fields of study, research, or ac­tivity.
