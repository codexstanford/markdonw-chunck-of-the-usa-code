
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 6A— PUBLIC HEALTH SERVICE
#### § 254t. Demonstration project
##### (b) Procedure

An individual that receives assistance under this section with regard to the program described in section 254 l –1 of this title shall comply with all rules and requirements described in such section (other than subparagraphs (A) and (B) of section 254 l –1(b)(1) of this title) in order to receive assistance under this section.
