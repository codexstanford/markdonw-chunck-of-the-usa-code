
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 96— BIOMASS ENERGY AND ALCOHOL FUELS
#### § 8835. General requirements regarding financial assistance
##### (e) Deposit of amounts received

All amounts received by the Secretary of Energy as fees, interest, repayment of principal, and any other moneys received by the Secretary from operations under section 8832, 8833, or 8834 of this title shall be deposited in the general fund of  1 Treasury of the United States as miscellaneous receipts.
