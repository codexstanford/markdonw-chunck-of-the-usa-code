
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 21— CIVIL RIGHTS
#### § 2000e–16c. Coverage of previously exempt State employees
##### (e) Attorney’s fees

If the individual referred to in subsection (a) is the prevailing party in a proceeding under this subsection, 1 attorney’s fees may be allowed by the court in accordance with the standards prescribed under section 2000e–5(k) of this title .
