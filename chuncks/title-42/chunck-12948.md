
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 55— NATIONAL ENVIRONMENTAL POLICY
#### § 4369. Miscellaneous reports
##### (c) Comment by Government agencies and the public

The reports provided for in section 5910  1 of this title shall be made available to the public for comment, and to the heads of affected agencies for comment and, in the case of recommendations for action, for response.
