
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 66— DOMESTIC VOLUNTEER SERVICES
#### § 5001. Grants and contracts for volunteer service projects
##### (c) Conditions upon award of grant or contract

The Director shall not award any grant or contract under this part for a project in any State to any agency or organization unless, if such State has a State agency established or designated pursuant to section 3025(a)(1) of this title , such agency itself is the recipient of the award or such agency has been afforded at least forty-five days in which to review the proj­ect application and make recommendations thereon.
