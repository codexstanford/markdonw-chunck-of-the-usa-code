
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 6A— PUBLIC HEALTH SERVICE
#### § 285l–5. Application
##### (c) Limitation

Nothing in sections 285 l –2 to 285 l –5 of this title shall be construed to require a manufacturer that is currently not required to perform animal testing to perform such tests. Nothing in sections 285 l –2 to 285 l –5 of this title shall be construed to require a manufacturer to perform redundant endpoint specific testing.
