
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 6A— PUBLIC HEALTH SERVICE
#### § 300d–71. Maintenance of the national toll-free number and other communication capabilities
##### (c) Authorization of appropriations
