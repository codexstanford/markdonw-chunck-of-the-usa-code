
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 110— FAMILY VIOLENCE PREVENTION AND SERVICES
#### § 10409. Grants for Indian tribes
##### (a) Grants authorized

The Secretary, in consultation with tribal governments pursuant to Executive Order No. 13175 ( 25 U.S.C. 450 note)  1 and in accordance with section 20126 of title 34 , shall continue to award grants for Indian tribes from amounts appropriated under section 10403(a)(2)(B) of this title to carry out this section.
