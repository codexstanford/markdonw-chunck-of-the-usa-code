
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 7— SOCIAL SECURITY
#### § 1396o. Use of enrollment fees, premiums, deductions, cost sharing, and similar charges
##### (h) Indexing nominal cost sharing

In applying this section and subsections (c) and (e) of section 1396 o –1 of this title, with respect to cost sharing that is “nominal” in amount, the Secretary shall increase such “nominal” amounts for each year (beginning with 2006) by the annual percentage increase in the medical care component of the consumer price index for all urban consumers (U.S. city average) as rounded up in an appropriate manner.
