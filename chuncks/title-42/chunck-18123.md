
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 105— COMMUNITY SERVICES PROGRAMS
#### § 9840a. Early Head Start programs
##### (c) Persons eligible to participate

Persons who may participate in programs described in subsection (a) include

(1) pregnant women; and

(2) families with children under age 3; who meet the eligibility criteria specified in section 9840(a)(1) of this title , including the criteria specified in section 9840(a)(1)(B)(ii) of this title .
