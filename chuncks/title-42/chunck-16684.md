
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 85— AIR POLLUTION PREVENTION AND CONTROL
#### § 7651e. Allowances for States with emissions rates at or below 0.80 lbs/mmBtu
##### (b) Notification of Administrator

Pursuant to section 7651b(a)(1) of this title , each Governor of a State eligible to make an election under paragraph  1 (a) shall notify the Administrator of such election. In the event that the Governor of any such State fails to notify the Administrator of the Governor’s elections, the Administrator shall allocate allowances pursuant to section 7651d of this title .
