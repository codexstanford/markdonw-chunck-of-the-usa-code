
# Title 42— THE PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE
### CHAPTER 85— AIR POLLUTION PREVENTION AND CONTROL
#### § 7409. National primary and secondary ambient air quality standards
##### (c) National primary ambient air quality standard for nitrogen dioxide

The Administrator shall, not later than one year after August 7, 1977 , promulgate a national primary ambient air quality standard for NO 2 concentrations over a period of not more than 3 hours unless, based on the criteria issued under section 7408(c) of this title , he finds that there is no significant evidence that such a standard for such a period is requisite to protect public health.
