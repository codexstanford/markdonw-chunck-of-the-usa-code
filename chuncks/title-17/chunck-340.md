
# Title 17— COPYRIGHTS
### CHAPTER 13— PROTECTION OF ORIGINAL DESIGNS
#### § 1310. Application for registration
###### (e) Sworn Statement .

The application for registration shall be accompanied by a statement under oath by the applicant or the applicant’s duly authorized agent or representative, setting forth, to the best of the applicant’s knowledge and belief

(1) that the design is original and was created by the designer or designers named in the application;

(2) that the design has not previously been registered on behalf of the applicant or the applicant’s predecessor in title; and

(3) that the applicant is the person entitled to protection and to registration under this chapter. If the design has been made public with the design notice prescribed in section 1306, the statement shall also describe the exact form and position of the design notice.
