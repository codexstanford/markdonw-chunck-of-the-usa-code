
# Title 17— COPYRIGHTS
### CHAPTER 14— UNAUTHORIZED USE OF PRE-1972 SOUND RECORDINGS
#### § 1401. Unauthorized use of pre-1972 sound recordings
###### (j) Rule of Construction .

A rights owner of, or featured recording artist who performs on, a sound recording under this chapter shall be deemed to be an interested copyright party, as defined in section 1001, to the same extent as a copyright owner or featured recording artist under chapter 10.
