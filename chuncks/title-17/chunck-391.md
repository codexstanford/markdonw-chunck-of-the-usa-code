
# Title 17— COPYRIGHTS
### CHAPTER 14— UNAUTHORIZED USE OF PRE-1972 SOUND RECORDINGS
#### § 1401. Unauthorized use of pre-1972 sound recordings
###### (g) Application of Section 230 Safe Harbor .

For purposes of section 230 of the Communications Act of 1934 ( 47 U.S.C. 230 ), subsection (a) shall be considered to be a “law pertaining to intellectual property” under subsection (e)(2) of such section 230.
