
# Title 17— COPYRIGHTS
### CHAPTER 7— COPYRIGHT OFFICE
#### § 710. Emergency relief authority
###### (c) Statement Required .

Except as provided in subsection (d), not later than 20 days after taking any action that results in a provision being modified for a cumulative total of longer than 120 days, the Register shall submit to Congress a statement detailing the action taken, the relevant background, and rationale for the action.
