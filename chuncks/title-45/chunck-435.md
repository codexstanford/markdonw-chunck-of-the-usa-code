
# Title 45— RAILROADS
### CHAPTER 16— REGIONAL RAIL REORGANIZATION
#### § 797i. Limitations on liability
##### (a) Federal Government

The liability of the United States under an agreement entered into or benefit schedule prescribed under section 797  1 of this title or for payment of a termination allowance under section 797a of this title shall be limited to amounts appropriated under section 797 l 1 of this title.
