
# Title 45— RAILROADS
### CHAPTER 19— ROCK ISLAND RAILROAD EMPLOYEE ASSISTANCE
#### § 1012. Publications and reports

Within 45 days after May 30, 1980 , the Board shall publish, and make available for distribution by the Rock Island Railroad to all eligible employees, a document which describes in detail the rights of such employees under sections 1005, 1006, 1 1007, and 1014 of this title.
