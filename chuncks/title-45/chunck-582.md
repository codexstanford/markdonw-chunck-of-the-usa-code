
# Title 45— RAILROADS
### CHAPTER 21— ALASKA RAILROAD TRANSFER
#### § 1212. Applicability of other laws
##### (a) Actions subject to other laws

The provisions of chapter 5 of title 5 (popularly known as the Administrative Procedure Act, and including provisions popularly known as the Government in the Sunshine Act), chapter 10 of title 5, division A of subtitle III of title 54, section 303 of title 49 , and the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 ( 42 U.S.C. 4321 et seq.) shall not apply to actions taken pursuant to this chapter, except to the extent that such laws may be applicable to granting of rights-of-way under section 1208 of this title .
