
# Title 15— COMMERCE AND TRADE
### CHAPTER 14A— AID TO SMALL BUSINESS
#### § 650. Supervisory and enforcement authority for small business lending companies
##### (h) Appointment of receiver

(1) In any proceeding under subsection (f)(4) or subsection (g)(6)(C), 1 the court may take exclusive jurisdiction of a small business lending company or a non-Federally regulated lender and appoint a receiver to hold and administer the assets of the company or lender.

(2) Upon request of the Administrator, the court may appoint the Administrator as a receiver under paragraph (1).
