
# Title 15— COMMERCE AND TRADE
### CHAPTER 7— NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF STANDARDS AND TECHNOLOGY
#### § 272. Establishment, functions, and activities
##### (d) Management costs

In carrying out the extramural funding programs of the Institute, including the programs established under sections 278k and 278 l of this title, the Secretary may retain reasonable amounts of any funds appropriated pursuant to authorizations for these programs in order to pay for the Institute’s management of these programs.
