
# Title 15— COMMERCE AND TRADE
### CHAPTER 7— NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF STANDARDS AND TECHNOLOGY
#### § 278k–1. Competitive awards program
##### (a) Establishment

The Director shall establish within the Hollings Manufacturing Extension Partnership under section 278k of this title and section 278 l of this title a program of competitive awards among participants described in subsection (b) of this section for the purposes described in subsection (c).
