
# Title 15— COMMERCE AND TRADE
### CHAPTER 2D— INVESTMENT COMPANIES AND ADVISERS
#### § 80a–34. Unlawful representations and names
##### (c) Statement of registration under securities provisions

No provision of subsection (a) or (b) shall be construed to prohibit a statement that a person or security is registered under this chapter, the Securities Act of 1933 [ 15 U.S.C. 77a et seq.], or the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 [ 15 U.S.C. 78a et seq.], if such statement is true in fact and if the effect of such registration is not misrepresented.
