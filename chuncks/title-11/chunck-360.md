
# Title 11— BANKRUPTCY
### CHAPTER 11— REORGANIZATION
#### § 1187. Duties and reporting requirements of debtors
###### (b) Other Applicable Provisions .

A debtor, in addition to the duties provided in this title and as otherwise required by law, shall comply with the requirements of section 308 and paragraphs (2), (3), (4), (5), (6), and (7) of section 1116 of this title .
