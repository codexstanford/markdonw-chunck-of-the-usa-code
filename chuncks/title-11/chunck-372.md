
# Title 11— BANKRUPTCY
### CHAPTER 11— REORGANIZATION
#### § 1191. Confirmation of plan
###### (e) Special Rule .

Notwithstanding section 1129(a)(9)(A) of this title , a plan that provides for the payment through the plan of a claim of a kind specified in paragraph (2) or (3) of section 507(a) of this title may be confirmed under subsection (b) of this section.
