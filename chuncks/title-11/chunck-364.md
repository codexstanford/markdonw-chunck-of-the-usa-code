
# Title 11— BANKRUPTCY
### CHAPTER 11— REORGANIZATION
#### § 1188. Status conference
###### (c) Report .

Not later than 14 days before the date of the status conference under subsection (a), the debtor shall file with the court and serve on the trustee and all parties in interest a report that details the efforts the debtor has undertaken and will undertake to attain a consensual plan of reorganization.
