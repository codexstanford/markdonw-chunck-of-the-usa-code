
# Title 11— BANKRUPTCY
### CHAPTER 11— REORGANIZATION
#### § 1181. Inapplicability of other sections
###### (a) In General .

Sections 105(d), 1101(1), 1104, 1105, 1106, 1107, 1108, 1115, 1116, 1121, 1123(a)(8), 1123(c), 1127, 1129(a)(15), 1129(b), 1129(c), 1129(e), and 1141(d)(5) of this title do not apply in a case under this subchapter.
