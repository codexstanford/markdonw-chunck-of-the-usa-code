
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle II— Vessels and Seamen
### CHAPTER 151— NATIONAL MARITIME TRANSPORTATION ADVISORY COMMITTEES
#### § 15109. Administration
###### (l) Technical Assistance.

(1) In general .

The Secretary shall provide technical assistance to the Committee if requested by the Chairman.

(2) Committee consultation .

With respect to the committee established under section 15102, the Chairman of the committee shall seek expertise from the fishing industry, marine safety experts, the shipbuilding industry, and others as the committee determines appropriate.
