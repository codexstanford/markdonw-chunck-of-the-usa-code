
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle VIII— Miscellaneous
### CHAPTER 803— ICE AND DERELICTS
#### § 80302. Patrol services
###### (d) Administration .

The Commandant, under the direction of the Secretary of the department in which the Coast Guard is operating, shall carry out the services provided for in this section and shall assign necessary aircraft, material, and personnel of the Coast Guard. On request of such Secretary, the head of an agency may detail personnel, lend or contribute material or equipment, or otherwise assist in carrying out the services provided for in this section.
