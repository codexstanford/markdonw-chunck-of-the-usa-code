
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle II— Vessels and Seamen
### CHAPTER 31— GENERAL
#### § 3106. Master key control system
###### (b) Prohibited Use .

Crew not included on the list described in subsection (a)(2) shall not have access to or use the master key unless in an emergency and shall immediately notify the master and owner of the vessel following use of such key.
