
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 534— TANKER SECURITY FLEET
#### § 53405. Obligations and rights under operating agreements
###### (c) Documentation Requirement .

Each vessel covered by the operating agreement, including an agreement terminated under section 53404(c)(2), shall remain documented under chapter 121 of this title until the date the operating agreement would terminate according to its terms.
