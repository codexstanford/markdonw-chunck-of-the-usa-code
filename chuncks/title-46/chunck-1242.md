
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 513— UNITED STATES MERCHANT MARINE ACADEMY
#### § 51322. Protection of cadets from sexual assault onboard vessels
###### (g) Sea Year Survey.

(1) Requirement .

The Maritime Administrator shall require each cadet from the Academy, upon completion of the cadet’s Sea Year, to complete a survey regarding the environment and conditions during the Sea Year of the vessel to which the cadet was assigned.

(2) Availability .

The Maritime Administrator shall make available to the public for each year

(A) the questions used in the survey required by paragraph (1); and

(B) the aggregated data received from such surveys.
