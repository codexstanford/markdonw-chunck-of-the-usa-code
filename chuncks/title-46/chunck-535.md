
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle II— Vessels and Seamen
### CHAPTER 121— DOCUMENTATION OF VESSELS
#### § 12113. Fishery endorsement
###### (g) Vessels Purchased Through Fishing Capacity Reduction Program .

A vessel purchased by the Secretary of Commerce through a fishing capacity reduction program under the Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act ( 16 U.S.C. 1801 et seq.) or section 308 of the Interjurisdictional Fisheries Act of 1986 ( 16 U.S.C. 4107 ) is not eligible for a fishery endorsement, and any fishery endorsement issued for that vessel is invalid.
