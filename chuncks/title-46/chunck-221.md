
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle II— Vessels and Seamen
### CHAPTER 49— OCEANGOING NON-PASSENGER COMMERCIAL VESSELS
#### § 4901. Surveillance requirements
###### (d) Notice of Video and Audio Surveillance .

The owner of a vessel to which this section applies shall provide clear and conspicuous signs on board the vessel notifying the crew of the presence of video and audio surveillance equipment.
