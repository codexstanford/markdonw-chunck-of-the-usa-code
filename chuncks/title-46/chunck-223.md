
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle II— Vessels and Seamen
### CHAPTER 49— OCEANGOING NON-PASSENGER COMMERCIAL VESSELS
#### § 4901. Surveillance requirements
###### (f) Retention Requirements .

The owner of a vessel to which this section applies shall retain all records of audio and video surveillance for not less than 1 year after the footage is obtained. Any video and audio surveillance found to be associated with an alleged incident should be preserved for not less than 5 years from the date of the alleged incident.
