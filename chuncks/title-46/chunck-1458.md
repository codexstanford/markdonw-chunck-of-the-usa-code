
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 534— TANKER SECURITY FLEET
#### § 53408. Regulatory relief
###### (b) Other Restrictions .

The restrictions of section 55305(a) of this title concerning the building, rebuilding, or documentation of a vessel in a foreign country shall not apply to a vessel for any day the operator of the vessel is receiving payments for the operation of that vessel under an operating agreement under this chapter.
