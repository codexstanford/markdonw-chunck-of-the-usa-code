
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 521— MISCELLANEOUS
#### § 52101. Reemployment rights for certain merchant seamen
###### (d) Equivalence to Military Selective Service Act Certificate .

For purposes of reemployment rights and benefits provided by this section, a certification under subsection (c) shall be considered to be the equivalent of a certificate described in section 9(a) of the Military Selective Service Act ( 50 U.S.C. 3808(a) ).
