
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 513— UNITED STATES MERCHANT MARINE ACADEMY
#### § 51322. Protection of cadets from sexual assault onboard vessels
###### (f) Maintenance of Sexual Assault Training Records .

The Maritime Administrator shall require the owner or operator of a commercial vessel, and the seafarer union for a commercial vessel, to maintain records of sexual assault training for any person required to have such training.
