
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle IV— Regulation of Ocean Shipping
### CHAPTER 425— NATIONAL SHIPPER ADVISORY COMMITTEE
#### § 42503. Administration
###### (d) Status of Members.

(1) In general .

Except as provided in paragraph (2), with respect to a member of the Committee whom the Commission appoints to represent an entity or group

(A) the member is authorized to represent the interests of the applicable entity or group; and

(B) requirements under Federal law that would interfere with such representation and that apply to a special Government employee (as defined in section 202(a) of title 18 ), including requirements relating to employee conduct, political activities, ethics, conflicts of interest, and corruption, do not apply to the member.

(2) Exception .

Notwithstanding subsection (b), a member of the Committee shall be treated as a special Government employee for purposes of the committee service of the member if the member, without regard to service on the Committee, is a special Government employee.
