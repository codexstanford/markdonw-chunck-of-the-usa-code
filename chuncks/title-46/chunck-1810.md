
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 571— GENERAL AUTHORITY
#### § 57103. Donation of nonretention vessels in the National Defense Reserve Fleet
###### (e) Limitations .

If at any time prior to delivery of the vessel to the recipient, the Secretary determines that a different disposition of the vessel would better serve the interests of the Government, the Secretary shall pursue the more favorable disposition of the obsolete vessel and shall not be liable for any damages that may result from an intended recipient’s reliance upon a proposed transfer.
