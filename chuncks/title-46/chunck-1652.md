
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 541— MISCELLANEOUS
#### § 54101. Assistance for small shipyards
###### (e) Matching Requirements; Allocation.

(1) Federal funding .

Federal funds for any eligible project under this section shall not exceed 75 percent of the total cost of such project.

(2) Allocation of funds.

(A) In general .

The Administrator may not award more than 25 percent of the funds made available to carry out this section for any fiscal year to any small shipyard in one geographic location that has more than 600 employees.

(B) Ineligibility .

A maritime training center that has received funds awarded under section 51706 of title 46 , United States Code, shall not be eligible for grants under this subsection for training purposes in the same fiscal year.
