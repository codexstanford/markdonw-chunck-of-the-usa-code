
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle II— Vessels and Seamen
### CHAPTER 112— MERCHANT MARINER BENEFITS
#### § 11201. Eligibility for veterans’ burial and cemetery benefits
###### (b) Reimbursement for Benefits Provided .

The Secretary shall reimburse the Secretary of Veterans Affairs for the value of benefits that the Secretary of Veterans Affairs provides for an individual by reason of eligibility under this section.
