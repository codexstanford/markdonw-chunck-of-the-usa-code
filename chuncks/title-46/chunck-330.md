
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle II— Vessels and Seamen
### CHAPTER 77— SUSPENSION AND REVOCATION
#### § 7704a. Sexual harassment or sexual assault as grounds for suspension or revocation
###### (b) Sexual Assault .

If it is shown at a hearing under this chapter that a holder of a license, certificate of registry, or merchant mariner’s document issued under this part, within 10 years before the beginning of the suspension and revocation proceedings, is the subject of an official finding of sexual assault, then the license, certificate of registry, or merchant mariner’s document shall be revoked.
