
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 534— TANKER SECURITY FLEET
#### § 53403. Award of operating agreements
###### (c) Limitation .

The Secretary of Transportation may not award operating agreements under this chapter that require payments under section 53406 of this title for more than—. 1

(1) for each of fiscal years 2022 and 2023, 10 vessels; and

(2) for any subsequent fiscal year, 20 vessels.
