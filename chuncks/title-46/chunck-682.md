
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle II— Vessels and Seamen
### CHAPTER 151— NATIONAL MARITIME TRANSPORTATION ADVISORY COMMITTEES
#### § 15109. Administration
###### (h) Chairman; Vice Chairman.

(1) In general .

Each committee established under this chapter or to which this chapter applies shall elect a Chairman and Vice Chairman from among the committee’s members.

(2) Vice chairman acting as chairman .

The Vice Chairman shall act as Chairman in the absence or incapacity of, or in the event of a vacancy in the office of, the Chairman.
