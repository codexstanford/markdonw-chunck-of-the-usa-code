
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 537— LOANS AND GUARANTEES
#### § 53733. Shipyard modernization and improvement
###### (e) Transfer of Amounts .

The Administrator may accept the transfer of amounts from a department, agency, or instrumentality of the United States Government and may use those amounts to cover the cost (as defined in section 502 of the Federal Credit Reform Act of 1990 ( 2 U.S.C. 661a )) of making guarantees or commitments to guarantee under this section.
