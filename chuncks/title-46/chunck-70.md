
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle II— Vessels and Seamen
### CHAPTER 31— GENERAL
#### § 3106. Master key control system
###### (d) Penalty .

Any crew member who uses the master key without having been granted access pursuant to subsection (a)(2) shall be liable to the United States Government for a civil penalty of not more than $1,000 and may be subject to suspension or revocation under section 7703.
