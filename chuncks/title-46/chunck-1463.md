
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 534— TANKER SECURITY FLEET
#### § 53412. Acquisition of Fleet vessels
###### (a) In General .

Upon replacement of a Fleet vessel under an operating agreement under this chapter, and subject to agreement by the program participant of the vessel, the Secretary of Transportation is authorized, subject to the concurrence of the Secretary of Defense, acquire the vessel being replaced for inclusion in the National Defense Reserve Fleet.
