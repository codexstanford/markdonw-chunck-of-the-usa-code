
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle II— Vessels and Seamen
### CHAPTER 71— LICENSES AND CERTIFICATES OF REGISTRY
#### § 7106. Duration of licenses
###### (b) Advance Renewals .

A renewed license issued under this part may be issued up to 8 months in advance but is not effective until the date that the previously issued license expires or until the completion of any active suspension or revocation of that previously issued license, whichever is later.
