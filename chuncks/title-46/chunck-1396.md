
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 532— CABLE SECURITY FLEET
#### § 53207. National security requirements
###### (a) Contingency Agreement Required .

The Secretary shall include in each Operating Agreement under this chapter a requirement that the Contractor enter into a Contingency Agreement with the Operating Agency. The Operating Agency shall negotiate and enter into a Contingency Agreement with each Contractor as promptly as practicable after the Contractor has entered into an Operating Agreement under this chapter.
