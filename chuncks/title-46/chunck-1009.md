
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle IV— Regulation of Ocean Shipping
### CHAPTER 425— NATIONAL SHIPPER ADVISORY COMMITTEE
#### § 42502. National Shipper Advisory Committee
###### (c) Membership.

(1) In general .

The Committee shall consist of 24 members appointed by the Commission in accordance with this section.

(2) Expertise .

Each member of the Committee shall have particular expertise, knowledge, and experience in matters relating to the function of the Committee.

(3) Representation .

REPRESENTATION.—  1  Members of the Committee shall be appointed as follows: —  1

(A) Twelve members shall represent entities who import cargo to the United States using ocean common carriers.

(B) Twelve members shall represent entities who export cargo from the United States using ocean common carriers.
