
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle II— Vessels and Seamen
### CHAPTER 49— OCEANGOING NON-PASSENGER COMMERCIAL VESSELS
#### § 4901. Surveillance requirements
###### (e) Access to Video and Audio Records .

The owner of a vessel to which this section applies shall ensure that access to records of video and audio surveillance is not used as part of a labor action against a crew member or employment dispute unless used in a criminal or civil action.
