
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle IV— Regulation of Ocean Shipping
### CHAPTER 413— ENFORCEMENT
#### § 41305. Award of reparations
###### (c) Additional Amounts .

On a showing that the injury was caused by an activity prohibited by section subsection  1 (b) or (c) of section 41102, paragraph (3) or (6) of section 41104(a), or paragraph (1) or (3) of section 41105, the Commission may order the payment of additional amounts, but the total recovery of a complainant may not exceed twice the amount of the actual injury.
