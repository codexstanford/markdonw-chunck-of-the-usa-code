
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 532— CABLE SECURITY FLEET
#### § 53205. Obligations and rights under operating agreements
###### (f) Replacement Vessel .

A Contractor may replace a vessel under an Operating Agreement with another vessel that is eligible to be included in the Fleet under section 53202(b), if the Secretary and the Operating Agency jointly determine that the replacement vessel meets national security requirements and approve the replacement.
