
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle II— Vessels and Seamen
### CHAPTER 121— DOCUMENTATION OF VESSELS
#### § 12108. Authority to extend duration of vessel certificates
###### (a) Certificates .

Provided a vessel is in compliance with inspection requirements in section 3313, the Secretary of the department in which in the Coast Guard is operating may, if the Secretary makes the determination described in subsection (b), extend, for a period of not more than 1 year, an expiring certificate of documentation issued for a vessel under chapter 121.
