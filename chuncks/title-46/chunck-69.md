
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle II— Vessels and Seamen
### CHAPTER 31— GENERAL
#### § 3106. Master key control system
###### (c) Requirements for Log Book .

The log book described in subsection (a)(3) and required to be included in a safety management system under section 3203(a)(6)

(1) may be electronic; and

(2) shall be located in a centralized location that is readily accessible to law enforcement personnel.
