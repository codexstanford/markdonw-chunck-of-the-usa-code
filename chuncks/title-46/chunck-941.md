
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle IV— Regulation of Ocean Shipping
### CHAPTER 413— ENFORCEMENT
#### § 41302. Investigations
###### (a) In General .

The Federal Maritime Commission, on complaint or its own motion, may investigate any conduct  1 agreement, fee, or charge that the Commission believes may be in violation of this part. The Commission may by order disapprove, cancel, or modify any agreement that operates in violation of this part.
