
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle II— Vessels and Seamen
### CHAPTER 151— NATIONAL MARITIME TRANSPORTATION ADVISORY COMMITTEES
#### § 15109. Administration
###### (d) Acceptance of Volunteer Services .

A member of a committee established under this chapter or to which this chapter applies may serve on such committee on a voluntary basis without pay without regard to section 1342 of title 31 or any other law.
