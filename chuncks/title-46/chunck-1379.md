
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 532— CABLE SECURITY FLEET
#### § 53203. Award of operating agreements
###### (b) Procedure for Applications.

(1) Acceptance of applications .

Beginning no later than 60 days after the effective date of this chapter, the Secretary shall accept applications for enrollment of vessels in the Cable Fleet.

(2) Action on applications .

Within 120 days after receipt of an application for enrollment of a vessel in the Cable Fleet, the Secretary shall approve the application in conjunction with the Operating Agency, and shall enter into an Operating Agreement with the applicant, or provide in writing the reason for denial of that application.
