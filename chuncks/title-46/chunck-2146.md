
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle VII— Security and Drug Enforcement
### CHAPTER 703— MARITIME SECURITY
#### § 70306. Report on terrorist threats
###### (a) Content .

The Secretary shall submit an annual report to Congress on the threat from acts of terrorism to United States ports and vessels operating from those ports. The Secretary shall include a description of activities undertaken under title I of the Maritime Transportation Security Act of 2002 ( Public Law 107–295 , 116 Stat. 2066 ) and an analysis of the effect of those activities on port security against acts of terrorism.
