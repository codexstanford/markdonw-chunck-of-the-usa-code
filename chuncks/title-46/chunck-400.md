
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle II— Vessels and Seamen
### CHAPTER 101— GENERAL
#### § 10104. Requirement to report sexual offenses
###### (f) Applicability; Regulations.

(1) Regulations .

The Secretary may issue regulations to implement the requirements of this section.

(2) Interim reports .

Any report required to be made to the Commandant under this section shall be made to the Coast Guard National Command Center, until regulations implementing the procedures required by this section are issued.
