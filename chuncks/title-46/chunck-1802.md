
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 571— GENERAL AUTHORITY
#### § 57101. Placement of vessels in National Defense Reserve Fleet
###### (b) Removal From Fleet .

A vessel placed in the Fleet under subsection (a) may not be traded out or sold from the Fleet, except as provided in section 57102, 57103, or 57104 or chapter 533, 537, 573, or 575 of this title.
