
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 513— UNITED STATES MERCHANT MARINE ACADEMY
#### § 51318. Policy on sexual harassment, dating violence, domestic violence, sexual assault, and stalking
###### (f) Definitions .

In this section and section 51319 of this title :

(1) Dating violence; domestic violence; stalking .

The terms “dating violence”, “domestic violence”, and “stalking” have the meanings given those terms is  1 section 40002(a) of the Violence Against Women Act of 1994 ( 42 U.S.C. 13925(a) ). 2

(2) Sexual assault .

The term “sexual assault” means an offense classified as a forcible or nonforcible sex offense under the uniform crime reporting system of the Federal Bureau of Investigation.
