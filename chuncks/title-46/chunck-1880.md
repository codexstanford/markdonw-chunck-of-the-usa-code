
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 575— CONSTRUCTION, CHARTER, AND SALE OF VESSELS
#### § 57532. Operation of experimental vessels
###### (d) Rights of Seamen .

A seaman engaged in vessel operations of the Secretary under this section and employed through a general agent in connection with a charter or agreement under this section is entitled to all the rights and remedies provided in sections 1(a) and (c), 3(c), and 4 of the Act of March 24, 1943 ( 50 U.S.C. 4701(a) , (c), 4703(c), and 4704).
