
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle IV— Regulation of Ocean Shipping
### CHAPTER 413— ENFORCEMENT
#### § 41302. Investigations
###### (b) Effectiveness of Agreement, Fee, or Charge During Investigation .

Unless an injunction is issued under section 41306 or 41307 of this title, an agreement, fee, or charge under investigation by the Commission remains in effect until the Commission issues its order.
