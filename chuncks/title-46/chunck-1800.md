
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 571— GENERAL AUTHORITY
#### § 57100. National Defense Reserve Fleet
###### (g) Vessel Status .

A vessel in the National Defense Reserve Fleet determined by the Maritime Administration to be of insufficient value to remain in the National Defense Reserve Fleet shall remain a vessel within the meaning of that term in section 3 of title 1 , United States Code, and subject to the rights and responsibilities of a vessel under admiralty law at least until such time as the vessel is delivered to a dismantling facility or is disposed of otherwise from the National Defense Reserve Fleet.
