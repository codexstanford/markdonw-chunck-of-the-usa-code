
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle V— Merchant Marine
### CHAPTER 531— MARITIME SECURITY FLEET
#### § 53104. Effectiveness of operating agreements
###### (a) Effectiveness, Generally .

The Secretary may enter into an operating agreement under this chapter for fiscal year 2006. Except as provided in subsection (b), the agreement shall be effective only for 1 fiscal year, but shall be renewable, subject to the availability of appropriations, for each subsequent fiscal year through the end of fiscal year 2035.
