
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle IV— Regulation of Ocean Shipping
### CHAPTER 409— OCEAN TRANSPORTATION INTERMEDIARIES
#### § 40903. Suspension or revocation of license
###### (b) Failure To Maintain Bond, Proof of Insurance, or Other Surety .

The Commission may revoke an ocean transportation intermediary’s license for failure to maintain a bond, proof of insurance, or other surety as required by section 40902(a) of this title .
