
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle III— Maritime Liability
### CHAPTER 305— EXONERATION AND LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
#### § 30523. General limit of liability
###### (a) In General .

Except as provided in section 30524 of this title , the liability of the owner of a vessel for any claim, debt, or liability described in subsection (b) shall not exceed the value of the vessel and pending freight. If the vessel has more than one owner, the proportionate share of the liability of any one owner shall not exceed that owner’s proportionate interest in the vessel and pending freight.
