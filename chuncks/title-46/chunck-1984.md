
# Title 46— SHIPPING
## Subtitle VII— Security and Drug Enforcement
### CHAPTER 700— PORTS AND WATERWAYS SAFETY
#### § 70006. Establishment by Secretary of the department in which the Coast Guard is operating of anchorage grounds and regulations generally
###### (b) Definition .

As used in this section “navigable waters of the United States” includes all waters of the territorial sea of the United States as described in Presidential Proclamation No. 5928 of December 27, 1988 .
