
# Title 22— FOREIGN RELATIONS AND INTERCOURSE
### CHAPTER 32— FOREIGN ASSISTANCE
#### § 2291m. Program to provide assistance to build the capacity of foreign law enforcement agencies with respect to covered synthetic drugs
##### (a) In general

Notwithstanding section 2420 of this title , the Secretary of State shall establish a program to provide assistance to strengthen the capacity of law enforcement agencies of the countries described in subsection (c) to help such agencies to identify, track, and improve their forensics detection capabilities with respect to covered synthetic drugs.
