
# Title 22— FOREIGN RELATIONS AND INTERCOURSE
### CHAPTER 78— TRAFFICKING VICTIMS PROTECTION
#### § 7103b. United States Advisory Council on Human Trafficking
##### (e) Reports

Not later than 1 year after May 29, 2015 , and each year thereafter until the date described in subsection (h), 1 the Council shall submit a report that contains the findings derived from the reviews conducted pursuant to subsection (d)(2) to

(1) the chair of the Task Force;

(2) the members of the Group;

(3) the Committees on Foreign Affairs, Homeland Security, Appropriations, and the Judiciary of the House of Representatives; and

(4) the Committees on Foreign Relations, Appropriations, Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs, and the Judiciary of the Senate.
