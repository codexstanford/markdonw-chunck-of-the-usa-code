
# Title 22— FOREIGN RELATIONS AND INTERCOURSE
### CHAPTER 63— SUPPORT FOR EAST EUROPEAN DEMOCRACY (SEED)
#### § 5452. Environmental initiatives for Poland and Hungary
##### (e) Funding of EPA activities

To enable the Environmental Protection Agency to carry out subsections (b), (c), and (d), there are authorized to be appropriated $10,000,000 for the 3-year period beginning October 1, 1989 , to carry out chapter 1 of part I of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961 ( 22 U.S.C. 2151 and following; relating to development assistance) or chapter 4 of Part  1 II of that Act ( 22 U.S.C. 2346 and following; relating to the economic support fund). These funds may be used to carry out those subsections notwithstanding any provision of law relating to the use of foreign assistance funds.
