
# Title 22— FOREIGN RELATIONS AND INTERCOURSE
### CHAPTER 48A— TAIWAN ENHANCED RESILIENCE
#### § 3362. Task force to counter economic coercion by the People’s Republic of China
##### (b) Establishment of Task Force

Not later than 180 days after December 23, 2022 , the President shall establish an interagency task force to be known as the “Countering Economic Coercion Task Force” (referred to in this section as the “Task Force”).
