
# Title 22— FOREIGN RELATIONS AND INTERCOURSE
### CHAPTER 48A— TAIWAN ENHANCED RESILIENCE
#### § 3351. Modernizing Taiwan’s security capabilities to deter and, if necessary, defeat aggression by the People’s Republic of China
##### (b) Taiwan security programs

The Secretary of State, in consultation with the Secretary of Defense, shall use the authorities under this section to strengthen the United States-Taiwan defense relationship, and to support the acceleration of the modernization of Taiwan’s defense capabilities, consistent with the Taiwan Relations Act ( Public Law 96–8 ) [ 22 U.S.C. 3301 et seq.].
