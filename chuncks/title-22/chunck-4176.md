
# Title 22— FOREIGN RELATIONS AND INTERCOURSE
### CHAPTER 52— FOREIGN SERVICE
#### § 3922. Utilization of Foreign Service personnel system by other agencies
###### (b) Subject to section 3921(b) of this title

(1) the agency heads referred to in subsection (a), and

(2) the head of any other agency (to the extent authority to utilize the Foreign Service personnel system is granted to such agency head under any other chapter), shall in the case of their respective agencies exercise the functions vested in the Secretary by this chapter.
