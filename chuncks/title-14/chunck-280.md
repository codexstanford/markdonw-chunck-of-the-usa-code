
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE I— ESTABLISHMENT, POWERS, DUTIES, AND ADMINISTRATION
### CHAPTER 11— ACQUISITIONS
#### § 1111. Acquisition workforce authorities
###### (a) Expedited Hiring Authority.

(1) In general .

For the purposes of section 3304 of title 5 , the Commandant may

(A) designate any category of acquisition positions within the Coast Guard as shortage category positions; and

(B) use the authorities in such section to recruit and appoint highly qualified persons directly to positions so designated.

(2) Reports .

The Commandant shall include in reports under section 1102 information described in such section regarding positions designated under this subsection.
