
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE IV— COAST GUARD AUTHORIZATIONS AND REPORTS TO CONGRESS
### CHAPTER 51— REPORTS
#### § 5109. Report on gender diversity in the Coast Guard
###### (a) In General .

Not later than January 15, 2022 , and biennially thereafter, the Commandant shall submit to the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure of the House of Representatives and the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation of the Senate a report on gender diversity in the Coast Guard.
