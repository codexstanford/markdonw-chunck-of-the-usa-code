
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE I— ESTABLISHMENT, POWERS, DUTIES, AND ADMINISTRATION
### CHAPTER 11— ACQUISITIONS
#### § 1158. Authority to enter into transactions other than contracts and grants to procure cost-effective, advanced technology for mission-critical needs
###### (a) In General .

Subject to subsections (b) and (c), the Commandant may enter into transactions (other than contracts, cooperative agreements, and grants) to operate, test, and acquire cost-effective technology for the purpose of meeting the mission needs of the Coast Guard.
