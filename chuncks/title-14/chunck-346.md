
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE II— PERSONNEL
### CHAPTER 19— COAST GUARD ACADEMY
#### § 1904. Advisory Board on Women at the Coast Guard Academy
###### (g) Working Groups .

The Advisory Board may establish one or more working groups to assist the Advisory Board in carrying out its duties, including working groups composed in part of cadets at the Academy who are not current members of the Advisory Board.
