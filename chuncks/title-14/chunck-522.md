
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE II— PERSONNEL
### CHAPTER 25— PERSONNEL; GENERAL PROVISIONS
#### § 2514. Career flexibility to enhance retention of members
###### (a) Programs Authorized .

The Commandant may carry out a program under which members of the Coast Guard may be inactivated from active duty in order to meet personal or professional needs and returned to active duty at the end of such period of inactivation from active duty.
