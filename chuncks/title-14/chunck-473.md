
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE II— PERSONNEL
### CHAPTER 21— PERSONNEL; OFFICERS
#### § 2166. Continuation on active duty; Coast Guard officers with certain critical skills
###### (d) Policy .

The Commandant shall carry out this section by prescribing policy which shall specify the criteria to be used in designating any critical skill, specialty, or career field for purposes of subsection (b).
