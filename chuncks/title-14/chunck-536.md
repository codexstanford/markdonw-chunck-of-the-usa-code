
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE II— PERSONNEL
### CHAPTER 25— PERSONNEL; GENERAL PROVISIONS
#### § 2521. Advisory Board on Women in the Coast Guard
###### (b) Membership .

The Advisory Board established under subsection (a) shall be composed of such number of members as the Commandant considers appropriate, selected by the Commandant through a public selection process from among applicants for membership on the Board. The members of the Board shall, to the extent practicable, represent the diversity of the Coast Guard. The members of the Committee shall include an equal number of each of the following:

(1) Active duty officers of the Coast Guard.

(2) Active duty enlisted members of the Coast Guard.

(3) Members of the Coast Guard Reserve.

(4) Retired members of the Coast Guard.
