
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE IV— COAST GUARD AUTHORIZATIONS AND REPORTS TO CONGRESS
### CHAPTER 51— REPORTS
#### § 5107. Major acquisition program risk assessment
###### (a) In General .

Not later than October 15 of each year, the Commandant of the Coast Guard shall provide to the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure of the House of Representatives and the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation of the Senate a briefing regarding a current assessment of the risks associated with all current major acquisition programs, as that term is defined in section 5103(f).
