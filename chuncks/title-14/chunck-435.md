
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE II— PERSONNEL
### CHAPTER 21— PERSONNEL; OFFICERS
#### § 2131. College student pre-commissioning initiative
###### (a) In General .

There is authorized within the Coast Guard a college student pre-commissioning initiative program (in this section referred to as the “Program” ) for eligible undergraduate students to enlist and receive a guaranteed commission as an officer in the Coast Guard.
