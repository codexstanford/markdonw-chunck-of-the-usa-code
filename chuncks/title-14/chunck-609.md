
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE II— PERSONNEL
### CHAPTER 29— COAST GUARD FAMILY SUPPORT, CHILD CARE, AND HOUSING
#### § 2903. Reimbursement for adoption expenses
###### (b) Adoptions Covered .

An adoption for which expenses may be reimbursed under this section includes an adoption by a single individual, an infant adoption, an intercountry adoption, and an adoption of a child with special needs (as defined in section 473(c) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 673(c) )).
