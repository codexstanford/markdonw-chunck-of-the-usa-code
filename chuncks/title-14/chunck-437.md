
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE II— PERSONNEL
### CHAPTER 21— PERSONNEL; OFFICERS
#### § 2131. College student pre-commissioning initiative
###### (c) Enlistment and Obligation .

Individuals selected and accept to participate in the Program shall enlist in the Coast Guard in pay grade E–3 with a 4-year duty obligation and 4-year inactive Reserve obligation.
