
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE II— PERSONNEL
### CHAPTER 19— COAST GUARD ACADEMY
#### § 1904. Advisory Board on Women at the Coast Guard Academy
###### (e) Meetings .

The Advisory Board shall meet with the Commandant at least once each academic year on the activities of the Advisory Board. The Advisory Board shall meet in person with the Superintendent of the Academy not less than twice each academic year on the duties of the Advisory Board.
