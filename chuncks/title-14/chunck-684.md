
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE III— COAST GUARD RESERVE AND AUXILIARY
### CHAPTER 37— COAST GUARD RESERVE
#### § 3743. Eligibility for promotion
###### (b) Exception .

A Reserve officer who has been considered but not recommended for retention in an active status by a board convened under subsection 3752(a)  1 of this title is not eligible for consideration for promotion.
