
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE II— PERSONNEL
### CHAPTER 19— COAST GUARD ACADEMY
#### § 1904. Advisory Board on Women at the Coast Guard Academy
###### (c) Appointment; Term .

Cadets shall serve on the Advisory Board pursuant to appointment by the Superintendent of the Academy. Appointments shall be made not later than 60 days after the date of the swearing in of a new class of cadets at the Academy. The term of membership of a cadet on the Advisory Board shall be 1 academic year.
