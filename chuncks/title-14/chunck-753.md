
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE IV— COAST GUARD AUTHORIZATIONS AND REPORTS TO CONGRESS
### CHAPTER 51— REPORTS
#### § 5111. Report on diversity at Coast Guard Academy
###### (a) In General .

Not later than January 15, 2021 , and annually thereafter, the Commandant shall submit a report on diversity at the Coast Guard Academy to the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure of the House of Representatives and the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation of the Senate.
