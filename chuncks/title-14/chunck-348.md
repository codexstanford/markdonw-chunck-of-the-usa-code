
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE II— PERSONNEL
### CHAPTER 19— COAST GUARD ACADEMY
#### § 1905. Coast Guard Academy minority outreach team program
###### (a) In General .

There is established within the Coast Guard Academy a minority outreach team program (in this section referred to as the “Program”) under which officers, including minority officers and officers from territories and other possessions of the United States, who are Academy graduates may volunteer their time to recruit minority students and strengthen cadet retention through mentorship of cadets.
