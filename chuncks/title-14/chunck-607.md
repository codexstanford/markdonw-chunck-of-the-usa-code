
# Title 14— COAST GUARD
## SUBTITLE II— PERSONNEL
### CHAPTER 29— COAST GUARD FAMILY SUPPORT, CHILD CARE, AND HOUSING
#### § 2902. Surveys of Coast Guard families
###### (c) Federal Recordkeeping .

Each individual surveyed under subsection (a) shall be considered an employee of the United States for purposes of section 3502(3)(A)(i) of title 44 .
