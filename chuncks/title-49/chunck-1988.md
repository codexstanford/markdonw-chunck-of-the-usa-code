
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE V— RAIL PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 247— AMTRAK ROUTE SYSTEM
#### § 24706. Discontinuance
###### (d) Congressional Notification of Discontinuance .

Except as provided in subsection (c), not later than 210 days before discontinuing service over a route, Amtrak shall give written notice of such discontinuance to all of the members of Congress representing any State or district in which the discontinuance would occur.
