
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE VII— AVIATION PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 401— GENERAL PROVISIONS
#### § 40109. Authority to exempt
###### (b) Safety Regulation .

The Administrator of the Federal Aviation Administration may grant an exemption from a regulation prescribed in carrying out sections  1 40103(b)(1) and (2) of this title when the Administrator decides the exemption is in the public interest.
