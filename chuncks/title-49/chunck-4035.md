
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE VII— AVIATION PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 461— INVESTIGATIONS AND PROCEEDINGS
#### § 46111. Certificate actions in response to a security threat
###### (c) Hearings .

When conducting a hearing under this section, the administrative law judge shall not be bound by findings of fact or interpretations of laws and regulations of the Administrator of the Federal Aviation Administration or the Administrator of the Transportation Security Administration.
