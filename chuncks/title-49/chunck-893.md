
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE III— GENERAL AND INTERMODAL PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 63— BUREAU OF TRANSPORTATION STATISTICS
#### § 6305. Advisory council on transportation statistics
###### (e) Applicability of Chapter 10 of Title 5.

Chapter 10 of title 5 shall apply to the advisory council established under this section, except that section 1013 of title 5 shall not apply.
