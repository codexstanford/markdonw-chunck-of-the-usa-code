
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE VIII— PIPELINES
### CHAPTER 601— SAFETY
#### § 60115. Technical safety standards committees
###### (f) Expenses .

A member of a committee under this section is entitled to expenses under section 5703 of title 5 . A payment under this subsection does not make a member an officer or employee of the Government. This subsection does not apply to members regularly employed by the Government.
