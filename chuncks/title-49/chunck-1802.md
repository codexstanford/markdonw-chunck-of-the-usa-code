
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE V— RAIL PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 229— RAIL IMPROVEMENT GRANTS
#### § 22902. Capital investment grants to support intercity passenger rail service
###### (d) State Rail Plans .

State rail plans completed before the date of enactment of the Passenger Rail Investment and Improvement Act of 2008 that substantially meet the requirements of chapter 227 of this title, as determined by the Secretary pursuant to section 22506  1 of this title, shall be deemed by the Secretary to have met the requirements of subsection (c)(1)(A) of this section.
