
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE VIII— PIPELINES
### CHAPTER 601— SAFETY
#### § 60128. Dumping within pipeline rights-of-way
###### (b) Definition .

For purposes of this section, the term “solid waste” has the meaning given that term in section 1004(27) of the Solid Waste Disposal Act ( 42 U.S.C. 6903(27) ).
