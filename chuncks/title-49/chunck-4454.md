
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE VII— AVIATION PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 481— AIRPORT AND AIRWAY TRUST FUND AUTHORIZATIONS
#### § 48102. Research and development
###### (g) Annual Submission of the National Aviation Research Plan .

The Administrator shall submit the national aviation research plan to Congress no later than the date of submission of the President’s budget request to Congress for that fiscal year, as required under section 44501(c).
