
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE IV— INTERSTATE TRANSPORTATION
### CHAPTER 147— ENFORCEMENT; INVESTIGATIONS; RIGHTS; REMEDIES
#### § 14705. Limitation on actions by and against carriers
###### (c) Damages .

A person must file a complaint with the Board or Secretary, as applicable, to recover damages under section 14704(b) within 2 years after the claim accrues.
