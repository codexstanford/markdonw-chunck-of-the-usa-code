
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE VII— AVIATION PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 475— NOISE
#### § 47529. Nonaddition rule
###### (a) General Limitations .

Except as provided in subsection (b) of this section and section 47530 of this title , a person may operate a civil subsonic turbojet aircraft with a maximum weight of more than 75,000 pounds that is imported into the United States after November 4, 1990 , only if the aircraft

(1) complies with the stage 3 noise levels; or

(2) was purchased by the person importing the aircraft into the United States under a legally binding contract made before November 5, 1990 .
