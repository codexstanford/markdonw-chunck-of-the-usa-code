
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE III— GENERAL AND INTERMODAL PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 67— MULTIMODAL INFRASTRUCTURE INVESTMENTS
#### § 6702. Local and regional project assistance
###### (g) Project Planning.

(1) In general .

Of the amounts made available to carry out the program for each fiscal year, not less than 5 percent shall be made available for the planning, preparation, or design of eligible projects.

(2) Nonapplicability of certain limitations .

Subparagraphs (A) and (B) of subsection (c)(2) shall not apply with respect to amounts made available for planning, preparation, or design under paragraph (1).
