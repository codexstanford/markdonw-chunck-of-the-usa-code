
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE V— RAIL PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 247— AMTRAK ROUTE SYSTEM
#### § 24706. Discontinuance
###### (e) Applicability .

This section applies to all service over routes provided by Amtrak, notwithstanding any provision of section 24701 of this title or any other provision of this title except section 24702(b).
