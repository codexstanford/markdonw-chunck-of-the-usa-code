
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE IV— INTERSTATE TRANSPORTATION
### CHAPTER 149— CIVIL AND CRIMINAL PENALTIES
#### § 14905. Penalties for violations of rules relating to loading and unloading motor vehicles
###### (b) Criminal Penalties .

Whoever knowingly violates section 14103(b) of this title shall be fined under title 18 or imprisoned not more than 2 years, or both.
