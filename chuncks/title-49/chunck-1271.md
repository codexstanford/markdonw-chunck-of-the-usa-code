
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE IV— INTERSTATE TRANSPORTATION
### CHAPTER 145— FEDERAL-STATE RELATIONS
#### § 14504a. Unified Carrier Registration System plan and agreement
###### (b) Applicability of Provisions to Freight Forwarders .

A freight forwarder that operates commercial motor vehicles and is not required to register as a carrier pursuant to section 13903(b)  1 shall be subject to the provisions of this section as if the freight forwarder is a motor carrier.
