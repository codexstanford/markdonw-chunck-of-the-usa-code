
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE I— DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION
### CHAPTER 1— ORGANIZATION
#### § 102. Department of Transportation
###### (i) Chief Travel and Tourism Officer.

(1) Establishment .

There is established in the Office of the Secretary of Transportation a position, to be known as the “Chief Travel and Tourism Officer”.

(2) Duties .

The Chief Travel and Tourism Officer shall collaborate with the Assistant Secretary for Aviation and International Affairs to carry out

(A) the National Travel and Tourism Infrastructure Strategic Plan under section 1431(e) of Public Law 114–94 ( 49 U.S.C. 301 note); and

(B) other travel- and tourism-related matters involving the Department of Transportation.
