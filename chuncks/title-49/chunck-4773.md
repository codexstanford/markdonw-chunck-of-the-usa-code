
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE IX— MULTIMODAL FREIGHT TRANSPORTATION
### CHAPTER 701— MULTIMODAL FREIGHT POLICY
#### § 70102. National freight strategic plan
###### (c) Updates .

Not later than 5 years after the date of completion of the national freight strategic plan under subsection (a), and every 5 years thereafter, the Assistant Secretary shall update the plan and publish the updated plan on the public Internet Web site of the Department of Transportation.
