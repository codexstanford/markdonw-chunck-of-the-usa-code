
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE VII— AVIATION PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 471— AIRPORT DEVELOPMENT
#### § 47136. Zero-emission airport vehicles and infrastructure
###### (i) Testing Required.

(1) In general .

A sponsor of a public-use airport may not use funds made available under the program to acquire a zero-emission vehicle unless that make, model, or type of vehicle has been tested by a Federal vehicle testing facility acceptable to the Secretary.

(2) Penalties for false statements .

A certification of compliance under paragraph (1) shall be considered a certification required under this subchapter for purposes of section 47126.
