
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE III— GENERAL AND INTERMODAL PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 67— MULTIMODAL INFRASTRUCTURE INVESTMENTS
#### § 6701. National infrastructure project assistance
###### (b) Establishment .

There is established a program under which the Secretary shall provide to eligible entities grants, on a competitive basis pursuant to single-year or multiyear grant agreements, for projects described in subsection (d).
