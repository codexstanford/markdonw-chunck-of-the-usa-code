
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE VII— AVIATION PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 448— UNMANNED AIRCRAFT SYSTEMS
#### § 44805. Small Unmanned  1 aircraft safety standards
###### (e) 3 Nonapplicability of Other Laws .

The process for authorizing the operation of small unmanned aircraft systems under subsection (a) may allow for operation of any applicable small unmanned aircraft systems within the national airspace system without requiring

(1) airworthiness certification requirements under section 44704 of this title ; or

(2) type certification under part 21 of title 14, Code of Federal Regulations.
