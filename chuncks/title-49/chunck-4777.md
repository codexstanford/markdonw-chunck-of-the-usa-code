
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE IX— MULTIMODAL FREIGHT TRANSPORTATION
### CHAPTER 701— MULTIMODAL FREIGHT POLICY
#### § 70103. National Multimodal Freight Network
###### (c) Redesignation of National Multimodal Freight Network .

Not later than 5 years after the initial designation under subsection (b), and every 5 years thereafter, the Assistant Secretary, using the designation factors described in subsection (b), shall redesignate the National Multimodal Freight Network.
