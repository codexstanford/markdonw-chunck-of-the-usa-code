
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE X— MISCELLANEOUS
### CHAPTER 803— CONTRABAND
#### § 80304. Administrative
###### (d) Attorney General .

The Attorney General, or officers, employees, or agents of the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives, Department of Justice  1 designated by the Attorney General, shall carry out the laws referred to in section 80306(b) of this title to the extent that the violation of this chapter involves contraband described in section 80302(a)(2) or (a)(5).
