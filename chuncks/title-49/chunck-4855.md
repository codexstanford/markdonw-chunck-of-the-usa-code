
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE X— MISCELLANEOUS
### CHAPTER 805— MISCELLANEOUS
#### § 80501. Damage to transported property
###### (a) Criminal Penalty .

A person willfully damaging, or attempting to damage, property in the possession of an air carrier, motor carrier, or rail carrier and being transported in interstate or foreign commerce, shall be fined under title 18, imprisoned for not more than 10 years, or both. In a criminal proceeding under this section, a shipping document for the property is prima facie evidence of the places to which and from which the property was being transported.
