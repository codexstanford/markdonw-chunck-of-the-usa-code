
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE II— OTHER GOVERNMENT AGENCIES
### CHAPTER 13— SURFACE TRANSPORTATION BOARD
#### § 1304. Reports
###### (c) Complaints.

(1) In general .

The Board shall establish and maintain a database of complaints received by the Board.

(2) Quarterly reports .

The Board shall post a quarterly report of formal and informal service complaints received by the Board during the previous quarter that includes

(A) the date on which the complaint was received by the Board;

(B) a list of the type of each complaint;

(C) the geographic region of each complaint; and

(D) the resolution of each complaint, if appropriate.

(3) Written consent .

The quarterly report may identify a complainant that submitted an informal complaint only upon the written consent of the complainant.

(4) Website posting .

Each quarterly report shall be posted on the Board’s public website.
