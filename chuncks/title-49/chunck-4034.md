
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE VII— AVIATION PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 461— INVESTIGATIONS AND PROCEEDINGS
#### § 46111. Certificate actions in response to a security threat
###### (b) Hearings for Citizens .

An individual who is a citizen of the United States who is adversely affected by an order of the Administrator of the Federal Aviation Administration under subsection (a) is entitled to a hearing on the record.
