
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE VII— AVIATION PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 471— AIRPORT DEVELOPMENT
#### § 47171. Expedited, coordinated environmental review process
###### (l) Solicitation and Consideration of Comments .

In applying subsections (j) and (k), the Secretary shall solicit and consider comments from interested persons and governmental entities in accordance with the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 ( 42 U.S.C. 4321 et seq.).
