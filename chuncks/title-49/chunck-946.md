
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE III— GENERAL AND INTERMODAL PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 67— MULTIMODAL INFRASTRUCTURE INVESTMENTS
#### § 6702. Local and regional project assistance
###### (i) Credit Program Costs.

(1) In general .

Subject to paragraph (2), at the request of an eligible entity, the Secretary may use a grant provided to the eligible entity under the program to pay the subsidy or credit risk premium, and the administrative costs, of an eligible project that is eligible for Federal credit assistance under

(A) chapter 224; or

(B) chapter 6 of title 23.

(2) Limitation .

Not more than 20 percent of the funds made available to carry out the program for a fiscal year may be used to carry out paragraph (1).
