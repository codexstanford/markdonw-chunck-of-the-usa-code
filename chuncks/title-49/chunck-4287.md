
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE VII— AVIATION PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 471— AIRPORT DEVELOPMENT
#### § 47134. Airport investment partnership program
###### (d) Program Participation.

(1) Multiple airports .

The Secretary may consider applications under this section submitted by a public airport sponsor for multiple airports under the control of the sponsor if all airports under the control of the sponsor are located in the same State.

(2) Partial privatization .

A purchaser or lessee may be an entity in which a sponsor has an interest.
