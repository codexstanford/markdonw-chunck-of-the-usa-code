
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE I— DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION
### CHAPTER 3— GENERAL DUTIES AND POWERS
#### § 313. Nontraditional and Emerging Transportation Technology Council
###### (h) Transparency .

Not later than 1 year after the date of establishment of the Council, and not less frequently than annually thereafter until December 31, 2026 , the Council shall post on a publicly accessible website a report describing the activities of the Council during the preceding calendar year.
