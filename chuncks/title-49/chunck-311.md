
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE II— OTHER GOVERNMENT AGENCIES
### CHAPTER 11— NATIONAL TRANSPORTATION SAFETY BOARD
#### § 1117. Methodology
###### (c) Acceptable Limitation .

If the Board knows of more than 3 examples taken by regulated entities before the publication of the safety recommendation that were consistent with the recommendation, the brief summary under subsection (b)(3) may be limited to only 3 of those examples.
