
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE VII— AVIATION PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 475— NOISE
#### § 47528. Prohibition on operating certain aircraft not complying with stage 3 noise levels
###### (a) Prohibition .

Except as provided in subsection (b) or (f) of this section and section 47530 of this title , a person may operate after December 31, 1999 , a civil subsonic turbojet (for which an airworthiness certificate other than an experimental certificate has been issued by the Administrator) with a maximum weight of more than 75,000 pounds to or from an airport in the United States only if the Secretary of Transportation finds that the aircraft complies with the stage 3 noise levels.
