
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE I— DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION
### CHAPTER 1— ORGANIZATION
#### § 115. Transportation Security Oversight Board
###### (e) Consideration of Security Information .

A majority of the Board may vote to close a meeting of the Board to the public, except that meetings shall be closed to the public whenever classified, 1 sensitive security information, or information protected in accordance with section 40119(b), 2 will be discussed.
