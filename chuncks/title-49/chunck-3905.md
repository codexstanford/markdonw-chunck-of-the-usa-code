
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE VII— AVIATION PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 449— SECURITY
#### § 44935. Employment standards and training
###### (h) Technological Training.

(1) In general .

The Administrator shall require training to ensure that screeners are proficient in using the most up-to-date new technology and to ensure their proficiency in recognizing new threats and weapons.

(2) Periodic assessments .

The Administrator shall make periodic assessments to determine if there are dual use items and inform security screening personnel of the existence of such items.

(3) Current lists of dual use items .

Current lists of dual use items shall be part of the ongoing training for screeners.

(4) Dual use defined .

For purposes of this subsection, the term “dual use” item means an item that may seem harmless but that may be used as a weapon.
