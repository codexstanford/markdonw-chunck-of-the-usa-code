
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE V— RAIL PROGRAMS
### CHAPTER 201— GENERAL
#### § 20153. Audible warnings at highway-rail grade crossings
###### (f) Specific Rules .

The Secretary may, by regulation, provide that the following crossings over railroad lines shall be subject, in whole or in part, to the regulations required under this section:

(1) Private highway-rail grade crossings.

(2) Pedestrian crossings.

(3) Crossings utilized primarily by non­motorized vehicles and other special vehicles. Regulations issued under this subsection shall not apply to any location where persons are not authorized to cross the railroad.
