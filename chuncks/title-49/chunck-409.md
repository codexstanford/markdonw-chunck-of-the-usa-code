
# Title 49— TRANSPORTATION
## SUBTITLE II— OTHER GOVERNMENT AGENCIES
### CHAPTER 13— SURFACE TRANSPORTATION BOARD
#### § 1323. Service of notice in Board proceedings
###### (a) Designation of Agent .

A carrier providing transportation subject to the jurisdiction of the Board under subtitle IV shall designate an agent on whom service of notices in a proceeding before, and of actions of, the Board may be made.
