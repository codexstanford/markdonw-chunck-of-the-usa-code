
# Title 33— NAVIGATION AND NAVIGABLE WATERS
### CHAPTER 40— OIL POLLUTION
#### § 2713. Claims procedure
##### (c) Election

If a claim is presented in accordance with subsection (a) and

(1) each person to whom the claim is presented denies all liability for the claim, or

(2) the claim is not settled by any person by payment within 90 days after the date upon which (A) the claim was presented, or (B) advertising was begun pursuant to section 2714(b) of this title , whichever is later, the claimant may elect to commence an action in court against the responsible party or guarantor or to present the claim to the Fund.
