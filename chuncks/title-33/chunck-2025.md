
# Title 33— NAVIGATION AND NAVIGABLE WATERS
### CHAPTER 36— WATER RESOURCES DEVELOPMENT
#### § 2212. Inland waterway transportation
##### (a) Construction

65 percent of the costs of construction

(1) of each project authorized by title III of this Act,

(2) of the project authorized by section 652(j) of this title , and

(3) allocated to inland navigation for the project authorized by section 844 of this Act, shall be paid only from amounts appropriated from the general fund of the Treasury. 35 percent of such costs shall be paid only from amounts appropriated from the Inland Waterways Trust Fund. For purposes of this subsection, the term “construction” shall include planning, designing, engineering, surveying, the acquisition of all lands, easements, and rights-of-way necessary for the project, including lands for disposal of dredged material, and relocations necessary for the project.
