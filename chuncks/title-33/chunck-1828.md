
# Title 33— NAVIGATION AND NAVIGABLE WATERS
### CHAPTER 29— DEEPWATER PORTS
#### § 1503. License for ownership, construction, and operation of deepwater port
##### (f) Amendments, transfers, and reinstatements

The Secretary may amend, transfer, or reinstate a license issued under this chapter  2 if the Secretary finds that the amendment, transfer, or reinstatement is consistent with the requirements of this chapter.
