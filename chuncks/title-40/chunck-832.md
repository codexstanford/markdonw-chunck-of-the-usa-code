
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE III— INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY MANAGEMENT
### CHAPTER 115— INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY ACQUISITION PILOT PROGRAM
#### § 11502. Evaluation criteria and plans
###### (b) Test Plan .

Before the pilot program may be conducted under section 11501 of this title , the Administrator for Federal Procurement Policy shall submit to Congress a detailed test plan for the program, including a detailed description of the procedures to be used and a list of regulations that are to be waived.
