
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE IV— APPALACHIAN REGIONAL DEVELOPMENT
### CHAPTER 143— APPALACHIAN REGIONAL COMMISSION
#### § 14301. Establishment, membership, and employees
###### (f) Status of Personnel .

Members, alternates, officers, and employees of the Commission are not federal employees for any purpose, except the Federal Cochairman, the alternate to the Federal Cochairman, the staff of the Federal Cochairman, and federal employees detailed to the Commission under section 14306(a)(3) of this title .
