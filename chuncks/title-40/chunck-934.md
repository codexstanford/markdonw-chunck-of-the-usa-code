
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE IV— APPALACHIAN REGIONAL DEVELOPMENT
### CHAPTER 145— SPECIAL APPALACHIAN PROGRAMS
#### § 14509. High-speed broadband deployment initiative
###### (d) Sources of Assistance .

Subject to subsection (c), a grant provided under this section may be provided from amounts made available to carry out this section in combination with amounts made available

(1) under any other Federal program; or

(2) from any other source.
