
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE III— INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY MANAGEMENT
### CHAPTER 115— INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY ACQUISITION PILOT PROGRAM
#### § 11501. Authority to conduct pilot program
###### (b) Limitation on Amount .

The total amount obligated for contracts entered into under the pilot program conducted under this chapter may not exceed $375,000,000. The Administrator for Federal Procurement Policy shall monitor those contracts and ensure that contracts are not entered into in violation of this subsection.
