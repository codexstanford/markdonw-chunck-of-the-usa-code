
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE II— PUBLIC BUILDINGS AND WORKS
### CHAPTER 33— ACQUISITION, CONSTRUCTION, AND ALTERATION
#### § 3313. Procurement of life-cycle cost effective and energy efficient lighting systems
###### (g) Additional Energy Efficient Lighting Designations .

The Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency and the Secretary of Energy shall give priority to establishing Energy Star performance criteria or Federal Energy Management Program designations for additional lighting product categories that are appropriate for procurement and use in public buildings.
