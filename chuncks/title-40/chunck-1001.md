
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE V— REGIONAL ECONOMIC AND INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT
### CHAPTER 155— FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
#### § 15501. Economic and infrastructure development grants
###### (f) No Relocation Assistance .

Financial assistance authorized by this section may not be used to assist a person or entity in relocating from one area to another, except that financial assistance may be used as otherwise authorized by this subtitle to attract businesses to the region from outside the United States.
