
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE I— FEDERAL PROPERTY AND ADMINISTRATIVE SERVICES
### CHAPTER 5— PROPERTY MANAGEMENT
#### § 622. Collocation among United States Postal Service properties
###### (c) Submission of List of Postal Properties to Federal Agencies.

(1) In general .

Not later than 30 days after the completion of a list under subsection (a), the Council shall provide the list to each Federal agency.

(2) Review by federal agencies .

Not later than 90 days after the receipt of the list submitted under paragraph (1), each Federal agency shall

(A) review the list;

(B) review properties under the control of the Federal agency; and

(C) recommend collocations if appropriate.
