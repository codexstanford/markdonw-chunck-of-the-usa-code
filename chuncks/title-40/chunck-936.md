
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE IV— APPALACHIAN REGIONAL DEVELOPMENT
### CHAPTER 145— SPECIAL APPALACHIAN PROGRAMS
#### § 14509. High-speed broadband deployment initiative
###### (f) Request for Data .

Before making a grant for a project or activity described in subsection (b)(2), the Appalachian Regional Commission shall request from the Federal Communications Commission, the National Telecommunications and Information Administration, the Economic Development Administration, and the Department of Agriculture data on

(1) the level and extent of broadband service that exists in the area proposed to be served by the broadband service-related infrastructure; and

(2) the level and extent of broadband service that will be deployed in the area proposed to be served by the broadband service-related infrastructure pursuant to another Federal program.
