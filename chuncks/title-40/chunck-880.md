
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE IV— APPALACHIAN REGIONAL DEVELOPMENT
### CHAPTER 143— APPALACHIAN REGIONAL COMMISSION
#### § 14322. Approval of development plans, strategy statements, and projects
###### (c) Affirmative Vote Requirement Deemed Met .

After the appropriate state development plan and strategy statement are approved, certification by a state member, when joined by an affirmative vote of the Federal Cochairman, is deemed to satisfy the requirements for affirmative votes for decisions under section 14302(a) of this title .
