
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE II— PUBLIC BUILDINGS AND WORKS
### CHAPTER 87— PHYSICAL DEVELOPMENT OF NATIONAL CAPITAL REGION
#### § 8723. Capital improvements
###### (b) Submission of Multiyear Capital Improvement Plan .

By February 1 of each year, the Mayor of the District of Columbia shall submit to the Commission a copy of the multiyear capital improvements plan for the District of Columbia that the Mayor develops under section 444 of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act ( Public Law 93–198 , 87 Stat. 800 ). The Commission has 30 days in which to comment on the plan but may not change or disapprove of the plan.
