
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE III— INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY MANAGEMENT
### CHAPTER 113— RESPONSIBILITY FOR ACQUISITIONS OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
#### § 11314. Authority to acquire and manage information technology
###### (b) FTS 2000 Program .

The Administrator of General Services shall continue to manage the FTS 2000 program, and to coordinate the follow-on to that program, for and with the advice of the heads of executive agencies.
