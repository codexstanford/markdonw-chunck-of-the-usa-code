
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE II— PUBLIC BUILDINGS AND WORKS
### CHAPTER 33— ACQUISITION, CONSTRUCTION, AND ALTERATION
#### § 3309. Buildings and sites in the District of Columbia
###### (d) Contracts for Events in Stadium .

Notwithstanding the District of Columbia Stadium Act of 1957 ( Public Law 85–300 , 71 Stat. 619 ) or any other provision of law, the Armory Board may make contracts to conduct events in Robert F. Kennedy Stadium.
