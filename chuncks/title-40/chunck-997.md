
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE V— REGIONAL ECONOMIC AND INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT
### CHAPTER 155— FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
#### § 15501. Economic and infrastructure development grants
###### (b) Allocation of Funds .

A Commission shall allocate at least 40 percent of any grant amounts provided by the Commission in a fiscal year for projects described in paragraph (1), (2), (3), or (7) of subsection (a).
