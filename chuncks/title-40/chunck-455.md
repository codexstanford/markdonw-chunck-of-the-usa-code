
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE II— PUBLIC BUILDINGS AND WORKS
### CHAPTER 33— ACQUISITION, CONSTRUCTION, AND ALTERATION
#### § 3314. Baby changing facilities in restrooms
###### (a) Additional Requirement for the Construction, Alteration, and Acquisition of Public Buildings .

Except as provided in subsection (b) and subject to any reasonable accommodations that may be made for individuals in accordance with the Americans with Disabilities Act ( 42 U.S.C. 12101 et seq.) restrooms in a public building shall be equipped with baby changing facilities that the Administrator determines are physically safe, sanitary, and appropriate.
