
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE V— REGIONAL ECONOMIC AND INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT
### CHAPTER 157— ADMINISTRATIVE PROVISIONS
#### § 15705. Biannual meetings of representatives of all Commissions
###### (a) In General .

Representatives of each Commission, the Appalachian Regional Commission, and the Denali Commission shall meet biannually to discuss issues confronting regions suffering from chronic and contiguous distress and successful strategies for promoting regional development.
