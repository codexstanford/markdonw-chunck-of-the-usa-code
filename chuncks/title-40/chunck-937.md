
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE IV— APPALACHIAN REGIONAL DEVELOPMENT
### CHAPTER 145— SPECIAL APPALACHIAN PROGRAMS
#### § 14509. High-speed broadband deployment initiative
###### (g) Requirement .

For each fiscal year, not less than 65 percent of the amounts made available to carry out this section shall be used for grants for projects and activities described in subsection (b)(2).
