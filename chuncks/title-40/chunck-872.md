
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE IV— APPALACHIAN REGIONAL DEVELOPMENT
### CHAPTER 143— APPALACHIAN REGIONAL COMMISSION
#### § 14309. Personal financial interests
###### (c) Federal Cochairman, Alternate to Federal Cochairman, and Federal Officers and Employees .

The Federal Cochairman, the alternate to the Federal Cochairman, and any federal officer or employee detailed to duty with the Commission under section 14306(a)(3) of this title are not subject to this section but remain subject to sections 202–209 of title 18.
