
# Title 40— PUBLIC BUILDINGS, PROPERTY, AND WORKS
## SUBTITLE IV— APPALACHIAN REGIONAL DEVELOPMENT
### CHAPTER 145— SPECIAL APPALACHIAN PROGRAMS
#### § 14510. Drug abuse mitigation initiative
###### (c) Sources of Assistance .

Subject to subsection (b), a grant provided under this section may be provided from amounts made available to carry out this section in combination with amounts made available

(1) under any other Federal program (subject to the availability of subsequent appropriations); or

(2) from any other source.
