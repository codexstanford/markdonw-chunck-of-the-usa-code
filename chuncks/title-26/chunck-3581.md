
# Title 26— INTERNAL REVENUE CODE
## Subtitle A— Income Taxes
### CHAPTER 1— NORMAL TAXES AND SURTAXES
#### § 1378. Taxable year of S corporation
##### (b) Permitted year defined

For purposes of this section, the term “permitted year” means a taxable year which

(1) is a year ending December 31, or

(2) is any other accounting period for which the corporation establishes a business purpose to the satisfaction of the Secretary. For purposes of paragraph (2), any deferral of income to shareholders shall not be treated as a business purpose.
