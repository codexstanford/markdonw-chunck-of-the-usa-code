
# Title 26— INTERNAL REVENUE CODE
## Subtitle A— Income Taxes
### CHAPTER 1— NORMAL TAXES AND SURTAXES
#### § 171. Amortizable bond premium
##### (e) Treatment as offset to interest payments

Except as provided in regulations, in the case of any taxable bond

(1) the amount of any bond premium shall be allocated among the interest payments on the bond under rules similar to the rules of subsection (b)(3), and

(2) in lieu of any deduction under subsection (a), the amount of any premium so allocated to any interest payment shall be applied against (and operate to reduce) the amount of such interest payment. For purposes of the preceding sentence, the term “taxable bond” means any bond the interest of which is not excludable from gross income.
