
# Title 26— INTERNAL REVENUE CODE
## Subtitle A— Income Taxes
### CHAPTER 1— NORMAL TAXES AND SURTAXES
#### § 483. Interest on certain deferred payments
##### (a) Amount constituting interest

For purposes of this title, in the case of any payment

(1) under any contract for the sale or exchange of any property, and

(2) to which this section applies, there shall be treated as interest that portion of the total unstated interest under such contract which, as determined in a manner consistent with the method of computing interest under section 1272(a), is properly allocable to such payment.
