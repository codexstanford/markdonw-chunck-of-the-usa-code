
# Title 26— INTERNAL REVENUE CODE
## Subtitle A— Income Taxes
### CHAPTER 1— NORMAL TAXES AND SURTAXES
#### § 1258. Recharacterization of gain from certain financial transactions
##### (a) General rule

In the case of any gain

(1) which (but for this section) would be treated as gain from the sale or exchange of a capital asset, and

(2) which is recognized on the disposition or other termination of any position which was held as part of a conversion transaction, such gain (to the extent such gain does not exceed the applicable imputed income amount) shall be treated as ordinary income.
