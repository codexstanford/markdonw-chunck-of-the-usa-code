
# Title 44— PUBLIC PRINTING AND DOCUMENTS
### CHAPTER 29— RECORDS MANAGEMENT BY THE ARCHIVIST OF THE UNITED STATES AND BY THE ADMINISTRATOR OF GENERAL SERVICES
#### § 2912. Preservation of electronic messages and other records
###### (c) Review of Regulations Required .

The Archivist shall periodically review and, as necessary, amend the regulations promulgated under subsection (a).
