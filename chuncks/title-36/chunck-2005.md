
# Title 36— PATRIOTIC AND NATIONAL OBSERVANCES, CEREMONIES, AND ORGANIZATIONS
## Subtitle III— Treaty Obligation Organizations
### CHAPTER 3001— THE AMERICAN NATIONAL RED CROSS
#### § 300103. Membership and chapters
###### (a) Membership .

Membership in the corporation is open to all the people of the United States and its territories and possessions, on payment of an amount specified, or as otherwise provided, in the bylaws.
