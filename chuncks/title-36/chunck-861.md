
# Title 36— PATRIOTIC AND NATIONAL OBSERVANCES, CEREMONIES, AND ORGANIZATIONS
## Subtitle II— Patriotic and National Organizations
### CHAPTER 709— FUTURE FARMERS OF AMERICA
#### § 70904. Governing body
###### (b) Officers .

The officers of the FFA, the terms of officers, and the election of officers, are as provided in the constitution or bylaws of the FFA, except that such officers shall include

(1) a national advisor;

(2) an executive secretary; and

(3) a treasurer.
