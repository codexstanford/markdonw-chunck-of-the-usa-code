
# Title 36— PATRIOTIC AND NATIONAL OBSERVANCES, CEREMONIES, AND ORGANIZATIONS
## Subtitle II— Patriotic and National Organizations
### CHAPTER 709— FUTURE FARMERS OF AMERICA
#### § 70909. Relationship to Federal agencies
###### (d) Cooperation With State Boards .

The Federal agencies also may cooperate with State boards and other organizations for career and technical education to assist in the promotion of activities of the FFA.
