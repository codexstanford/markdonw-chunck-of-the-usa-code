
# Title 36— PATRIOTIC AND NATIONAL OBSERVANCES, CEREMONIES, AND ORGANIZATIONS
## Subtitle II— Patriotic and National Organizations
### CHAPTER 1547— NON COMMISSIONED OFFICERS ASSOCIATION OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA, INCORPORATED
#### § 154704. Membership
###### (b) Nondiscrimination .

The terms of membership may not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, disability, age, or national origin.
