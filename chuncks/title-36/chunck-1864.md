
# Title 36— PATRIOTIC AND NATIONAL OBSERVANCES, CEREMONIES, AND ORGANIZATIONS
## Subtitle II— Patriotic and National Organizations
### CHAPTER 2205— UNITED STATES OLYMPIC AND PARALYMPIC COMMITTEE
#### § 220524. General duties of national governing bodies
###### (b) Rule of Construction .

Nothing in this section shall be construed to preempt or otherwise abrogate the duty of care of a national governing body under State law or the common law.
