
# Title 12— BANKS AND BANKING
### CHAPTER 44— TRUTH IN SAVINGS
#### § 4305. Distribution of schedules
##### (b) Distribution in case of certain initial deposits

If

(1) a depositor is not physically present at an office of a depository institution at the time an initial deposit is accepted with respect to an account established by or for such person; and

(2) the schedule required under section 4303(a) of this title has not been furnished previously to such depositor, the depository institution shall mail the schedule to the depositor at the address shown on the records of the depository institution for such account no later than 10 days after the date of the initial deposit.
