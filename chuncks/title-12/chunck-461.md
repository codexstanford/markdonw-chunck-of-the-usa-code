
# Title 12— BANKS AND BANKING
### CHAPTER 3— FEDERAL RESERVE SYSTEM
#### § 504. Civil money penalty
##### (e) Assessment; etc.

Any penalty imposed under subsection (a), (b), or (c) shall be assessed and collected  3 by

(1) in the case of a national bank, by the Comptroller of the Currency; and

(2) in the case of a State member bank, by the Board, in the manner provided in subparagraphs (E), (F), (G), and (I) of section 1818(i)(2) of this title for penalties imposed (under such section) and any such assessment shall be subject to the provisions of such section.
