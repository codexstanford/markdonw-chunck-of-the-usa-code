
# Title 12— BANKS AND BANKING
### CHAPTER 12— SAVINGS ASSOCIATIONS
#### § 1467. Examination fees
##### (e) Regulations

The Comptroller may prescribe regulations with respect to

(1) the computation of, and the assessment for, the cost of conducting examinations pursuant to this section; and

(2) the collection and use of such assessments and any fees under this section. Such regulations may establish formulas to determine a fee or schedule of fees to cover the costs of examinations and also to cover the cost of processing applications, filings, notices, and requests for approvals by the appropriate Federal banking agency or the designee of the Comptroller.
