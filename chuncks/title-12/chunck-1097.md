
# Title 12— BANKS AND BANKING
### CHAPTER 13— NATIONAL HOUSING
#### § 1701x–1. Home inspection counseling
##### (c) Requirements for HUD-approved counseling agencies

Each counseling agency certified pursuant by  1 the Secretary to provide housing counseling services shall provide each of their clients, as part of the home purchase counseling process, the materials specified in subparagraphs (C) and (D) of subsection (a)(1).
