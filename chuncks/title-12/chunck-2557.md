
# Title 12— BANKS AND BANKING
### CHAPTER 17— BANK HOLDING COMPANIES
#### § 1841. Definitions
###### (n) Incorporated Definitions .

For purposes of this chapter, the terms “depository institution”, “insured depository institution”, “appropriate Federal banking agency”, “default”, “in danger of default”, and “State bank supervisor” have the same meanings as in section 3 of the Federal Deposit Insurance Act [ 12 U.S.C. 1813 ].
