
# Title 12— BANKS AND BANKING
### CHAPTER 13— NATIONAL HOUSING
#### § 1701z–8. Energy conservation and renewable-resource demonstration
##### (h) Report on evaluation criteria to be used and results sought prior to funding of projects

Prior to undertaking any demonstration proj­ect under this section, the Secretary shall specify and report to the Congress the criteria by which the Secretary will evaluate the effectiveness of the project and the results to be sought.
