
# Title 12— BANKS AND BANKING
### CHAPTER 53— WALL STREET REFORM AND CONSUMER PROTECTION
#### § 5519. Exclusion for auto dealers
##### (d) Federal Trade Commission authority

Notwithstanding section 57a of title 15 , the Federal Trade Commission is authorized to prescribe rules under sections 45 and 57a(a)(1)(B) of title 15. 2 in accordance with section 553 of title 5 , with respect to a person described in subsection (a).
