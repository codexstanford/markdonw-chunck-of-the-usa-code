
# Title 12— BANKS AND BANKING
### CHAPTER 13— NATIONAL HOUSING
#### § 1715z–17. Shared appreciation mortgages for single family housing
##### (g) Number of mortgages and loans

The aggregate number of mortgages and loans insured under this section and section 1715z–10(c)  1 of this title in any fiscal year may not exceed 10 percent of the aggregate number of mortgages and loans insured by the Secretary under this subchapter during the preceding fiscal year.
