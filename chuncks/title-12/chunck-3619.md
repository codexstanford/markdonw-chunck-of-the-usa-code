
# Title 12— BANKS AND BANKING
### CHAPTER 38A— SINGLE FAMILY MORTGAGE FORECLOSURE
#### § 3764. Record of foreclosure and sale
##### (b) Effect of statements

The items set forth in subsection (a) shall

(1) be prima facie evidence of the truth of such facts in any Federal or State court; and

(2) evidence a conclusive presumption in favor of bona fide purchasers and encumbrancers for value without notice. Encumbrancers for value include liens placed by lenders who provide the purchaser with purchase money in exchange for a security interest in the newly-conveyed property.
