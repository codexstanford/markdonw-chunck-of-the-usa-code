
# Title 30— MINERAL LANDS AND MINING
### CHAPTER 25— SURFACE MINING CONTROL AND RECLAMATION
#### § 1260. Permit approval or denial
##### (d) Prime farmland mining permit

(1) In addition to finding the application in compliance with subsection (b) of this section, if the area proposed to be mined contains prime farmland pursuant to section 1257(b)(16) of this title , the regulatory authority shall, after consultation with the Secretary of Agriculture, and pursuant to regulations issued hereunder by the Secretary of  2 Interior with the concurrence of the Secretary of Agriculture, grant a permit to mine on prime farmland if the regulatory authority finds in writing that the operator has the technological capability to restore such mined area, within a reasonable time, to equivalent or higher levels of yield as non-mined prime farmland in the surrounding area under equivalent levels of management and can meet the soil reconstruction standards in section 1265(b)(7) of this title . Except for compliance with subsection (b), the requirements of this paragraph (1) shall apply to all permits issued after August 3, 1977 .

(2) Nothing in this subsection shall apply to any permit issued prior to August 3, 1977 , or to any revisions or renewals thereof, or to any existing surface mining operations for which a permit was issued prior to August 3, 1977 .
