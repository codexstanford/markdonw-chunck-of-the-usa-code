
# Title 30— MINERAL LANDS AND MINING
### CHAPTER 27— GEOTHERMAL ENERGY
#### § 1531. Feasibility study loan program
##### (d) Interest rate; term

Each loan made pursuant to this section shall bear interest at a discount or interest rate equal to the rate in effect (at the time the loan is made) for water resources planning projects under section 80 of the Water Resources Development Act of 1974 ( 42 U.S.C. 1962(d) –17(a)). 1 Each loan shall be for such term as the Secretary deems appropriate, but not in excess of ten years for loans under subsection (b) or thirty years for loans under subsection (c).
