
# Title 30— MINERAL LANDS AND MINING
### CHAPTER 27— GEOTHERMAL ENERGY
#### § 1522. Establishment of program
##### (j) Appropriations

There may be appropriated to the Geothermal Resources Development Fund (established pursuant to section 1144 1 of this title), for purposes of this section, such amounts as are authorized for such purposes in the law referred to in subsection (a) or in other legislation hereafter enacted.
