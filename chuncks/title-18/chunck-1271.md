
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 111— SHIPPING
#### § 2281. Violence against maritime fixed platforms
###### (c) Bar To Prosecution .

It is a bar to Federal prosecution under subsection (a) for conduct that occurred within the United States that the conduct involved was during or in relation to a labor dispute, and such conduct is prohibited as a felony under the law of the State in which it was committed. For purposes of this section, the term “labor dispute” has the meaning set forth in section 13(c) of the Norris-LaGuardia Act, as amended ( 29 U.S.C. 113(c) ), and the term “State” means a State of the United States, the District of Columbia, and any commonwealth, territory, or possession of the United States.
