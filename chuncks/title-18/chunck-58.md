
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 2— AIRCRAFT AND MOTOR VEHICLES
#### § 40A. Operation of unauthorized unmanned aircraft over wildfires
###### (b) Exceptions .

This section does not apply to the operation of an unmanned aircraft conducted by a unit or agency of the United States Government or of a State, tribal, or local government (including any individual conducting such operation pursuant to a contract or other agreement entered into with the unit or agency) for the purpose of protecting the public safety and welfare, including firefighting, law enforcement, or emergency response.
