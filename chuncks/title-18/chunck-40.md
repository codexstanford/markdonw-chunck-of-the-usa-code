
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 2— AIRCRAFT AND MOTOR VEHICLES
#### § 37. Violence at international airports
###### (c) Bar to Prosecution .

It is a bar to Federal prosecution under subsection (a) for conduct that occurred within the United States that the conduct involved was during or in relation to a labor dispute, and such conduct is prohibited as a felony under the law of the State in which it was committed. For purposes of this section, the term “labor dispute” has the meaning set forth in section 2(c)  1 of the Norris-LaGuardia Act, as amended ( 29 U.S.C. 113(c) ), and the term “State” means a State of the United States, the District of Columbia, and any commonwealth, territory, or possession of the United States.
