
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 317— INSTITUTIONS FOR WOMEN
#### § 4322. Use of restraints on prisoners during the period of pregnancy, labor, and postpartum recovery prohibited
###### (e) Violation Reporting Process .

The Director of the Bureau of Prisons, in consultation with the Director of the United States Marshals Service, shall establish a process through which a prisoner may report a violation of this section.
