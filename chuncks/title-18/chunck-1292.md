
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 111— SHIPPING
#### § 2285. Operation of submersible vessel or semi-submersible vessel without nationality
###### (h) Definitions .

In this section, the terms “submersible vessel”, “semi-submersible vessel”, “vessel of the United States”, and “vessel without nationality” have the meaning given those terms in section 70502 of title 46 .
