
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 2— AIRCRAFT AND MOTOR VEHICLES
#### § 40A. Operation of unauthorized unmanned aircraft over wildfires
###### (a) In General .

Except as provided in subsection (b), an individual who operates an unmanned aircraft and knowingly or recklessly interferes with a wildfire suppression, or law enforcement or emergency response efforts  1 related to a wildfire suppression, shall be fined under this title, imprisoned for not more than 2 years, or both.
