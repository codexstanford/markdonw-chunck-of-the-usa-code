
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 303— BUREAU OF PRISONS
#### § 4051. Treatment of primary caretaker parents and other individuals
###### (b) Geographic Placement.

(1) Establishment of office .

The Director shall establish within the Bureau of Prisons an office that determines the placement of prisoners.

(2) Placement of prisoners .

In determining the placement of a prisoner, the office established under paragraph (1) shall

(A) if the prisoner has children, consider placing the prisoner as close to the children as possible; and

(B) consider any other factor that the office determines to be appropriate.
