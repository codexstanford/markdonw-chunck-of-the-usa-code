
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 117— TRANSPORTATION FOR ILLEGAL SEXUAL ACTIVITY AND RELATED CRIMES
#### § 2421A. Promotion or facilitation of prostitution and reckless disregard of sex trafficking
###### (d) Mandatory Restitution .

Notwithstanding sections  3 3663 or 3663A and in addition to any other civil or criminal penalties authorized by law, the court shall order restitution for any violation of subsection (b)(2). The scope and nature of such restitution shall be consistent with section 2327(b).
