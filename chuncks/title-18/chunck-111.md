
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 9— BANKRUPTCY
#### § 158. Designation of United States attorneys and agents of the Federal Bureau of Investigation to address abusive reaffirmations of debt and materially fraudulent statements in bankruptcy schedules
###### (d) Bankruptcy Procedures .

The bankruptcy courts shall establish procedures for referring any case that may contain a materially fraudulent statement in a bankruptcy schedule to the individuals designated under this section.
