
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 228— DEATH SENTENCE
#### § 3595. Review of a sentence of death
###### (a) Appeal .

In a case in which a sentence of death is imposed, the sentence shall be subject to review by the court of appeals upon appeal by the defendant. Notice of appeal must be filed within the time specified for the filing of a notice of appeal. An appeal under this section may be consolidated with an appeal of the judgment of conviction and shall have priority over all other cases.
