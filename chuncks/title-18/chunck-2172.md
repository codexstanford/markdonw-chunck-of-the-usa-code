
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 303— BUREAU OF PRISONS
#### § 4051. Treatment of primary caretaker parents and other individuals
###### (e) Parenting Classes .

The Director shall provide voluntary parenting classes to each prisoner who is a primary caretaker parent, and such classes shall be made available to prisoners with limited English proficiency in compliance with title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 ( 42 U.S.C. 2000d et seq.).
