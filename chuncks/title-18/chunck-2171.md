
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 303— BUREAU OF PRISONS
#### § 4051. Treatment of primary caretaker parents and other individuals
###### (d) Intake and Assessments .

The Director shall assess the need for family-focused programming at intake, such as questions about children, gauge interest in parenting resources, and concerns about their child or caregiving, and administer ongoing assessment to better inform, identify, and make recommendations about the mother’s parental role and familial needs.
