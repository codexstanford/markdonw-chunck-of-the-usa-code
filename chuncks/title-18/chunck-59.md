
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 2— AIRCRAFT AND MOTOR VEHICLES
#### § 40A. Operation of unauthorized unmanned aircraft over wildfires
###### (c) Definitions .

In this section, the following definitions apply:

(1) Unmanned aircraft .

The term “unmanned aircraft” has the meaning given the term in section 44801 of title 49 , United States Code.

(2) Wildfire .

The term “wildfire” has the meaning given that term in section 2 of the Emergency Wildfire Suppression Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1856m ).

(3) Wildfire suppression .

The term “wildfire suppression” means an effort to contain, extinguish, or suppress a wildfire.
