
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 44— FIREARMS
#### § 925B. Reporting of background check denials to State authorities
###### (c) Amendment of Report .

If a report is made in accordance with subsection (b) and, after such report is made, the Federal Bureau of Investigation determines that the receipt of a firearm by a person for whom the report was made would not violate subsection (g) or (n) of section 922 or State, local, or Tribal law, the Attorney General shall notify any law enforcement authority and any prosecutor to whom the report was made of that determination.
