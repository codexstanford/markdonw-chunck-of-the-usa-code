
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 11— BRIBERY, GRAFT, AND CONFLICTS OF INTEREST
#### § 207. Restrictions on former officers, employees, and elected officials of the executive and legislative branches
###### (l) Contract Advice by Former Details .

Whoever, being an employee of a private sector organization assigned to an agency under chapter 37 of title 5, within one year after the end of that assignment, knowingly represents or aids, counsels, or assists in representing any other person (except the United States) in connection with any contract with that agency shall be punished as provided in section 216 of this title .
