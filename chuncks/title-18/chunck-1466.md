
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 117— TRANSPORTATION FOR ILLEGAL SEXUAL ACTIVITY AND RELATED CRIMES
#### § 2421A. Promotion or facilitation of prostitution and reckless disregard of sex trafficking
###### (e) Affirmative Defense .

It shall be an affirmative defense to a charge of violating subsection (a), or subsection (b)(1) where the defendant proves, by a preponderance of the evidence, that the promotion or facilitation of prostitution is legal in the jurisdiction where the promotion or facilitation was targeted.
