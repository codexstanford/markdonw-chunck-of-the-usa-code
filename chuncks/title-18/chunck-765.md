
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 63— MAIL FRAUD AND OTHER FRAUD OFFENSES
#### § 1350. Failure of corporate officers to certify financial reports
###### (b) Content .

The statement required under subsection (a) shall certify that the periodic report containing the financial statements fully complies with the requirements of section 13(a) or 15(d) of the Securities Exchange Act pf  1 1934 ( 15 U.S.C. 78m or 78 o (d)) and that information contained in the periodic report fairly presents, in all material respects, the financial condition and results of operations of the issuer.
