
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 44— FIREARMS
#### § 925B. Reporting of background check denials to State authorities
###### (b) Requirements for Report .

A report is made in accordance with this subsection if the report is made under subsection (a) within 24 hours after the NICS denies a firearm transfer in accordance with section 922(t) of title 18 , United States Code, except that the making of the report may be delayed for so long as is necessary to avoid compromising an ongoing investigation.
