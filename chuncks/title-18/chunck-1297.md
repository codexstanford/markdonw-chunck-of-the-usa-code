
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 111A— DESTRUCTION OF, OR INTERFERENCE WITH, VESSELS OR MARITIME FACILITIES
#### § 2291. Destruction of vessel or maritime facility
###### (c) Penalty .

Whoever is fined or imprisoned under subsection (a) as a result of an act involving a vessel that, at the time of the violation, carried high-level radioactive waste (as that term is defined in section 2(12) of the Nuclear Waste Policy Act of 1982 ( 42 U.S.C. 10101(12) )  1 or spent nuclear fuel (as that term is defined in section 2(23) of the Nuclear Waste Policy Act of 1982 ( 42 U.S.C. 10101(23) ), 1 shall be fined under this title, imprisoned for a term up to life, or both.
