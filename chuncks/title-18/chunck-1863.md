
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 223— WITNESSES AND EVIDENCE
#### § 3512. Foreign requests for assistance in criminal investigations and prosecutions
###### (d) Search Warrant Limitation .

An application for execution of a request for a search warrant from a foreign authority under this section, other than an application for a warrant issued as provided under section 2703 of this title , shall be filed in the district in which the place or person to be searched is located.
