
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 111A— DESTRUCTION OF, OR INTERFERENCE WITH, VESSELS OR MARITIME FACILITIES
#### § 2293. Bar to prosecution
###### (b) Definitions .

In this section:

(1) Labor dispute .

The term “labor dispute” has the same meaning given that term in section 13(c) of the Act to amend the Judicial Code and to define and limit the jurisdiction of courts sitting in equity, and for other purposes ( 29 U.S.C. 113(c) , commonly known as the Norris-LaGuardia Act).

(2) State .

The term “State” means a State of the United States, the District of Columbia, and any commonwealth, territory, or possession of the United States.
