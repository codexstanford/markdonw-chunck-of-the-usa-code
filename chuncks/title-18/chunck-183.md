
# Title 18— CRIMES AND CRIMINAL PROCEDURE
### CHAPTER 11— BRIBERY, GRAFT, AND CONFLICTS OF INTEREST
#### § 226. Bribery affecting port security
###### (b) Definition .

In this section, the term “secure or restricted area” means an area of a vessel or facility designated as secure in an approved security plan, as required under section 70103 of title 46 , United States Code, and the rules and regulations promulgated under that section.
