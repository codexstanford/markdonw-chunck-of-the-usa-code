
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 3— DEPARTMENT OF VETERANS AFFAIRS
#### § 324. Cost of War Toxic Exposures Fund
###### (e) Estimates for Congressional Consideration .

The Secretary shall include in documents submitted to Congress in support of the President’s budget submitted pursuant to section 1105 of title 31 detailed estimates of the sums described in subsection (c) for the applicable fiscal year.
