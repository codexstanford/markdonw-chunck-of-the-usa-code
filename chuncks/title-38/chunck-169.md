
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 5— AUTHORITY AND DUTIES OF THE SECRETARY
#### § 548. Advisory Committee on United States Outlying Areas and Freely Associated States
###### (a) Establishment .

The Secretary shall establish an advisory committee, to be known as the “Advisory Committee on United States Outlying Areas and Freely Associated States”, to provide advice and guidance to the Secretary on matters relating to covered veterans.
