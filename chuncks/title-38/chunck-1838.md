
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 63— OUTREACH ACTIVITIES
#### § 6303. Outreach services
###### (a) Requirement To Provide Services .

In carrying out the purposes of this subchapter, the Secretary shall provide the outreach services specified in subsections (b) through (d). In areas where a significant number of eligible veterans and eligible dependents speak a language other than English as their principal language, such services shall, to the maximum feasible extent, be provided in the principal language of such persons.
