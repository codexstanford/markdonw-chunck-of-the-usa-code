
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 23— BURIAL BENEFITS
#### § 2308. Transportation of deceased veteran to a national cemetery or a covered veterans’ cemetery
###### (a) In General .

The Secretary may pay, in addition to any amount paid pursuant to section 2303 of this title regarding veterans described in subparagraph (B) or (C) of subsection (a)(2) of such section, or pursuant to section 2307 of this title , the cost of transportation of the deceased veteran described in subsection (b) for burial in a national cemetery or a covered veterans’ cemetery. Such payment shall not exceed the cost of transportation to the national cemetery nearest the veteran’s last place of residence in which burial space is available.
