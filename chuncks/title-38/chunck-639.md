
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 17— HOSPITAL, NURSING HOME, DOMICILIARY, AND MEDICAL CARE
#### § 1725A. Access to walk-in care
###### (d) Federally-Qualified Health Centers .

Whenever practicable, the Secretary may use a Federally-qualified health center (as defined in section 1905( l )(2)(B) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 1396d ( l )(2)(B))) to carry out this section.
