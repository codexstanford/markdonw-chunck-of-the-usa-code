
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 36— ADMINISTRATION OF EDUCATIONAL BENEFITS
#### § 3696. Prohibition on certain advertising, sales, and enrollment practices
###### (a) Prohibition on Engaging in Substantial Misrepresentation .

An educational institution with a course or program of education approved under this chapter, and an entity that owns such an educational institution, shall not engage in substantial misrepresentation described in subsection (b).
