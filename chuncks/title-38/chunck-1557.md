
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 41— JOB COUNSELING, TRAINING, AND PLACEMENT SERVICE FOR VETERANS
#### § 4103. Directors and Assistant Directors for Veterans’ Employment and Training; additional Federal personnel
###### (c) Coordination With State Departments of Labor and Veterans Affairs .

Each Director for Veterans’ Employment and Training for a State shall coordinate the Director’s activities under this chapter with the State department of labor and the State department of veterans affairs.
