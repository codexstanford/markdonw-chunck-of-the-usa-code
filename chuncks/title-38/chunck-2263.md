
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 76— HEALTH PROFESSIONALS EDUCATIONAL ASSISTANCE PROGRAM
#### § 7699A. Obligated service
###### (a) In General .

Each participant in the Program shall provide service as a full-time employee of the Department at a Vet Center (as defined in section 7699(d) of this title ) for the period of obligated service set forth in the agreement of the participant entered into under section 7604 of this title .
