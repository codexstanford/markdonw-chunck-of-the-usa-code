
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 5— AUTHORITY AND DUTIES OF THE SECRETARY
#### § 531. Requirements relating to naming of Department property
###### (a) Naming Limitation .

Except as expressly provided by law or as authorized under subsection (b), a facility, structure, or real property of the Department, and a major portion (such as a wing or floor) of any such facility, structure, or real property, may be named only for the geographic area in which the facility, structure, or real property is located.
