
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 3— DEPARTMENT OF VETERANS AFFAIRS
#### § 324. Cost of War Toxic Exposures Fund
###### (f) Procedures for Estimates .

The Secretary may, after consultation with the Committee on Appropriations of the Senate and the Committee on Appropriations of the House of Representatives, establish policies and procedures for developing the annual detailed estimates required by subsection (e).
