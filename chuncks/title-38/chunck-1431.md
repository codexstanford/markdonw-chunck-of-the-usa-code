
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 36— ADMINISTRATION OF EDUCATIONAL BENEFITS
#### § 3696. Prohibition on certain advertising, sales, and enrollment practices
###### (k) Rule of Construction Regarding State Approving Agencies and Risk-based Surveys .

Nothing in this section shall be construed to prohibit a State approving agency from conducting any risk-based survey the State approving agency considers appropriate at any educational institution that it considers appropriate for oversight purposes.
