
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 19— INSURANCE
#### § 1949. Beneficiaries
###### (a) Designation .

A person who enrolls in United States Government life insurance may designate a beneficiary of the insurance policy. Subject to regulations, the insured person shall at all times have the right to change the beneficiary or beneficiaries of a United States Government life insurance policy without the consent of such beneficiary or beneficiaries.
