
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 17— HOSPITAL, NURSING HOME, DOMICILIARY, AND MEDICAL CARE
#### § 1720J. Emergent suicide care
###### (b) Eligibility .

An individual is eligible for emergent suicide care under subsection (a) if the individual is in an acute suicidal crisis and is either of the following:

(1) A veteran (as defined in section 101).

(2) An individual described in section 1720I(b) of this title .
