
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 20— BENEFITS FOR HOMELESS VETERANS
#### § 2013. Program to improve retention of housing by formerly homeless veterans and veterans at risk of becoming homeless
###### (a) Program Required .

The Secretary shall carry out a program under which the Secretary shall provide case management services to improve the retention of housing by veterans who were previously homeless and are transitioning to permanent housing and veterans who are at risk of becoming homeless.
