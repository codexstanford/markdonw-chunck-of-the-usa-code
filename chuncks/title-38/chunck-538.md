
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 17— HOSPITAL, NURSING HOME, DOMICILIARY, AND MEDICAL CARE
#### § 1710C. Traumatic brain injury: plans for rehabilitation and reintegration into the community
###### (g) State Designated Protection and Advocacy System Defined .

In this section, the term “State protection and advocacy system” means a system established in a State under subtitle C of the Developmental Disabilities Assistance and Bill of Rights Act of 2000 ( 42 U.S.C. 15041 et seq.) to protect and advocate for the rights of persons with development disabilities.
