
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 7— EMPLOYEES
#### § 704. Waiver of pay limitation for certain employees
###### (a) Employees of Veterans Health Administration Impacted by Closure or Realignment .

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Secretary may waive any annual premium or aggregate limitation on pay for an employee of the Veterans Health Administration for the calendar year during which

(1) the official duty station of the employee is closed; or

(2) the office, facility, activity, or organization of the employee is realigned.
