
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 76— HEALTH PROFESSIONALS EDUCATIONAL ASSISTANCE PROGRAM
#### § 7673. Scholarship
###### (d) Payment of Educational Expenses by Educational Institutions .

The Secretary may arrange with an educational institution in which a participant in the Program is enrolled for the payment of the educational expenses described in subsection (a). Such payments may be made without regard to subsections (a) and (b) of section 3324 of title 31 .
