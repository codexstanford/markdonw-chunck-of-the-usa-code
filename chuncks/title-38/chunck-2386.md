
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 81— ACQUISITION AND OPERATION OF HOSPITAL AND DOMICILIARY FACILITIES; PROCUREMENT AND SUPPLY; ENHANCED-USE LEASES OF REAL PROPERTY
#### § 8127. Small business concerns owned and controlled by veterans: contracting goals and preferences
###### (e) Eligibility of Small Business Concerns .

A small business concern may be awarded a contract under this section only if the small business concern and the veteran owner of the small business concern are listed in the database of veteran-owned businesses maintained by the Administrator under section 36 of the Small Business Act.

[(f) Transferred. Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title VIII, § 862(b)(2) , Jan. 1, 2021 , 134 Stat. 3778 .]
