
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 33— POST–9/11 EDUCATIONAL ASSISTANCE
#### § 3320. Edith Nourse Rogers STEM Scholarship
###### (h) Covered Clinical Training Program Defined .

In this section, the term “covered clinical training program” means any clinical training required by a health care professional to be licensed to practice in a State or locality.
