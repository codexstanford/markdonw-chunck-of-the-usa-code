
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 18— BENEFITS FOR CHILDREN OF VIETNAM VETERANS AND CERTAIN OTHER VETERANS
#### § 1812. Covered birth defects
###### (a) Identification .

The Secretary shall identify the birth defects of children of women Vietnam veterans that

(1) are associated with the service of those veterans in the Republic of Vietnam during the Vietnam era; and

(2) result in permanent physical or mental disability.
