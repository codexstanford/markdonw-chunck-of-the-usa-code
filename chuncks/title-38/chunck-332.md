
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 11— COMPENSATION FOR SERVICE-CONNECTED DISABILITY OR DEATH
#### § 1164. Presumptions of service-connection for Coronavirus Disease 2019
###### (d) Symptoms of COVID– 19.

For purposes of subsection (a), symptoms of COVID–19 are those symptoms that competent medical evidence demonstrates are experienced by an individual affected and directly related to COVID–19.
