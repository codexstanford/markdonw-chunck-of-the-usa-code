
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 1— GENERAL
#### § 117. Advance appropriations for certain accounts
###### (b) Estimates Required .

The Secretary shall include in documents submitted to Congress in support of the President’s budget submitted pursuant to section 1105 of title 31 , United States Code, detailed estimates of the funds necessary for the covered accounts of the Department for the fiscal year following the fiscal year for which the budget is submitted.
