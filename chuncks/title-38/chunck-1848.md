
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 63— OUTREACH ACTIVITIES
#### § 6307. Outreach for eligible dependents
###### (b) Information as to Availability of Outreach Services for Dependents .

The Secretary shall ensure that the availability of outreach services and assistance for eligible dependents under this subchapter is made known through a variety of means, including the Internet, announcements in veterans publications, and announcements to the media.
