
# Title 38— VETERANS’ BENEFITS
### CHAPTER 53— SPECIAL PROVISIONS RELATING TO BENEFITS
#### § 5303B. Character of service determinations
###### (b) Provision of Information .

If the Secretary determines under subsection (a) that an individual is barred to a benefit under the laws administered by the Secretary, the Secretary shall provide to such individual information regarding the ability of the individual to address such condition, including pursuant to section 5303 of this title and chapter 79 of title 10.
