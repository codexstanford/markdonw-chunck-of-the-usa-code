
# Title 41— PUBLIC CONTRACTS
## Subtitle II— Other Advertising and Contract Provisions
### CHAPTER 61— ADVERTISING
#### § 6102. Exceptions from advertising requirement
###### (f) Forest Products From Indian Reservations .

Lumber and other forest products produced by Indian enterprises from forests on Indian reservations may be sold under regulations the Secretary of the Interior prescribes, without compliance with section 6101 of this title .
