
# Title 41— PUBLIC CONTRACTS
## Subtitle I— Federal Procurement Policy
### CHAPTER 45— CONTRACT FINANCING
#### § 4505. Payments for commercial products and commercial services
###### (c) Limitation on Advance Payments .

Advance payments made under section 4501 of this title for commercial products or commercial services may include payments, in a total amount not more than 15 percent of the contract price, in advance of any performance of work under the contract.
