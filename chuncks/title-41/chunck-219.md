
# Title 41— PUBLIC CONTRACTS
## Subtitle I— Federal Procurement Policy
### CHAPTER 19— SIMPLIFIED ACQUISITION PROCEDURES
#### § 1909. Management of purchase cards
###### (e) Relationship to Department of Defense Purchase Card Regulations .

The requirements of this section shall not apply to the Department of Defense. See section 4754 of title 10 for provisions relating to management of purchase cards in the Department.
