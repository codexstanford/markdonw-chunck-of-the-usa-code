
# Title 41— PUBLIC CONTRACTS
## Subtitle III— Contract Disputes
### CHAPTER 71— CONTRACT DISPUTES
#### § 7105. Agency boards
###### (c) Tennessee Valley Authority Board.

(1) Establishment .

The Board of Directors of the Tennessee Valley Authority may establish a board of contract appeals of the Tennessee Valley Authority of an indeterminate number of members.

(2) Appointment of members and compensation .

The Board of Directors of the Tennessee Valley Authority shall establish criteria for the appointment of members to the agency board established under paragraph (1), and shall designate a chairman of the agency board. The chairman and other members of the agency board shall receive compensation, at the daily equivalent of the rates determined under section 5372a of title 5 , for each day they are engaged in the actual performance of their duties as members of the agency board.
