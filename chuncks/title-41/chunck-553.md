
# Title 41— PUBLIC CONTRACTS
## Subtitle I— Federal Procurement Policy
### CHAPTER 47— MISCELLANEOUS
#### § 4713. Authorities relating to mitigating supply chain risks in the procurement of covered articles
###### (e) Delegation .

The head of an executive agency may not delegate the authority provided in subsection (a) or the responsibility identified in subsection (f) to an official below the level one level below the Deputy Secretary or Principal Deputy Director.
