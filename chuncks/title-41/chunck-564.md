
# Title 41— PUBLIC CONTRACTS
## Subtitle II— Other Advertising and Contract Provisions
### CHAPTER 61— ADVERTISING
#### § 6101. Advertising requirement for Federal Government purchases and sales
###### (a) Definitions .

In this section

(1) Appropriation .

The term “appropriation” includes amounts made available by legislation under section 9104 of title 31 .

(2) Federal government .

The term “Federal Government” includes the government of the District of Columbia.
