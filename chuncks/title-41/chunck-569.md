
# Title 41— PUBLIC CONTRACTS
## Subtitle II— Other Advertising and Contract Provisions
### CHAPTER 61— ADVERTISING
#### § 6102. Exceptions from advertising requirement
###### (b) Bureau of Interparliamentary Union for Promotion of International Arbitration .

Section 6101 of this title does not apply to the Bureau of Interparliamentary Union for Promotion of International Arbitration with respect to necessary stenographic reporting services by contract.
