
# Title 41— PUBLIC CONTRACTS
## Subtitle II— Other Advertising and Contract Provisions
### CHAPTER 67— SERVICE CONTRACT LABOR STANDARDS
#### § 6707. Enforcement and administration of chapter
###### (e) Exclusion of Fringe Benefit Payments in Determining Overtime Pay .

In determining any overtime pay to which a service employee is entitled under Federal law, the regular or basic hourly rate of pay of the service employee does not include any fringe benefit payments computed under this chapter which are excluded from the definition of “regular rate” under section 7(e) of the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 ( 29 U.S.C. 207(e) ).
