
# Title 41— PUBLIC CONTRACTS
## Subtitle I— Federal Procurement Policy
### CHAPTER 47— MISCELLANEOUS
#### § 4711. Linking of award and incentive fees to acquisition outcomes
###### (d) Guidance for Department of Defense .

The Department of Defense shall continue to be subject to guidance on award and incentive fees issued by the Secretary of Defense pursuant to section 814 of the John Warner National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2007 ( Public Law 109–364 , 10 U.S.C. 2302 note).
