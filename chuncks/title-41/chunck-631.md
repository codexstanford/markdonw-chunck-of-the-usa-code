
# Title 41— PUBLIC CONTRACTS
## Subtitle II— Other Advertising and Contract Provisions
### CHAPTER 67— SERVICE CONTRACT LABOR STANDARDS
#### § 6704. Limitation on minimum wage
###### (a) In General .

A contractor that makes a contract with the Federal Government, the principal purpose of which is to furnish services through the use of service employees, and any subcontractor, may not pay less than the minimum wage specified under section 6(a)(1) of the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 ( 29 U.S.C. 206(a)(1) ) to an employee engaged in performing work on the contract.
