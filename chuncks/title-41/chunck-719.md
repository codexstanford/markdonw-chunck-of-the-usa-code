
# Title 41— PUBLIC CONTRACTS
## Subtitle IV— Miscellaneous
### CHAPTER 85— COMMITTEE FOR PURCHASE FROM PEOPLE WHO ARE BLIND OR SEVERELY DISABLED
#### § 8504. Procurement requirements for the Federal Government
###### (b) Exception .

This section does not apply to the procurement of a product that is available from an industry established under chapter 307 of title 18 and that is required under section 4124 of title 18 to be procured from that industry.
