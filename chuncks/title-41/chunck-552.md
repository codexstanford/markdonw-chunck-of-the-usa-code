
# Title 41— PUBLIC CONTRACTS
## Subtitle I— Federal Procurement Policy
### CHAPTER 47— MISCELLANEOUS
#### § 4713. Authorities relating to mitigating supply chain risks in the procurement of covered articles
###### (d) Confidentiality .

The notice required by subsection (b)(2) shall be kept confidential until a determination with respect to a covered procurement action has been made pursuant to subsection (b)(3).
