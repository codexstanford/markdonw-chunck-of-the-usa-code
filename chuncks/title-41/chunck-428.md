
# Title 41— PUBLIC CONTRACTS
## Subtitle I— Federal Procurement Policy
### CHAPTER 41— TASK AND DELIVERY ORDER CONTRACTS
#### § 4103. General authority
###### (a) Authority To Award .

Subject to the requirements of this section, section 4106 of this title , and other applicable law, the head of an executive agency may enter into a task or delivery order contract for procurement of services or property.
