
# Title 41— PUBLIC CONTRACTS
## Subtitle III— Contract Disputes
### CHAPTER 71— CONTRACT DISPUTES
#### § 7108. Payment of claims
###### (c) Reimbursement .

Payments made pursuant to subsections (a) and (b) shall be reimbursed to the fund provided by section 1304 of title 31 by the agency whose appropriations were used for the contract out of available amounts or by obtaining additional appropriations for purposes of reimbursement.
