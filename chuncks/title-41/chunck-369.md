
# Title 41— PUBLIC CONTRACTS
## Subtitle I— Federal Procurement Policy
### CHAPTER 35— TRUTHFUL COST OR PRICING DATA
#### § 3507. Interest and penalties for certain overpayments
###### (b) Liability Not Affected by Refusal To Submit Certification .

Any liability under this section of a contractor that submits cost or pricing data but refuses to submit the certification required by section 3502(b) of this title with respect to the cost or pricing data is not affected by the refusal to submit the certification.
