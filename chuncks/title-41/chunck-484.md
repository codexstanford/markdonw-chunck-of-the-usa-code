
# Title 41— PUBLIC CONTRACTS
## Subtitle I— Federal Procurement Policy
### CHAPTER 45— CONTRACT FINANCING
#### § 4505. Payments for commercial products and commercial services
###### (a) Terms and Conditions for Payments .

Payments under section 4501 of this title for commercial products or commercial services may be made under terms and conditions that the head of the executive agency determines are appropriate or customary in the commercial marketplace and are in the best interests of the Federal Government.
