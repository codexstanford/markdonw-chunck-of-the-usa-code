
# Title 39— POSTAL SERVICE
### CHAPTER 30— NONMAILABLE MATTER
#### § 3015. Nonmailable plant pests and injurious animals
###### (b) Plant Pests .

Any plant pest, the movement of which is prohibited pursuant to section 103 or 104 of the Federal Plant Pest Act ( 7 U.S.C. 150bb or 150cc), 1 constitutes nonmailable matter.
