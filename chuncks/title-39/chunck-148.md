
# Title 39— POSTAL SERVICE
### CHAPTER 30— NONMAILABLE MATTER
#### § 3015. Nonmailable plant pests and injurious animals
###### (d) Illegally Taken Fish, Wildlife, or Plants .

Any fish, wildlife, or plant, the conveyance of which is prohibited pursuant to section 3 of the Lacey Act Amendments of 1981 ( 16 U.S.C. 3372 ), constitutes nonmailable matter.
