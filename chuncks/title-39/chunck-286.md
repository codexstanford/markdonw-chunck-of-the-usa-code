
# Title 39— POSTAL SERVICE
### CHAPTER 37— NONPOSTAL SERVICES
#### § 3703. Postal Service program for State governments
###### (c) Approval Required .

The Postal Service may not establish the program under subsection (a) unless the Governors of the Postal Service approve such program by a recorded vote that is publicly disclosed on the Postal Service website with a majority of the Governors then in office voting for approval.
