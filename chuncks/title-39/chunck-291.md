
# Title 39— POSTAL SERVICE
### CHAPTER 37— NONPOSTAL SERVICES
#### § 3705. Transparency and accountability for nonpostal services
###### (c) Audits .

The Inspector General shall regularly audit the data collection systems and procedures used in collecting information and preparing the report required under subsection (a). The results of any such audit shall be submitted to the Postal Service and the Postal Regulatory Commission.
