
# Title 39— POSTAL SERVICE
### CHAPTER 30— NONMAILABLE MATTER
#### § 3015. Nonmailable plant pests and injurious animals
###### (c) Plants .

Any plant, article, or matter, the importation or interstate shipment of which is prohibited pursuant to the Act of August 20, 1912 ( 37 Stat. 315 , chapter 308; 7 U.S.C. 151 et seq.) (commonly known as the “Plant Quarantine Act”), 1 constitutes nonmailable matter.
