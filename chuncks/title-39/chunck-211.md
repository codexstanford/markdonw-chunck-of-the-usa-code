
# Title 39— POSTAL SERVICE
### CHAPTER 36— POSTAL RATES, CLASSES, AND SERVICES
#### § 3631. Applicability; definitions and updates
###### (a) Applicability .

This subchapter shall apply with respect to

(1) priority mail;

(2) expedited mail;

(3) bulk parcel post;

(4) bulk international mail; and

(5) mailgrams; subject to subsection (d) and any changes the Postal Regulatory Commission may make under section 3642.
