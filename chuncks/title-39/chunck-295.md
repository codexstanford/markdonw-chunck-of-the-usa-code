
# Title 39— POSTAL SERVICE
### CHAPTER 37— NONPOSTAL SERVICES
#### § 3705. Transparency and accountability for nonpostal services
###### (g) Experimental Product Authority .

The Postal Service may conduct market tests of agreements pursuant to section 3703 and section 3704 of this chapter subject to the same terms and conditions described in section 3641 of this title . For the purposes of a market test, the cost coverage requirements in sections 3703 and 3704 of this chapter shall not apply.
