
# Title 31— MONEY AND FINANCE
## SUBTITLE III— FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT
### CHAPTER 33— DEPOSITING, KEEPING, AND PAYING MONEY
#### § 3353. Compliance
###### (c) Compliance Enforcement Pilot Programs .

The Director of the Office of Management and Budget may establish 1 or more pilot programs that shall test potential accountability mechanisms with appropriate incentives and consequences tied to success in ensuring compliance with this section and eliminating improper payments.
