
# Title 31— MONEY AND FINANCE
## SUBTITLE I— GENERAL
### CHAPTER 3— DEPARTMENT OF THE TREASURY
#### § 310. Financial Crimes Enforcement Network
###### (j) Analytical Experts.

(1) In general .

FinCEN shall maintain financial experts capable of identifying, tracking, and tracing money laundering and terrorist-financing networks in order to conduct and support civil and criminal anti-money laundering and countering the financing of terrorism investigations conducted by the United States Government.

(2) FinCEN analytical hub .

FinCEN, upon a reasonable request from a Federal agency, shall, in collaboration with the requesting agency and the appropriate Federal functional regulator, analyze the potential anti-money laundering and countering the financing of terrorism activity that prompted the request.
