
# Title 31— MONEY AND FINANCE
## SUBTITLE IV— MONEY
### CHAPTER 53— MONETARY TRANSACTIONS
#### § 5336. Beneficial ownership information reporting requirements
###### (g) Regulations .

In promulgating regulations carrying out this section, the Director shall reach out to members of the small business community and other appropriate parties to ensure efficiency and effectiveness of the process for the entities subject to the requirements of this section.
