
# Title 31— MONEY AND FINANCE
## SUBTITLE I— GENERAL
### CHAPTER 3— DEPARTMENT OF THE TREASURY
#### § 316. Treasury Attaché Program
###### (b) Number of Attachés.

(1) In general .

The number of Treasury Financial Attachés appointed under this section at any one time shall be not fewer than 6 more employees than the number of employees of the Department of the Treasury serving as Treasury attachés on the date of enactment of this section.

(2) Additional posts .

The Secretary of the Treasury may establish additional posts subject to the availability of appropriations.
