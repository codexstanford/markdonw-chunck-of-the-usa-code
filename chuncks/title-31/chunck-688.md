
# Title 31— MONEY AND FINANCE
## SUBTITLE IV— MONEY
### CHAPTER 53— MONETARY TRANSACTIONS
#### § 5312. Definitions and application
###### (c) Additional Definitions .

For purposes of this subchapter, the following definitions shall apply:

(1) 1 Certain institutions included in definition .

The term “financial institution” (as defined in subsection (a)) includes the following:

(A) 2 Any futures commission merchant, commodity trading advisor, or commodity pool operator registered, or required to register, under the Commodity Exchange Act.
