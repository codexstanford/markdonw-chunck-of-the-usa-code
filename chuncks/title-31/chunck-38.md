
# Title 31— MONEY AND FINANCE
## SUBTITLE I— GENERAL
### CHAPTER 3— DEPARTMENT OF THE TREASURY
#### § 312. Terrorism and financial intelligence
###### (h) Deployment of Staff .

The Secretary of the Treasury may detail, without regard to the provisions of section 300.301 of title 5, Code of Federal Regulations, any employee in the OTFI to any position in the OTFI for which the Secretary has determined there is a need.
