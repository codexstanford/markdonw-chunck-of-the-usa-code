
# Title 31— MONEY AND FINANCE
## SUBTITLE I— GENERAL
### CHAPTER 3— DEPARTMENT OF THE TREASURY
#### § 310. Financial Crimes Enforcement Network
###### (f) FinCEN Domestic Liaisons.

(1) Establishment of office .

There is established in FinCEN an Office of Domestic Liaison, which shall be headed by the Chief Domestic Liaison.

(2) Location .

The Office of the Domestic Liaison shall be located in the District of Columbia.
