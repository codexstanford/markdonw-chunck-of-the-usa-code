
# Title 31— MONEY AND FINANCE
## SUBTITLE IV— MONEY
### CHAPTER 53— MONETARY TRANSACTIONS
#### § 5336. Beneficial ownership information reporting requirements
###### (j) Authorization of Appropriations .

There are authorized to be appropriated to FinCEN for each of the 3 fiscal years beginning on the effective date of the regulations promulgated under subsection (b)(4), such sums as may be necessary to carry out this section, including allocating funds to the States to pay reasonable costs relating to compliance with the requirements of such section.
