
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 53— CONTROL OF ILLEGALLY TAKEN FISH AND WILDLIFE
#### § 3371. Definitions
###### (k) Taken and Taking.

(1) Taken .

The term “taken” means captured, killed, or collected and, with respect to a plant, also means harvested, cut, logged, or removed.

(2) Taking .

The term “taking” means the act by which fish, wildlife, or plants are taken.

(l) The term “transport” means to move, convey, carry, or ship by any means, or to deliver or receive for the purpose of movement, conveyance, carriage, or shipment.
