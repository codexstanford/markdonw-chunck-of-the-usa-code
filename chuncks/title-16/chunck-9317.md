
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 77— YUKON RIVER SALMON
#### § 5708. Administrative matters
##### (c) Status as Federal employees

Except for officials of the United States Government, individuals described in subsection (b) shall not be considered to be Federal employees while engaged in the actual performance of duties, except for the purposes of injury compensation or tort claims liability as provided in chapter 81 of title 5 and chapter 71  1 of title 28.
