
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 1— NATIONAL PARKS, MILITARY PARKS, MONUMENTS, AND SEASHORES
#### § 410eeee–17. Applicable provisions of other law
##### (a) Cooperative agreements

The provisions of section 460ww–1(e)(1) of this title shall apply to the New River Gorge National River  1 in the same manner and to the same extent as such provisions apply to the Gauley River National Recreation Area.
