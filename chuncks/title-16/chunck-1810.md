
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 1— NATIONAL PARKS, MILITARY PARKS, MONUMENTS, AND SEASHORES
#### § 423l–5. Park administration
##### (a) Applicable laws

The Secretary, acting through the Director of the National Park Service, shall administer the battlefield park in accordance with sections 423 l –1 to 423 l –6 of this title and laws generally applicable to units of the National Park System, including the Act of August 25, 1916 ( 16 U.S.C. 1 et seq.)  1 and the Act of August 21, 1935 ( 16 U.S.C. 461 et seq.). 1
