
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 35— ENDANGERED SPECIES
#### § 1536. Interagency cooperation
##### (k) Exemption decision not considered major Federal action; environmental impact statement

An exemption decision by the Committee under this section shall not be a major Federal action for purposes of the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 [ 42 U.S.C. 4321 et seq.]: Provided , That an environmental impact statement which discusses the impacts upon endangered species or threatened species or their critical habitats shall have been previously prepared with respect to any agency action exempted by such order.
