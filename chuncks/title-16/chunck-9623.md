
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 84— HEALTHY FOREST RESTORATION
#### § 6592c. Emergency actions
##### (d) Administrative review of authorized emergency actions

An authorized emergency action carried out under this section shall not be subject to objection under the predecisional administrative review processes established under section 6515 of this title and section 428 of the Department of the Interior, Environment, and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 2012 ( 16 U.S.C. 6515 note; Public Law 112–74 ).
