
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 12— FEDERAL REGULATION AND DEVELOPMENT OF POWER
#### § 824. Declaration of policy; application of subchapter
##### (e) “Public utility” defined

The term “public utility” when used in this subchapter and subchapter III of this chapter means any person who owns or operates facilities subject to the jurisdiction of the Commission under this subchapter (other than facilities subject to such jurisdiction solely by reason of section 824e(e), 824e(f), 1 824i, 824j, 824j–1, 824k, 824 o , 824 o –1, 824p, 824q, 824r, 824s, 824t, 824u, or 824v of this title).
