
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 1— NATIONAL PARKS, MILITARY PARKS, MONUMENTS, AND SEASHORES
#### § 460ooo–2. Purposes of the Acquisition Planning District
##### (a) In general

The Secretary shall negotiate with land owners for the acquisition of lands and interest in lands suitable for Conservation Area expansion that meet the purposes described in section 460 ooo –3(a) of this title. The Secretary shall only acquire property under this subchapter pursuant to section 460 ooo –6 of this title.
