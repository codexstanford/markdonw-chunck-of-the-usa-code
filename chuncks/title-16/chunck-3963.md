
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 1— NATIONAL PARKS, MILITARY PARKS, MONUMENTS, AND SEASHORES
#### § 460gggg. Numu Newe Special Management Area
##### (a) Definitions

In this section:

(1) Management plan

The term “management plan” means the management plan for the Special Management Area developed under subsection (d).

(2) Secretary

The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

(3) Special Management Area

The term “Special Management Area” means the Numu Newe Special Management Area established by subsection (b).
