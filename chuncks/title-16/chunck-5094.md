
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 6— GAME AND BIRD PRESERVES; PROTECTION
#### § 691d. Applicability of certain statutes

Sections 715f to 715i and 715 l to 715n  1 of this title are made applicable for the purposes of sections 691, 691a, and 691c of this title in the same manner and to the same extent as though they were enacted as a part of those sections.
