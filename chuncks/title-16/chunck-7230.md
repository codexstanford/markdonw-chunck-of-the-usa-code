
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 35— ENDANGERED SPECIES
#### § 1542. Authorization of appropriations
##### (b) Exemptions

There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary to assist him and the Endangered Species Committee in carrying out their functions under sections  1 1536(e), (g), and (h) of this title not to exceed $600,000 for each of fiscal years 1988, 1989, 1990, 1991, and 1992.
