
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 1— NATIONAL PARKS, MILITARY PARKS, MONUMENTS, AND SEASHORES
#### § 460l–17. Miscellaneous provisions
##### (g) Nonapplication of section 200306(a)(3) of title 54 to nonreimbursable costs of the United States

section  1 200306(a)(3) of title 54 shall not apply to costs allocated to recreation and fish and wildlife enhancement which are borne by the United States as a nonreimbursable project cost pursuant to section 460 l –13(a) or section 460 l –14(b)(1) of this title.
