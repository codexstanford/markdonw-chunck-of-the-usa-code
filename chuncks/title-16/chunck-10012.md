
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 100— NUTRIA ERADICATION AND CONTROL
#### § 8102. Nutria eradication program
##### (a) Grant authority

The Secretary of the Interior (referred to in this chapter as the “Secretary”), 1 may provide financial assistance to a State, in an amount that is in proportion to the total impacted area of such State affected by nutria, that has demonstrated to the Secretary sufficient need for a program to implement measures to eradicate or control nutria and restore marshland, public and private wetlands, and agricultural lands damaged by nutria.
