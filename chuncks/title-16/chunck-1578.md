
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 1— NATIONAL PARKS, MILITARY PARKS, MONUMENTS, AND SEASHORES
#### § 410kkk–2. Administration
##### (a) In general

The park shall be administered by the Secretary in accordance with this subchapter and with laws generally applicable to units of the National Park System, including the Act of August 25, 1916 ( 39 Stat. 535 ; 16 U.S.C. 1 et seq.)  1 and the Act of August 21, 1935 ( 49 Stat. 666 ; 16 U.S.C. 461 et seq.). 1
