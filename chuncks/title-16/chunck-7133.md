
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 33— COASTAL ZONE MANAGEMENT
#### § 1468. Regional Ocean Partnerships.
##### (c) Governing bodies of Regional Ocean Partnerships

A Regional Ocean Partnership shall have a governing body that

(1) shall be comprised, at a minimum, of voting members from each coastal state  2 participating in the Regional Ocean Partnership, designated by the Governor of the coastal state;  2 and

(2) may include such other members as the partnership considers appropriate.
