
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 1— NATIONAL PARKS, MILITARY PARKS, MONUMENTS, AND SEASHORES
#### § 460ww–1. Administration
##### (d) Application of other provisions

The provisions of section 1278(a) of this title , 3 shall apply to the recreation area in the same manner and to the same extent as such provisions apply to river segments referred to in such provisions.
