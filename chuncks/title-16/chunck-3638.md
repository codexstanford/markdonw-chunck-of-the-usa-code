
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 1— NATIONAL PARKS, MILITARY PARKS, MONUMENTS, AND SEASHORES
#### § 460nnn–51. Establishment of advisory council
##### (h) Applicable law

The advisory committee  2 shall be subject to the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 ( 43 U.S.C. 1701 et seq.) and chapter 10 of title 5.
