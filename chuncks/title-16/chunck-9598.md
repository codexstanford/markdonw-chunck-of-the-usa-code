
# Title 16— CONSERVATION
### CHAPTER 84— HEALTHY FOREST RESTORATION
#### § 6592. Wildfire risk reduction
##### (b) Treatment

Of the Federal land or Indian forest land or rangeland that has been identified as having a very high wildfire hazard potential, the Secretary of the Interior and the Secretary of Agriculture, acting through the Chief of the Forest Service, shall, by not later than September 30, 2027 , conduct restoration treatments and improve the Fire Regime Condition Class of 10,000,000 acres that are located in

(1) the wildland-urban interface; or

(2) a public drinking water source area.
