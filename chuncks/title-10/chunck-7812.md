
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle D— Air Force and Space Force
### CHAPTER 951— TRAINING GENERALLY
#### § 9417. Degree granting authority for Air University
###### (a) Authority .

Except as provided in sections 9414 and 9415 of this title, under regulations prescribed by the Secretary of the Air Force, the commander of the Air University may, upon the recommendation of the faculty of the Air University components, confer appropriate degrees upon graduates who meet the degree requirements.
