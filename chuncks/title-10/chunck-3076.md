
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 76— MISSING PERSONS
#### § 1504. Subsequent board of inquiry
###### (a) Additional Board .

If information that may result in a change of status of a person covered by a determination under section 1503(i) of this title becomes available within one year after the date of the transmission of a report with respect to the person under section 1502(a)(2) of this title , the Secretary concerned shall appoint a board under this section to conduct an inquiry into the information.
