
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 209— OPERATIONAL CONTRACT SUPPORT
#### § 3154. Contingency contracting matters covered
###### (b) Interagency Plans .

To the extent practicable, the joint policy for contingency contracting required by section 3151 of this title should be taken into account in the development of interagency plans for stabilization and reconstruction operations, consistent with the report submitted by the President under section 1035 of the John Warner National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2007 ( Public Law 109–364 ; 120 Stat. 2388 ) on interagency operating procedures for the planning and conduct of stabilization and reconstruction operations.
