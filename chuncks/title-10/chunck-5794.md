
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 279— CONTRACTOR AUDITS AND ACCOUNTING
#### § 3841. Examination of records of contractor
###### (f) Limitation .

The authority of the head of an agency under subsection (b), and the authority of the Comptroller General under subsection (d), with respect to a contract or subcontract shall expire three years after final payment under such contract or subcontract.
