
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 49— MISCELLANEOUS PROHIBITIONS AND PENALTIES
#### § 987. Terms of consumer credit extended to members and dependents: limitations
###### (g) Servicemembers Civil Relief Act Protections Unaffected .

Nothing in this section may be construed to limit or otherwise affect the applicability of section 207 of the Servicemembers Civil Relief Act ( 50 U.S.C. 3937 ).
