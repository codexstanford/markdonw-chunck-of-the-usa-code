
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 21— DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE INTELLIGENCE MATTERS
#### § 429. Appropriations for Defense intelligence elements: accounts for transfers; transfer authority
###### (e) Defense Intelligence Element Defined .

In this section, the term “Defense intelligence element” means any of the Department of Defense agencies, offices, and elements included within the definition of “intelligence community” under section 3(4) of the National Security Act of 1947 ( 50 U.S.C. 3003(4) ).
