
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 53— MISCELLANEOUS RIGHTS AND BENEFITS
#### § 1030. Bonus to encourage Department of Defense personnel to refer persons for appointment as officers to serve in health professions
###### (d) Amount of Bonus .

The amount of the bonus payable for a referral under subsection (a) may not exceed $2,000. The amount shall be payable as provided in subsection (e).
