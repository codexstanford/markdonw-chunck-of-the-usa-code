
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 271— TRUTHFUL COST OR PRICING DATA (TRUTH IN NEGOTIATIONS)
#### § 3702. Required cost or pricing data and certification
###### (d) Applicability of Chapter .

Except as provided under section 3703 of this title , this chapter applies to contracts entered into by the head of an agency on behalf of a foreign government.
