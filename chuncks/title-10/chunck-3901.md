
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 112— CYBER SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM
#### § 2200b. Grant program
###### (b) Purposes .

The proceeds of grants under this section may be used by an institution of higher education for the following purposes:

(1) Faculty development.

(2) Curriculum development.

(3) Laboratory improvements.

(4) Faculty research in information security.
