
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 32— OFFICER STRENGTH AND DISTRIBUTION IN GRADE
#### § 526a. Authorized strength after December 31, 2022 : general officers and flag officers on active duty
###### (f) Exclusion of Officers Departing From Joint Duty Assignments .

The limitations in subsection (a) do not apply to an officer released from a joint duty assignment, but only during the 60-day period beginning on the date the officer departs the joint duty assignment. The Secretary of Defense may authorize the Secretary of a military department to extend the 60-day period by an additional 120 days, except that not more than three officers on active duty from each armed force may be covered by the additional extension at the same time.
