
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 101— TRAINING GENERALLY
#### § 2014. Administrative actions adversely affecting military training or other readiness activities
###### (g) Definition .

In this section, the term “Executive agency” has the meaning given such term in section 105 of title 5 , except that the term does not include the Government Accountability Office.
