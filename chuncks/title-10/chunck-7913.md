
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle D— Air Force and Space Force
### CHAPTER 961— CIVIL RESERVE AIR FLEET
#### § 9512. Civil Reserve Air Fleet contracts: payment rate
###### (b) Regulations .

The Secretary shall prescribe regulations for purposes of subsection (a). The Secretary may exclude from the applicability of those regulations any airlift services contract made through the use of competitive procedures.
