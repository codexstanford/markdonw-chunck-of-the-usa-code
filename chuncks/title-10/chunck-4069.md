
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 134— MISCELLANEOUS ADMINISTRATIVE PROVISIONS
#### § 2264. Reimbursement for assistance provided to nongovernmental entertainment-oriented media producers
###### (a) In General .

There shall be credited to the applicable appropriations account or fund from which the expenses described in subsection (b) were charged any amounts received by the Department of Defense as reimbursement for such expenses.
