
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 2— DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE
#### § 113. Secretary of Defense
###### (o) Notification of Certain Overseas Contingency Operations for Purposes of Inspector General Act of 1978.

The Secretary of Defense shall provide the Chair of the Council of Inspectors General on Integrity and Efficiency written notification of the commencement or designation of a military operation as an overseas contingency operation upon the earlier of

(1) a determination by the Secretary that the overseas contingency operation is expected to exceed 60 days; or

(2) the date on which the overseas contingency operation exceeds 60 days.
