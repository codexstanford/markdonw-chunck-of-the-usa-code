
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 59— SEPARATION
#### § 1175a. Voluntary separation pay and benefits
###### (f) Computation of Voluntary Separation Pay .

The Secretary concerned shall specify the amount of voluntary separation pay that an individual or defined group of members of the armed forces may be paid under subsection (e)(1). No member may receive as voluntary separation pay an amount greater than four times the full amount of separation pay for a member of the same pay grade and years of service who is involuntarily separated under section 1174 of this title .
