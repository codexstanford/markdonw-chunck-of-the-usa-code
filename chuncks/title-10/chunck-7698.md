
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle D— Air Force and Space Force
### CHAPTER 908— THE SPACE FORCE
#### § 9083. Office of the Chief of Space Operations: function; composition
###### (c) Organization .

Except as otherwise specifically prescribed by law, the Office of the Chief of Space Operations shall be organized in such manner, and the members of the Office of the Chief of Space Operations shall perform such duties and have such titles, as the Secretary of the Air Force may prescribe.
