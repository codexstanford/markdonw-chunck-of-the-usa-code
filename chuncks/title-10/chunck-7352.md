
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle C— Navy and Marine Corps
### CHAPTER 863— NAVAL VESSELS
#### § 8675. Vessels stricken from Naval Vessel Register: sale
###### (a) Appraisal of Vessels Stricken From Naval Vessel Register .

The Secretary of the Navy shall appraise each vessel stricken from the Naval Vessel Register under section 8674 of this title .
