
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 157— TRANSPORTATION
#### § 2636a. Loss or damage to personal property transported at Government expense: full replacement value; deduction from amounts due carriers
###### (c) Inapplicability of Related Limits .

The limitations on amounts of claims that may be settled under section 3721(b) of title 31 do not apply to a carrier’s contractual obligation to pay full replacement value under this section.
