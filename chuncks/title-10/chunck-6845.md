
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle B— Army
### CHAPTER 773— DISPOSAL OF OBSOLETE OR SURPLUS MATERIAL
#### § 7688. Armor-piercing ammunition and components: condition on disposal
###### (c) Special Rule for Non-Armor-Piercing Components .

A component of the armor-piercing ammunition that is not itself armor-piercing and is not subjected to metal reclamation as described in subsection (b) may not be used as a component in the production of new or remanufactured armor-piercing ammunition other than for sale to a law enforcement or other governmental agency or for a government-to-government sale or commercial export to a foreign government under the Arms Export Control Act ( 22 U.S.C. 2751 ).
