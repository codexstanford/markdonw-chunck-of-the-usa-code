
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 388— PROCUREMENT TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE COOPERATIVE AGREEMENT PROGRAM
#### § 4955. Funding
###### (b) Determinations on Scope of Operations .

A determination of whether a procurement technical assistance program is operating on a Statewide basis or on less than a Statewide basis or is operated wholly within one or more service areas of the Bureau of Indian Affairs by an eligible entity referred to in section 4951(1)(D) of this title shall be made in accordance with regulations prescribed by the Secretary of Defense.
