
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle E— Reserve Components
### CHAPTER 1409— CONTINUATION OF OFFICERS ON THE RESERVE ACTIVE-STATUS LIST AND SELECTIVE EARLY REMOVAL
#### § 14704. Selective early removal from the reserve active-status list
###### (c) Separation of Officers Selected .

In the case of an officer recommended for separation in the report of a board under subsection (a), the Secretary may separate the officer in accordance with section 14514 of this title .
