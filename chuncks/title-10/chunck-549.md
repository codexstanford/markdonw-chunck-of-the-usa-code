
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 9A— AUDIT
#### § 240i. Annual report on unfunded priorities
###### (a) In General .

Not later than 10 days after the date on which the budget of the President for a fiscal year is submitted to Congress pursuant to section 1105 of title 31 , the Under Secretary of Defense (Comptroller) shall submit to the Secretary of Defense, the Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff, and the congressional defense committees a report on unfunded priorities of the Department of Defense related to audit readiness and remediation.
