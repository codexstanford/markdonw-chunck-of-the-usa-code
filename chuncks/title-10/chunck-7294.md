
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle C— Navy and Marine Corps
### CHAPTER 861— SECRETARY OF THE NAVY: MISCELLANEOUS POWERS AND DUTIES
#### § 8617. National Museum of the United States Navy
###### (f) Additional Terms and Conditions .

The Secretary of the Navy may require such additional terms and conditions in connection with a contract, cooperative agreement, or other agreement under subsection (a) or a lease under subsection (d) as the Secretary considers appropriate to protect the interests of the United States.
