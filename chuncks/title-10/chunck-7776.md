
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle D— Air Force and Space Force
### CHAPTER 945— COMPUTATION OF RETIRED PAY
#### § 9362. Recomputation of retired pay to reflect advancement on retired list
###### (c) Rounding to Next Lower Dollar .

The amount computed under subsection (b), if not a multiple of $1, shall be rounded to the next lower multiple of $1.
