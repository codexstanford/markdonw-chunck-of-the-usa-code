
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 101— TRAINING GENERALLY
#### § 2015. Program to assist members in obtaining professional credentials
###### (a) Program Required .

The Secretary of Defense and the Secretary of Homeland Security, with respect to the Coast Guard when it is not operating as a service in the Navy, shall carry out a program to enable members of the armed forces to obtain, while serving in the armed forces, professional credentials that translate into civilian occupations.
