
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle B— Army
### CHAPTER 705— THE ARMY STAFF
#### § 7038. Office of Army Reserve: appointment of Chief
###### (c) Term; Reappointment .

The Chief of Army Reserve is appointed for a period of four years, but may be removed for cause at any time. An officer serving as Chief of Army Reserve may be reappointed for one additional four-year period.
