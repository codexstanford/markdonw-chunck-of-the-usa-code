
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 341— ACQUISITION OF SERVICES GENERALLY
#### § 4501. Procurement of contract services: management structure
###### (a) Requirement for Management Structure .

The Secretary of Defense shall establish and implement a management structure for the procurement of contract services for the Department of Defense. The management structure shall provide, at a minimum, for the matters specified in subsections (b), (c), (d), and (e).
