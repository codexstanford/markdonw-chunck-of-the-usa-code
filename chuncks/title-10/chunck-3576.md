
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 88— MILITARY FAMILY PROGRAMS AND MILITARY CHILD CARE
#### § 1787. Reporting of child abuse
###### (b) Definition .

In this section, the term “child abuse and neglect” has the meaning provided in section 3 of the Child Abuse Prevention and Treatment Act ( Public Law 93–247 ; 42 U.S.C. 5101 note).
