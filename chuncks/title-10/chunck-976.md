
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 24— NUCLEAR POSTURE
#### § 492b. Biannual briefing on nuclear weapons and related activities
###### (a) In General .

On or about May 1 and November 1 of each year, the officials specified in subsection (b) shall provide to the Committees on Armed Services of the Senate and the House of Representatives a briefing on matters relating to nuclear weapons policies, operations, technology development, and other similar topics as requested by such committees.
