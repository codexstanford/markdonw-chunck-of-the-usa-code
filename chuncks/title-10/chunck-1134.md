
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 35— TEMPORARY APPOINTMENTS IN OFFICER GRADES
#### § 605. Promotion to certain grades for officers with critical skills: colonel, lieutenant colonel, major, captain; captain, commander, lieutenant commander, lieutenant
###### (e) Acceptance and Effective Date of Appointment .

Each appointment under this section, unless expressly declined, is, without formal acceptance, regarded as accepted on the date such appointment is made, and a member so appointed is entitled to the pay and allowances of the grade of the temporary promotion under this section from the date the appointment is made.
