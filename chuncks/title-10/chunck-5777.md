
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 277— CONTRACT FINANCING
#### § 3806. Action in case of fraud
###### (c) Reduction or Suspension of Payments .

The head of an agency receiving a recommendation under subsection (b) in the case of a contractor’s request for payment under a contract shall determine whether there is substantial evidence that the request is based on fraud. Upon making such a determination, the agency head may reduce or suspend further payments to the contractor under such contract.
