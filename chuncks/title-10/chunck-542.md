
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 9A— AUDIT
#### § 240e. Audits: use of commercial data integration and analysis products in preparing audits
###### (b) Use of Funding and Resources .

The Secretary of Defense may use science and technology funding, prototypes, and test and evaluation resources as appropriate in support of deployment of technologies and services as described in subsection (a).
