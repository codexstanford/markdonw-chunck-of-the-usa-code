
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle B— Army
### CHAPTER 773— DISPOSAL OF OBSOLETE OR SURPLUS MATERIAL
#### § 7687. Sale of excess, obsolete, or unserviceable ammunition and ammunition components
###### (g) Relationship to Arms Export Control Act .

Nothing in this section shall be construed to affect the applicability of section 38 of the Arms Export Control Act ( 22 U.S.C. 2778 ) to sales of ammunition or ammunition components on the United States Munitions List.
