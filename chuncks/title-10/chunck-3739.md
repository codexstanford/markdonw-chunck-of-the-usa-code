
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 104— UNIFORMED SERVICES UNIVERSITY OF THE HEALTH SCIENCES
#### § 2113b. Academic Health System
###### (a) In General .

The Secretary of Defense may establish an Academic Health System to integrate the health care, health professions education, and health research activities of the military health system, including under this chapter, in the National Capital Region.
