
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 273— ALLOWABLE COSTS
#### § 3763. Bid and proposal costs: allowable costs
###### (c) Goal for Reimbursable Bid and Proposal Costs .

The Secretary shall establish a goal each fiscal year limiting the amount of reimbursable bid and proposal costs paid by the Department of Defense to an amount equal to not more than one percent of the total aggregate industry sales to the Department of Defense. To achieve such goal, the Secretary may not limit the payment of allowable bid and proposal costs for the covered year.
