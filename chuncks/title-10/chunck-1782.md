
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 47— UNIFORM CODE OF MILITARY JUSTICE
#### § 929. Art. 129. Burglary; unlawful entry
###### (b) Unlawful Entry .

Any person subject to this chapter who unlawfully enters

(1) the real property of another; or

(2) the personal property of another which amounts to a structure usually used for habitation or storage; shall be punished as a court-martial may direct.
