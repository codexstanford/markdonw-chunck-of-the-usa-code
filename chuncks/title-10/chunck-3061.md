
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 76— MISSING PERSONS
#### § 1501a. Public-private partnerships; other forms of support
###### (g) Definitions .

In this section:

(1) Cooperative agreement .

The term “cooperative agreement” means an authorized cooperative agreement as described in section 6305 of title 31 .

(2) Grant .

The term “grant” means an authorized grant as described in section 6304 of title 31 .

(3) Gift .

The term “gift” includes a devise or bequest.
