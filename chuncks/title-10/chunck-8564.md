
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle E— Reserve Components
### CHAPTER 1411— ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS RELATING TO INVOLUNTARY SEPARATION
#### § 14902. Separation for substandard performance and for certain other reasons
###### (a) Substandard Performance of Duty .

The Secretary of the military department concerned shall prescribe, by regulation, procedures for the review at any time of the record of any reserve officer to determine whether that officer should be required, because that officer’s performance has fallen below standards prescribed by the Secretary concerned, to show cause for retention in an active status.
