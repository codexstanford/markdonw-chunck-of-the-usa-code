
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle D— Air Force and Space Force
### CHAPTER 961— CIVIL RESERVE AIR FLEET
#### § 9515. Charter air transportation services: minimum annual purchase amount for carriers participating in Civil Reserve Air Fleet
###### (b) Consideration of Recommendations .

In carrying out subsection (a), the Secretary shall consider the recommendations on courses of action for the Civil Reserve Air Fleet as outlined in the report required by section 356 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2008 ( Public Law 110–181 ).
