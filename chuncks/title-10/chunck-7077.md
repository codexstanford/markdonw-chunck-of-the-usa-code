
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle C— Navy and Marine Corps
### CHAPTER 835— BANDS
#### § 8287. United States Marine Band; United States Marine Drum and Bugle Corps: composition; appointment and promotion of members
###### (e) Revocation of Appointment .

The Secretary of the Navy may revoke any appointment of a member of the Marine Band or Marine Drum and Bugle Corps. When a member’s appointment to a commissioned grade terminates under this subsection, such member is entitled, at the option of such member

(1) to be discharged from the Marine Corps; or

(2) to revert to the grade and status such member held at the time of appointment under this section.
