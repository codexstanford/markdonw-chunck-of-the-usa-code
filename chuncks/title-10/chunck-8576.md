
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle E— Reserve Components
### CHAPTER 1411— ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS RELATING TO INVOLUNTARY SEPARATION
#### § 14905. Officer considered for removal: retirement or discharge
###### (c) Separation Pay .

An officer who is discharged under subsection (b)(3) is entitled, if eligible therefor, to separation pay under section 1174(c) of this title .
