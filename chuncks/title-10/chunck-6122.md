
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 325— COST GROWTH—UNIT COST REPORTS (NUNN-MCCURDY)
#### § 4375. Breach of significant cost growth threshold or critical cost growth threshold: required action
###### (b) Breach of Critical Cost Growth Threshold .

If the program acquisition unit cost or procurement unit cost of a major defense acquisition program or designated major subprogram (as determined by the Secretary under section 4374 of this title ) increases by a percentage equal to or greater than the critical cost growth threshold for the program or subprogram, the Secretary of Defense shall take actions consistent with the requirements of sections 4376 and 4377 of this title.
