
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle B— Army
### CHAPTER 753— UNITED STATES MILITARY ACADEMY
#### § 7442a. Cadets: nomination in event of death, resignation, or expulsion from office of Member of Congress otherwise authorized to nominate
###### (c) Rule of Construction .

The nomination of a cadet by a Member of Congress pursuant to this section shall not be construed to permanently reallocate nominations under section 7442 of this title .
