
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 9A— AUDIT
#### § 240e. Audits: use of commercial data integration and analysis products in preparing audits
###### (a) Deployment of Data Analytics Capabilities .

The Secretary of Defense shall use competitive procedures under chapter 137  1 of this title to procure or develop technologies or services, including those based on commercially available information technologies and services to improve data collection and analyses to support preparation of auditable financial statements for the Department of Defense.
