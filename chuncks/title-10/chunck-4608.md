
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 157— TRANSPORTATION
#### § 2649. Civilian passengers and commercial cargoes: transportation on Department of Defense vessels, vehicles, and aircraft
###### (b) Crediting of Receipts .

Any amount received under subsection (a) with respect to transportation provided in response to an emergency, a disaster, or a request for humanitarian assistance may be credited to the appropriation, fund, or account used in incurring the obligation for which such amount is received. In all other cases, amounts received under subsection (a) shall be covered into the Treasury as miscellaneous receipts.
