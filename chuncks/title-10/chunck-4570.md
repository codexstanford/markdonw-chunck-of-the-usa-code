
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 157— TRANSPORTATION
#### § 2640. Charter air transportation of members of the armed forces or cargo
###### (e) Authority To Leave Unsafe Aircraft .

A representative of the Military Airlift Command, the Military Traffic Management Command, or such other agency as may be designated by the Secretary of Defense (or if there is no such representative reasonably available, the senior officer on board a chartered aircraft) may order members of the armed forces or cargo to leave a chartered aircraft if the representative (or officer) determines that a condition exists on the aircraft which may endanger the safety of the members or cargo.
