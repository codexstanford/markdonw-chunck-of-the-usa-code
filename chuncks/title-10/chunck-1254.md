
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 36— PROMOTION, SEPARATION, AND INVOLUNTARY RETIREMENT OF OFFICERS ON THE ACTIVE-DUTY LIST
#### § 649f. Failure of selection for promotion
###### (b) Inapplicability of Failure of Selection for Promotion to Officers Above Promotion Zone .

The reference in section 627 of this title to an officer above the promotion zone shall not apply in the promotion of officers described in subsection (a).
