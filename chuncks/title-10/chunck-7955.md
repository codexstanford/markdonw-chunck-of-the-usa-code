
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle D— Air Force and Space Force
### CHAPTER 967— UTILITIES AND SERVICES
#### § 9594. Department of the Air Force Military History Institute: fee for providing historical information to the public
###### (a) Authority .

Except as provided in subsection (b), the Secretary of the Air Force may charge a person a fee for providing the person with information from the United States Department of the Air Force Military History Institute that is requested by that person.
