
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 69— RETIRED GRADE
#### § 1370a. Officers entitled to retired pay for non-regular service
###### (h) Highest Permanent Grade Defined .

In this section, the term “highest permanent grade” means a grade at or below the grade of major general in the Army, Air Force, or Marine Corps or rear admiral in the Navy.
