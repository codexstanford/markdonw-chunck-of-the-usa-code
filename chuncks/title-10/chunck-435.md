
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 8— DEFENSE AGENCIES AND DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE FIELD ACTIVITIES
#### § 198. Office of Local Defense Community Cooperation
###### (b) Director .

The Office shall be headed by the Director of the Office of Local Defense Community Cooperation, who shall be appointed by the Secretary of Defense from among civilian employees of the Federal Government or private individuals who have the following:

(1) Experience in the interagency in the Executive Branch.

(2) Experience in the administration and management of Federal grants programs.
