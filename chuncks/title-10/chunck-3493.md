
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 87— DEFENSE ACQUISITION WORKFORCE
#### § 1743. Awards to recognize members of the acquisition workforce
###### (b) Number of Awards.

(1) In general .

The President of the Defense Acquisition University may make not more than five awards under subsection (a)(1) and one award under subsection (a)(2) each year.

(2) Limitation .

A member of the acquisition workforce may receive one award each year.
