
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 60— SEPARATION OF REGULAR OFFICERS FOR SUBSTANDARD PERFORMANCE OF DUTY OR FOR CERTAIN OTHER REASONS
#### § 1187. Officers eligible to serve on boards
###### (c) Ineligibility by Reason of Previous Consideration of Same Officer .

No person may be a member of more than one board convened under this chapter to consider the same officer.
