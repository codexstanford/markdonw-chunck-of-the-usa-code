
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 32— OFFICER STRENGTH AND DISTRIBUTION IN GRADE
#### § 526a. Authorized strength after December 31, 2022 : general officers and flag officers on active duty
###### (e) Temporary Exclusion for Assignment to Certain Temporary Billets.

(1) In general .

The limitations in subsection (a) do not apply to a general officer or flag officer assigned to a temporary joint duty assignment designated by the Secretary of Defense.

(2) Duration of exclusion .

A general officer or flag officer assigned to a temporary joint duty assignment as described in paragraph (1) may not be excluded under this subsection from the limitations in subsection (a) for a period of longer than one year.
