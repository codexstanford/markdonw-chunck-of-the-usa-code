
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 16— SECURITY COOPERATION
#### § 346. Distribution to certain foreign personnel of education and training materials and information technology to enhance military interoperability with the armed forces
###### (d) Applicability of Export Control Regimes .

The provision of learning content and information technology under this section shall be subject to the provisions of the Arms Export Control Act ( 22 U.S.C. 2751 et seq.) and any other export control regime under law relating to the transfer of military technology to foreign countries.
