
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 275— PROPRIETARY CONTRACTOR DATA AND RIGHTS IN TECHNICAL DATA
#### § 3781. Technical data: contractor justification for restrictions; review of restrictions
###### (a) Contracts Covered by Subchapter .

This subchapter applies to any contract for supplies or services entered into by the Department of Defense that includes provisions for the delivery of technical data.
