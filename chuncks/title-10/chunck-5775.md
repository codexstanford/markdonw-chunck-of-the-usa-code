
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 277— CONTRACT FINANCING
#### § 3806. Action in case of fraud
###### (a) Remedy Coordination Official Defined .

In this section, the term “remedy coordination official”, with respect to an agency, means the person or entity in that agency who coordinates within that agency the administration of criminal, civil, administrative, and contractual remedies resulting from investigations of fraud or corruption related to procurement activities.
