
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle D— Air Force and Space Force
### CHAPTER 979— REAL PROPERTY
#### § 9782. Maintenance and repair of real property
###### (d) Operation and Maintenance Funds .

The provision described in this subsection is a provision of a national defense authorization Act that authorizes funds to be appropriated for a fiscal year to the Air Force or the Space Force for operation and maintenance.
