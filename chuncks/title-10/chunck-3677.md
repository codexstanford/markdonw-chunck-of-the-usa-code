
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 101— TRAINING GENERALLY
#### § 2017. Limitation on establishment of postsecondary educational institutions pending notice to Congress
###### (a) Limitation .

The Secretary of Defense may not establish a postsecondary educational institution within the Department of Defense until a period of one year has elapsed following the date on which the Secretary notifies the congressional defense committees of the intent of the Secretary to establish the institution.
