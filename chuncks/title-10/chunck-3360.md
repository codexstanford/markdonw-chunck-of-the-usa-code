
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 81— CIVILIAN EMPLOYEES
#### § 1599j. Restricted reports of incidents of adult sexual assault
###### (e) Definitions .

In this section:

(1) Civilian employee .

The term “civilian employee” has the meaning given the term “employee” in section 2105 of title 5 .

(2) Sexual assault .

The term “sexual assault” has the meaning given that term in section 920 of this title (article 120 of the Uniform Code of Military Justice), and includes penetrative offenses and sexual contact offenses.
