
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 23— MISCELLANEOUS STUDIES AND REPORTS
#### § 484. Quarterly cyber operations briefings
###### (c) Documents .

Each briefing under subsection (a) shall include a classified placemat, summarizing the elements specified in paragraphs (1), (2), (3), and (5) of subsection (b), and an unclassified memorandum, summarizing the briefing’s contents.
