
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 113— DEFENSE CIVILIAN TRAINING CORPS
#### § 2200g. Establishment
###### (c) Use of Resources and Programs .

The Under Secretary of Defense for Acquisition and Sustainment may leverage the resources and programs of the acquisition research organization within a civilian college or university that is described under section 4142(a) of this title (commonly referred to as the “Acquisition Innovation Research Center”) to carry out the requirements of this chapter.
