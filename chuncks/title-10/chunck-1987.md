
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 47A— MILITARY COMMISSIONS
#### § 950g. Review by United States Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia Circuit; writ of certiorari to Supreme Court
###### (e) Review by Supreme Court .

The Supreme Court may review by writ of certiorari pursuant to section 1254 of title 28 the final judgment of the United States Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia Circuit under this section.
