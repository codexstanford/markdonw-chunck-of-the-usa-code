
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 163— MILITARY CLAIMS
#### § 2738. Property loss: reimbursement of members for certain losses of household effects caused by hostile action
###### (c) Limitation .

The Secretary may provide reimbursement under this section for a loss described in subsection (b) only to the extent that the loss is not reimbursed under insurance or under the authority of another provision of law.
