
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 83— CIVILIAN DEFENSE INTELLIGENCE EMPLOYEES
#### § 1610. Reductions and other adjustments in force
###### (c) Regulations Relating to Defense Intelligence SES .

The regulations relating to removal from the Defense Intelligence Senior Executive Service in a reduction in force or other adjustment in force shall be consistent with section 3595(a) of title 5 .
