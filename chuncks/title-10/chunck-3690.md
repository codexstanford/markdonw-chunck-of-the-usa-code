
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 102— JUNIOR RESERVE OFFICERS’ TRAINING CORPS
#### § 2035. Flexibility in authorities for management of programs and units
###### (c) Flexibility in Allocation and Use of Travel Funding .

The Secretaries of the military departments shall take appropriate actions to provide so-called regional directors of the Junior Reserve Officers’ Training Corps programs located at remote rural schools enhanced discretion in the allocation and use of funds for travel in connection with Junior Reserve Officers’ Training Corps activities.
