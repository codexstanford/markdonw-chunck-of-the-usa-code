
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 36— PROMOTION, SEPARATION, AND INVOLUNTARY RETIREMENT OF OFFICERS ON THE ACTIVE-DUTY LIST
#### § 649b. Selection for promotion
###### (b) No Recommendation for Promotion of Officers Below Promotion Zone .

Section 616(b) of this title shall not apply to the selection for promotion of officers described in subsection (a).
