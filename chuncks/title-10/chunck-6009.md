
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 307— TEST AND EVALUATION
#### § 4173. Department of Defense Test Resource Management Center
###### (g) Supervision of Director by Under Secretary .

The Director of the Center shall be subject to the supervision of the Under Secretary of Defense for Research and Engineering. The Director shall report directly to the Under Secretary, without the interposition of any other supervising official.
