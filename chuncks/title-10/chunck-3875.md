
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 111— SUPPORT OF SCIENCE, MATHEMATICS, AND ENGINEERING EDUCATION
#### § 2193b. Improvement of education in technical fields: program for support of elementary and secondary education in science, technology, engineering, art and design, and mathematics
###### (a) Authority for Program .

The Secretary of Defense may conduct a science, technology, engineering, art and design, and mathematics education improvement program known as the “Department of Defense STARBASE Program”. The Secretary shall carry out the program in coordination with the Secretaries of the military departments and the Secretary of the Department in which the Coast Guard is operating.
