
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 327— WEAPON SYSTEMS DEVELOPMENT AND RELATED MATTERS
#### § 4424. Mechanisms to speed deployment of successful weapon system component or technology prototypes
###### (c) Notification to Congress .

Within 30 days after the service acquisition executive of a military department selects a weapon system component or technology project for a follow-on production contract or other transaction, the service acquisition executive shall notify the congressional defense committees of the selection and provide a brief description of the rapid fielding project.
