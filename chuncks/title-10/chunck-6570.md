
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle B— Army
### CHAPTER 715— APPOINTMENTS IN THE REGULAR ARMY
#### § 7151. Commissioned officer grades

The commissioned grades in the Regular Army are:

(1) Major general.

(2) Brigadier general.

(3) Colonel.

(4) Lieutenant colonel.

(5) Major.

(6) Captain.

(7) First lieutenant.

(8) Second lieutenant.
