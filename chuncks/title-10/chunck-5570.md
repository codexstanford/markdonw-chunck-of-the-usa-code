
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 245— TASK AND DELIVERY ORDER CONTRACTS (MULTIPLE AWARD CONTRACTS)
#### § 3405. Task order contracts: advisory and assistance services
###### (e) Required Content of Solicitation and Contract.

(1) Solicitation .

The solicitation for the proposed task order contract shall include the information (regarding services) described in section 3403(b) of this title .

(2) Contract .

A task order contract entered into under this section shall contain the same information that is required by paragraph (1) to be included in the solicitation of offers for that contract.
