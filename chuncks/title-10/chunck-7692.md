
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle D— Air Force and Space Force
### CHAPTER 908— THE SPACE FORCE
#### § 9082. Chief of Space Operations
###### (b) Grade .

The Chief, while so serving, has the grade in the Space Force equivalent to the grade of general in the Army, Air Force, and Marine Corps, or admiral in the Navy without vacating the permanent grade of the officer.
