
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 80— MISCELLANEOUS INVESTIGATION REQUIREMENTS AND OTHER DUTIES
#### § 1561. Complaints of sexual harassment: investigation by commanding officers
###### (a) Action on Complaints Alleging Sexual Harassment .

A commanding officer or officer in charge of a unit, vessel, facility, or area of the Army, Navy, Air Force, Marine Corps, or Space Force who receives from a member of the command or a civilian employee under the supervision of the officer a complaint alleging sexual harassment by a member of the armed forces or a civilian employee of the Department of Defense shall carry out an investigation of the matter in accordance with this section.
