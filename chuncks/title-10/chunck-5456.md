
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 221— PLANNING AND SOLICITATION GENERALLY
#### § 3205. Simplified procedures for small purchases
###### (b) Prohibition on Dividing Contracts .

A proposed purchase or contract for an amount above the simplified acquisition threshold may not be divided into several purchases or contracts for lesser amounts in order to use the simplified procedures required by subsection (a).
