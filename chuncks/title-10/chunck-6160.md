
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 341— ACQUISITION OF SERVICES GENERALLY
#### § 4501. Procurement of contract services: management structure
###### (d) Contract Services Acquisition Categories .

The management structure implemented pursuant to subsection (a) shall provide that the Under Secretary shall establish contract services acquisition categories, based on dollar thresholds, for the purpose of establishing the level of review, decision authority, and applicable procedures in such categories.
