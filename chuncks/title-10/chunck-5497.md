
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 223— OTHER PROVISIONS RELATING TO PLANNING AND SOLICITATION GENERALLY
#### § 3243. Encouragement of new competitors: qualification requirement
###### (f) Restriction on Enforcement .

Except in an emergency as determined by the head of the agency, whenever the head of the agency determines not to enforce a qualification requirement for a solicitation, the agency may not thereafter enforce that qualification requirement unless the agency complies with the requirements of subsection (b).
