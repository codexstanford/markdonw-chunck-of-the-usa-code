
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 71— COMPUTATION OF RETIRED PAY
#### § 1406. Retired pay base for members who first became members before September 8, 1980 : final basic pay
###### (f) Coast Guard .

In the case of a member who is retired under any section of title 14, the member’s retired pay is computed under section 423(a)  1 of title 14 in the manner provided in that section.
