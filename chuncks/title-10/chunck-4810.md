
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 160— ENVIRONMENTAL RESTORATION
#### § 2702. Research, development, and demonstration program
###### (b) Special Permit .

The Administrator may use the authorities of section 3005(g) of the Solid Waste Disposal Act ( 42 U.S.C. 6925(g) ) to issue a permit for testing and evaluation which receives support under this section.
