
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 221— PLANNING AND SOLICITATION GENERALLY
#### § 3203. Exclusion of particular source or restriction of solicitation to small business concerns
###### (c) Inapplicability of Justification and Approval Requirements .

A contract awarded pursuant to the competitive procedures referred to in subsections (a)(1) and (b) shall not be subject to the justification and approval required by section 3204(e)(1) of this title .
