
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle D— Air Force and Space Force
### CHAPTER 967— UTILITIES AND SERVICES
#### § 9594. Department of the Air Force Military History Institute: fee for providing historical information to the public
###### (d) Retention of Fees .

Amounts received under subsection (a) for providing information in any fiscal year shall be credited to the appropriation or appropriations charged the costs of providing information to the public from the United States Department of the Air Force Military History Institute during that fiscal year.
