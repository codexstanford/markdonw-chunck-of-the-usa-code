
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 241— AWARDING OF CONTRACTS
#### § 3305. Pre-award debriefings
###### (a) Request for Debriefing .

When the contracting officer excludes an offeror submitting a competitive proposal from the competitive range (or otherwise excludes such an offeror from further consideration prior to the final source selection decision), the excluded offeror may request in writing, within three days after the date on which the excluded offeror receives notice of its exclusion, a debriefing prior to award.
