
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle E— Reserve Components
### CHAPTER 1407— FAILURE OF SELECTION FOR PROMOTION AND INVOLUNTARY SEPARATION
#### § 14508. Removal from the reserve active-status list for years of service: reserve general and flag officers
###### (b) Thirty-Five Years Service or Five Years in Grade for Major Generals and Rear Admirals .

Unless retired, transferred to the Retired Reserve, or discharged at an earlier date, each reserve officer of the Army, Air Force, or Marine Corps in the grade of major general, and each reserve officer of the Navy in the grade of rear admiral, shall, 30 days after completion of 35 years of commissioned service or on the fifth anniversary of the date of the officer’s appointment in the grade of major general or rear admiral, whichever is later, be separated in accordance with section 14514 of this title .
