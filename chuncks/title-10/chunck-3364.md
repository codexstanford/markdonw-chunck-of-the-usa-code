
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 83— CIVILIAN DEFENSE INTELLIGENCE EMPLOYEES
#### § 1602. Basic pay
###### (a) Authority To Fix Rates of Basic Pay .

The Secretary of Defense (subject to the provisions of this section) shall fix the rates of basic pay for positions established under section 1601 of this title in relation to the rates of pay provided for comparable positions in the Department of Defense and subject to the same limitations on maximum rates of pay established for employees of the Department of Defense by law or regulation.
