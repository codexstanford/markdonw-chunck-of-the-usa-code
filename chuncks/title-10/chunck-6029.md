
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 321— GENERAL MATTERS
#### § 4204. Milestone decision authority
###### (a) Service Acquisition Executive .

The milestone decision authority for a major defense acquisition program reaching Milestone A after October 1, 2016 , shall be the service acquisition executive of the military department that is managing the program, unless the Secretary of Defense designates, under subsection (b), another official to serve as the milestone decision authority.
