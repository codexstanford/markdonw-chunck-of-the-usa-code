
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle E— Reserve Components
### CHAPTER 1608— HEALTH PROFESSIONS STIPEND PROGRAM
#### § 16201. Financial assistance: health-care professionals in reserve components
###### (g) Amount of Stipend .

The amount of a stipend under an agreement under subsection (b), (c), (d), or (f) shall be the stipend rate in effect for participants in the Armed Forces Health Professions Scholarship Program under section 2121(d) of this title .
