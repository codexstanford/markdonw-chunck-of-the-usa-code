
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle E— Reserve Components
### CHAPTER 1214— READY RESERVE MOBILIZATION INCOME INSURANCE
#### § 12524. Enrollment and election of benefits
###### (b) Election of Benefit Amount .

The amount of a member’s monthly benefit under an enrollment shall be the basic benefit under subsection (a) of section 12525 of this title unless the member elects a different benefit under subsection (b) of such section within 60 days after first becoming a member of the Ready Reserve or within 60 days after being offered the opportunity to enroll, as the case may be.
