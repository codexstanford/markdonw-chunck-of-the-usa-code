
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle E— Reserve Components
### CHAPTER 1606— EDUCATIONAL ASSISTANCE FOR MEMBERS OF THE SELECTED RESERVE
#### § 16135. Failure to participate satisfactorily; penalties
###### (b) Effect of Repayment .

Any repayment under section 303a(e) or 373 of title 37 shall not affect the period of obligation of a member to serve as a Reserve in the Selected Reserve.
