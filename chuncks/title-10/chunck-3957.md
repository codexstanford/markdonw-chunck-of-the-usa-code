
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 131— PLANNING AND COORDINATION
#### § 2218. National Defense Sealift Fund
###### (i) Title or Management of Vessels .

Nothing in this section (other than subsection (c)(1)(E)) shall be construed to affect or modify title to, management of, or funding responsibilities for, any vessel of the National Defense Reserve Fleet, or assigned to the Ready Reserve Force component of the National Defense Reserve Fleet, as established by section 57100 of title 46 .
