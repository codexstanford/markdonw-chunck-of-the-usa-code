
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 257— CONTRACTS FOR LONG-TERM LEASE OR CHARTER OF VESSELS, AIRCRAFT, AND COMBAT VEHICLES
#### § 3675. Capital lease or lease-purchase treated as an acquisition
###### (b) Definitions .

In this section, the terms “capital lease” and “lease-purchase” have the meanings given those terms in Appendix B to Office of Management and Budget Circular A–11, as in effect on January 6, 2006 .
