
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 47A— MILITARY COMMISSIONS
#### § 948j. Military judge of a military commission
###### (b) Eligibility .

A military judge shall be a commissioned officer of the armed forces who is a member of the bar of a Federal court, or a member of the bar of the highest court of a State, and who is certified to be qualified for duty under section 826 of this title (article 26 of the Uniform Code of Military Justice) as a military judge of general courts-martial by the Judge Advocate General of the armed force of which such military judge is a member.
