
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 146— CONTRACTING FOR PERFORMANCE OF CIVILIAN COMMERCIAL OR INDUSTRIAL TYPE FUNCTIONS
#### § 2474. Centers of Industrial and Technical Excellence: designation; public-private partnerships
###### (f) Exclusion of Certain Expenditures From Percentage Limitation .

Amounts expended for the performance of a depot-level maintenance and repair workload by non-Federal Government personnel at a Center of Industrial and Technical Excellence under any contract shall not be counted for purposes of applying the percentage limitation in section 2466(a) of this title if the personnel are provided by private industry or other entities outside the Department of Defense pursuant to a public-private partnership.
