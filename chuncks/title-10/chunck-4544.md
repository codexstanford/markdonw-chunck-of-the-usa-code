
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 155— ACCEPTANCE OF GIFTS AND SERVICES
#### § 2615. Military museums and military education programs: cooperative agreement authority
###### (b) Cooperative Agreement Described .

For purposes of subsection (a), an authorized cooperative agreement is described in section 6305 of title 31 , except that the use of a cooperative agreement by the Secretary concerned is limited to nonprofit entities.
