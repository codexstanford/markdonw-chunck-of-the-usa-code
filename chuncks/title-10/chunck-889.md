
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 21— DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE INTELLIGENCE MATTERS
#### § 437. Congressional oversight
###### (c) Congressional Intelligence Committees Defined .

In this section, the term “congressional intelligence committees” has the meaning given the term in section 3 of the National Security Act of 1947 ( 50 U.S.C. 3003 ).
