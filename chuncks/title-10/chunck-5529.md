
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 241— AWARDING OF CONTRACTS
#### § 3305. Pre-award debriefings
###### (e) Information Not to Be Disclosed .

The debriefing conducted under subsections (a) and (b) may not disclose the number or identity of other offerors and shall not disclose information about the content, ranking, or evaluation of other offerors’ proposals.
