
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 273— ALLOWABLE COSTS
#### § 3763. Bid and proposal costs: allowable costs
###### (a) Regulations .

The Secretary of Defense shall prescribe regulations governing the payment by the Department of Defense of expenses incurred by contractors for bid and proposal costs. Such regulations shall provide that expenses incurred for bid and proposal costs shall be reported independently from other allowable indirect costs.
