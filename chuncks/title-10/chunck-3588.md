
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 88— MILITARY FAMILY PROGRAMS AND MILITARY CHILD CARE
#### § 1791. Funding for military child care
###### (b) Responsibility for Allocations of Certain Funds .

The Secretary of Defense shall be responsible for the allocation of Office of the Secretary of Defense level funds for military child development programs for children from birth through 12 years of age, and may not delegate such responsibility to the military departments.
