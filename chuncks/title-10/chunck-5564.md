
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 245— TASK AND DELIVERY ORDER CONTRACTS (MULTIPLE AWARD CONTRACTS)
#### § 3403. Task and delivery order contracts: general authority
###### (g) Inapplicability to Contracts for Advisory and Assistance Services .

Except as otherwise specifically provided in section 3405 of this title , this section does not apply to a task or delivery order contract for the procurement of advisory and assistance services (as defined in section 1105(g) of title 31 ).
