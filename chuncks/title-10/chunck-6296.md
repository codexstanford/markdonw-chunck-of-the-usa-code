
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 382— POLICIES AND PLANNING
#### § 4820. National technology and industrial base plans, policy, and guidance
###### (a) In General .

The Secretary of Defense shall prescribe regulations requiring consideration of the national technology and industrial base, in accordance with the strategy required by section 4811 of this title , in the development and implementation of acquisition plans for each major defense acquisition program.
