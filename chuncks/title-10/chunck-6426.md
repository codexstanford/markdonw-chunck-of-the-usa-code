
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 385— OTHER TECHNOLOGY BASE POLICIES AND PROGRAMS
#### § 4892. Availability of samples, drawings, information, equipment, materials, and certain services
###### (d) Use of Fees .

Fees received under subsections (a)(3) and (a)(4) may be credited to the appropriations or other funds of the activity making such services available.
