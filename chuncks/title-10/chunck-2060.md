
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 49— MISCELLANEOUS PROHIBITIONS AND PENALTIES
#### § 988. Prohibition on ownership or trading of stocks in certain companies by certain officials of the Department of Defense
###### (a) Prohibition .

Except as provided in subsection (b), a covered official of the Department of Defense may not own or purchase publicly traded stock of a company if that company is one of the 10 entities awarded the most amount of contract funds by the Department of Defense in a fiscal year during the five preceding fiscal years.
