
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 15— MILITARY SUPPORT FOR CIVILIAN LAW ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES
#### § 283. Situations involving bombings of places of public use, Government facilities, public transportation systems, and infrastructure facilities
###### (b) Rendering-safe Support .

Military explosive ordnance disposal units providing rendering-safe support to Department of Justice activities relating to the enforcement of section 175, 229, or 2332a of title 18 in emergency situations involving weapons of mass destruction shall provide such support in a manner consistent with the provisions of section 382  1 of this title.
