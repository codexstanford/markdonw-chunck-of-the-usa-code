
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 138— COOPERATIVE AGREEMENTS WITH NATO ALLIES AND OTHER COUNTRIES
#### § 2350n. North Atlantic Treaty Organization Joint Force Command
###### (a) Authorization .

The Secretary of Defense shall authorize the establishment of, and the participation by members of the armed forces in, the North Atlantic Treaty Organization Joint Force Command (in this section referred to as the “Joint Force Command”), to be established in the United States.
