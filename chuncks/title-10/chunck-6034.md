
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 321— GENERAL MATTERS
#### § 4204. Milestone decision authority
###### (f) Limitation on Authority to Designate Alternative MDA for Programs Addressing Joint Requirements .

The authority of the Secretary of Defense to designate an alternative milestone decision authority for a program with respect to which the Secretary determines that the program is addressing a joint requirement, as set forth in subsection (b)(1), shall apply only for a major defense acquisition program that reaches Milestone A after October 1, 2016 , and before October 1, 2019 .
