
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 58— BENEFITS AND SERVICES FOR MEMBERS BEING SEPARATED OR RECENTLY SEPARATED
#### § 1154. Assistance to eligible members and former members to obtain employment as teachers: Troops-to-Teachers Program
###### (k) Sunset .

The Program shall terminate on July 1, 2025 , with respect to the selection of new participants for the program. Participants in the Program as of that date may complete their program, and remain eligible for benefits under this section.
