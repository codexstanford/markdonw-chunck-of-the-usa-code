
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 155— ACCEPTANCE OF GIFTS AND SERVICES
#### § 2610. Competitions for excellence: acceptance of monetary awards
###### (b) Disposition of Awards .

A monetary award accepted under subsection (a) shall be credited to one or more nonappropriated fund accounts supporting morale, welfare, and recreation activities for the command, installation, or other activity that is recognized for the award. Amounts so credited may be expended only for such activities.
