
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 69— RETIRED GRADE
#### § 1370a. Officers entitled to retired pay for non-regular service
###### (b) Service-in-grade Requirement for Officers in Grades Below O–5.

In order to be credited with satisfactory service in an officer grade (other than a warrant officer grade) below the grade of lieutenant colonel or commander (in the case of the Navy), a person covered by subsection (a) must have served satisfactorily in that grade (as determined by the Secretary of the military department concerned) as a reserve commissioned officer in an active status, or in a retired status on active duty, for not less than six months.
