
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle B— Army
### CHAPTER 773— DISPOSAL OF OBSOLETE OR SURPLUS MATERIAL
#### § 7687. Sale of excess, obsolete, or unserviceable ammunition and ammunition components
###### (e) Verification of Demilitarization .

The Secretary shall establish procedures for ensuring that a purchaser of ammunition or ammunition components under this section demilitarizes the ammunition or ammunition components in accordance with any agreement to do so under subsection (a)(1). The procedures shall include onsite verification of demilitarization activities.
