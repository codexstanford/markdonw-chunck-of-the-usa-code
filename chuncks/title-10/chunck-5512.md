
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 241— AWARDING OF CONTRACTS
#### § 3302. Sealed bids
###### (c) Notice of Award .

The award of a contract shall be made by transmitting, in writing or by electronic means, notice of the award to the successful bidder. Within three days after the date of contract award, the head of the agency shall notify, in writing or by electronic means, each bidder not awarded the contract that the contract has been awarded.
