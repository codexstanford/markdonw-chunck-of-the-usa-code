
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 21— DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE INTELLIGENCE MATTERS
#### § 437. Congressional oversight
###### (a) Proposed Regulations .

Copies of regulations proposed to be prescribed under section 436 of this title (including any proposed revision to such regulations) shall be submitted to congressional defense committees and the congressional intelligence committees not less than 30 days before they take effect.
