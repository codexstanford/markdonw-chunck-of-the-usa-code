
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 245— TASK AND DELIVERY ORDER CONTRACTS (MULTIPLE AWARD CONTRACTS)
#### § 3405. Task order contracts: advisory and assistance services
###### (a) Advisory and Assistance Services Defined .

In this section, the term “advisory and assistance services” has the meaning given such term in section 1105(g) of title 31 .
