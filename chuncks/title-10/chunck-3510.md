
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 87— DEFENSE ACQUISITION WORKFORCE
#### § 1746a. Acquisition workforce educational partnerships
###### (g) Definitions .

In this section:

(1) Acquisition workforce .

The term “acquisition workforce” has the meaning given such term in section 1705(g) of this title .

(2) Extramural institutions .

The term “extramural institutions” means participants in an activity established under section 4142 of this title , public sector organizations, and nonprofit credentialing organizations.
