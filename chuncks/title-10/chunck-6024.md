
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 321— GENERAL MATTERS
#### § 4202. Authority to increase definitional threshold amounts: major defense acquisition programs; major systems
###### (c) Notification to Congressional Committees .

An adjustment under subsection (a) or (b) shall be effective after the Secretary transmits to the Committee on Armed Services of the Senate and the Committee on Armed Services of the House of Representatives a written notification of the adjustment.
