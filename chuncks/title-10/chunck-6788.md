
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle B— Army
### CHAPTER 764— ARMAMENTS INDUSTRIAL BASE
#### § 7555. ARMS Initiative loan guarantee program
###### (b) Advanced Budget Authority .

Loan guarantees under this section may not be committed except to the extent that appropriations of budget authority to cover their costs are made in advance, as required by section 504 of the Federal Credit Reform Act of 1990 ( 2 U.S.C. 661c ).
