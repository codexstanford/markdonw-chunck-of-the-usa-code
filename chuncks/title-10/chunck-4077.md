
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 135— SPACE PROGRAMS
#### § 2273a. Space Rapid Capabilities Office
###### (a) In General .

There is within the Space Force a program office known as the Space Rapid Capabilities Office (in this section referred to as the “Office”). The facilities of the Office may not be co-located with the headquarters facilities of the Air Force Space and Missile Systems Center.
