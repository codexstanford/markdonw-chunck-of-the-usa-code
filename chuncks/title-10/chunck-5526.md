
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 241— AWARDING OF CONTRACTS
#### § 3305. Pre-award debriefings
###### (b) When Debriefing to Be Conducted .

The contracting officer shall make every effort to debrief the unsuccessful offeror as soon as practicable but may refuse the request for a debriefing if it is not in the best interests of the Government to conduct a debriefing at that time.
