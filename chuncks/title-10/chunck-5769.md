
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 277— CONTRACT FINANCING
#### § 3804. Conditions for progress payments
###### (b) Limitation .

The Secretary shall ensure that progress payments referred to in subsection (a) are not made for more than 80 percent of the work accomplished under a defense contract so long as the Secretary has not made the contractual terms, specifications, and price definite.
