
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 327— WEAPON SYSTEMS DEVELOPMENT AND RELATED MATTERS
#### § 4421. Weapon system component or technology prototype projects: display of budget information
###### (c) Definitions .

In this section, the terms “budget” and “defense budget materials” have the meaning given those terms in section 234 of this title and the term “commercial product” has the meaning given that term in section 103 of title 41 .
