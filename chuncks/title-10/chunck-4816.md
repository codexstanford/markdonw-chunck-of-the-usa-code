
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 160— ENVIRONMENTAL RESTORATION
#### § 2703. Environmental restoration accounts
###### (d) Budget Reports .

In proposing the budget for any fiscal year pursuant to section 1105 of title 31 , the President shall set forth separately the amounts requested for environmental restoration programs of the Department of Defense and of each of the military departments under this chapter and under any other Act.
