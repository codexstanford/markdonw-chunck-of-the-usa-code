
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 138— COOPERATIVE AGREEMENTS WITH NATO ALLIES AND OTHER COUNTRIES
#### § 2350d. Cooperative acquisition and logistics support agreements: NATO countries
###### (d) Application of Chapter 137.

Except as otherwise provided in this section, the provisions of chapter 137  1 of this title apply to a contract entered into by the Secretary of Defense for the acquisition of logistics support under a Support or Procurement Partnership Agreement.
