
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle E— Reserve Components
### CHAPTER 1214— READY RESERVE MOBILIZATION INCOME INSURANCE
#### § 12533. Termination of program
###### (d) Termination of Payment of Benefits .

The Secretary may not make any benefit payment under the insurance program after November 18, 1997 , other than to an insured member who on that date (1) is serving on an order to covered service, (2) has been issued an order directing performance of covered service, or (3) has served on covered service before that date for which benefits under the program have not been paid to the member.
