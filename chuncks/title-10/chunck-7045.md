
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle C— Navy and Marine Corps
### CHAPTER 825— ADMINISTRATION
#### § 8227. Notifications on manning of afloat naval forces
###### (c) Special Rule .

For purposes of determining whether a percentage of manning fit or manning fill has been achieved, a sailor in a more senior paygrade may count as filling the billet of a more junior paygrade, but a sailor in a more junior paygrade may not count as filling the billet of a more senior paygrade.
