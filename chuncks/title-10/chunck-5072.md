
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 169— MILITARY CONSTRUCTION AND MILITARY FAMILY HOUSING
#### § 2837. Housing Requirements and Market Analysis
###### (b) Prioritization of Installations.

(1) In general .

Except as provided in paragraph (2), the Secretary concerned shall prioritize the conduct of HRMAs for military installations

(A) for which an HRMA has not been conducted during the five-year period preceding the date of the enactment of this section; or

(B) in locations with housing shortages.

(2) Existing 5-year requirement .

Paragraph (1) shall not apply to a military department that required an HRMA to be conducted for each military installation not less frequently than once every five years before the date of the enactment of this section.
