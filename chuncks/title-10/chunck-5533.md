
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 241— AWARDING OF CONTRACTS
#### § 3308. Protests
###### (a) Protest File.

(1) Establishment and access .

If, in the case of a solicitation for a contract issued by, or an award or proposed award of a contract by, the head of an agency, a protest is filed pursuant to the procedures in subchapter V of chapter 35 of title 31 and an actual or prospective offeror so requests, a file of the protest shall be established by the procuring activity and reasonable access shall be provided to actual or prospective offerors.

(2) Redacted information .

Information exempt from disclosure under section 552 of title 5 may be redacted in a file established pursuant to paragraph (1) unless an applicable protective order provides otherwise.
