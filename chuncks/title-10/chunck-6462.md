
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 388— PROCUREMENT TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE COOPERATIVE AGREEMENT PROGRAM
#### § 4957. Subcontractor information
###### (a) Contractors to Provide Information .

The Secretary of Defense shall require that any defense contractor in any year shall provide to an eligible entity with which the Secretary has entered into a cooperative agreement under this chapter, on the request of such entity, the information specified in subsection (b).
