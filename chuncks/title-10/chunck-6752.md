
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle B— Army
### CHAPTER 763— PROCUREMENT
#### § 7541. Army arsenals: treatment of unutilized or underutilized plant-capacity costs
###### (a) Estimate of Costs .

The Secretary of the Army shall include in the budget justification documents submitted to Congress in support of the President’s budget for a fiscal year submitted under section 1105 of title 31 an estimate of the funds to be required in that fiscal year to cover unutilized and underutilized plant-capacity costs at Army arsenals.
