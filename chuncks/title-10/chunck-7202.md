
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle C— Navy and Marine Corps
### CHAPTER 853— UNITED STATES NAVAL ACADEMY
#### § 8476. Operation of Naval Academy dairy farm
###### (d) Effect of Other Laws .

Nothing in section 8471 of this title shall be construed to require the Secretary of the Navy or the Superintendent of the Naval Academy to operate a dairy farm for the Naval Academy in Gambrills, Maryland, or any other location.
