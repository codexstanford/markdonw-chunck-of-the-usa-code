
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 47— UNIFORM CODE OF MILITARY JUSTICE
#### § 861. Art. 61. Waiver of right to appeal; withdrawal of appeal
###### (a) Waiver of Right to Appeal .

After entry of judgment in a general or special court-martial, under procedures prescribed by the Secretary concerned, the accused may waive the right to appellate review in each case subject to such review under section 866 of this title (article 66). Such a waiver shall be

(1) signed by the accused and by defense counsel; and

(2) attached to the record of trial.
