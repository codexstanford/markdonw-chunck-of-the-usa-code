
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 1— DEFINITIONS, RULES OF CONSTRUCTION, CROSS REFERENCES, AND RELATED MATTERS
#### § 102. Effect of certain amendments on conforming changes to tables of sections, tables of contents, and similar tabular entries
###### (a) Automatic Execution of Conforming Changes .

When an amendment to a covered defense law adds a section or larger organizational unit to the covered defense law, repeals or transfers a section or larger organizational unit in the covered defense law, or amends the designation or heading of a section or larger organizational unit in the covered defense law, that amendment also shall have the effect of amending any table of sections, table of contents, or similar tabular entries in the covered defense law to alter the table to conform to the changes made by the amendment.
