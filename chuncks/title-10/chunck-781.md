
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 19— CYBER AND INFORMATION OPERATIONS MATTERS
#### § 394. Authorities concerning military cyber operations
###### (c) Clandestine Activities or Operations .

A clandestine military activity or operation in cyberspace shall be considered a traditional military activity for the purposes of section 503(e)(2) of the National Security Act of 1947 ( 50 U.S.C. 3093(e)(2) ).
