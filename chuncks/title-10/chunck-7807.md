
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle D— Air Force and Space Force
### CHAPTER 951— TRAINING GENERALLY
#### § 9414b. United States Air Force Institute of Technology: administration
###### (b) Provost and Chief Academic Officer.

(1) In general .

There is established at the United States Air Force Institute of Technology the civilian position of Provost and Chief Academic Officer who shall be selected by the Secretary.

(2) Term .

An individual selected for the position of Provost and Chief Academic Officer shall serve in that position for a term of not more than five years and may be continued in that position for an additional term of up to five years.

(3) Compensation .

The individual serving as Provost and Chief Academic Officer is entitled to such compensation for such service as the Secretary shall prescribe for purposes of this section, but not more than the rate of compensation authorized for level IV of the Executive Schedule.
