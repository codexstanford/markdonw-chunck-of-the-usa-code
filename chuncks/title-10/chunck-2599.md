
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 57— DECORATIONS AND AWARDS
#### § 1124a. Cyber operations-peculiar awards
###### (d) Amounts .

The total amount of the award, or awards, made under this section for a novel action, invention, or technical achievement may not exceed $2,500, regardless of the number of persons who may be entitled to share therein.
