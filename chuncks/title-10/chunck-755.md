
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 19— CYBER AND INFORMATION OPERATIONS MATTERS
#### § 391. Reporting on cyber incidents with respect to networks and information systems of operationally critical contractors and certain other contractors
###### (a) Designation of Department Component to Receive Reports .

The Secretary of Defense shall designate a component of the Department of Defense to receive reports of cyber incidents from contractors in accordance with this section and section 393 of this title or from other governmental entities.
