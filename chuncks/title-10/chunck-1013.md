
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 24— NUCLEAR POSTURE
#### § 499a. Collection, storage, and sharing of data relating to nuclear security enterprise and nuclear forces
###### (b) Sharing of Data .

If the Director of Cost Assessment and Program Evaluation or the Director for Cost Estimating and Program Evaluation requests data relating to programs or projects from any element of the Department of Defense or from any element of the nuclear security enterprise of the National Nuclear Security Administration, that element shall provide that data in a timely manner.
