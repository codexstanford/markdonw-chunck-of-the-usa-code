
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 58— BENEFITS AND SERVICES FOR MEMBERS BEING SEPARATED OR RECENTLY SEPARATED
#### § 1155. Statement of benefits
###### (b) Statement for Reserves .

The Secretary concerned shall provide a member of a reserve component with a current assessment of benefits described in subsection (a) upon release of that member from active duty.
