
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 163— MILITARY CLAIMS
#### § 2738. Property loss: reimbursement of members for certain losses of household effects caused by hostile action
###### (d) Applicability of Other Authorities and Requirements .

Subsections (b), (d), (e), (f), and (g) of section 2733 of this title shall apply to a request for a reimbursement under this section as if the request were a claim against the United States.
