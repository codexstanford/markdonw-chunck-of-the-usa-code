
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle C— Navy and Marine Corps
### CHAPTER 865— SALVAGE FACILITIES
#### § 8702. Acquisition and transfer of vessels and equipment
###### (c) Reference to Authority To Advance Funds for Immediate Salvage Operations .

For authority for the Secretary of the Navy to advance to private salvage companies such funds as the Secretary considers necessary to provide for the immediate financing of salvage operations, see section 3808(b) of this title .
