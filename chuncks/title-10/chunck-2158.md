
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 53— MISCELLANEOUS RIGHTS AND BENEFITS
#### § 1052. Adoption expenses: reimbursement
###### (b) Adoptions Covered .

An adoption for which expenses may be reimbursed under this section includes an adoption by a single person, an infant adoption, an intercountry adoption, and an adoption of a child with special needs (as defined in section 473(c) of the Social Security Act ( 42 U.S.C. 673(c) )).
