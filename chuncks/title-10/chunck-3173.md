
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 80— MISCELLANEOUS INVESTIGATION REQUIREMENTS AND OTHER DUTIES
#### § 1561b. Confidential reporting of sexual harassment
###### (a) Reporting Process .

Notwithstanding section 1561 of this title , the Secretary of Defense shall prescribe in regulations a process by which a member of an armed force under the jurisdiction of the Secretary of a military department may confidentially allege a complaint of sexual harassment to an individual outside the immediate chain of command of the member.
