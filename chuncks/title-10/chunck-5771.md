
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 277— CONTRACT FINANCING
#### § 3805. Payments for commercial products and commercial services
###### (a) Terms and Conditions for Payments .

Payments under section 3801 of this title for commercial products and commercial services may be made under such terms and conditions as the head of the agency determines are appropriate or customary in the commercial marketplace and are in the best interests of the United States.
