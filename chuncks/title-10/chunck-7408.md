
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle C— Navy and Marine Corps
### CHAPTER 863— NAVAL VESSELS
#### § 8686. Support for transfers of decommissioned vessels and shipboard equipment
###### (a) Authority To Provide Assistance .

The Secretary of the Navy may provide an entity described in subsection (b) with assistance in support of a transfer of a vessel or shipboard equipment described in such subsection that is being executed under section 2572, 8676, 8677, or 8765 of this title, or under any other authority.
