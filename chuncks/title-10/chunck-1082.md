
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 32— OFFICER STRENGTH AND DISTRIBUTION IN GRADE
#### § 526. Authorized strength: general and flag officers on active duty
###### (l) Cessation of Applicability .

The provisions of this section shall not apply to the number of general officers and flag officers in the armed forces after December 31, 2022 . For provisions applicable to the number of such officers after that date, see section 526a of this title .
