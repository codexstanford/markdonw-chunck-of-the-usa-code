#### § 1065. Use of commissary stores and MWR facilities: certain veterans, caregivers for veterans, and Foreign Service officers
###### (f) Eligibility of Foreign Service Officers on Mandatory Home Leave .

A Foreign Service officer on mandatory home leave may be permitted to use military lodging referred to in subsection (h).
