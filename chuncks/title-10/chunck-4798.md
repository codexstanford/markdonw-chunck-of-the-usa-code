
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 160— ENVIRONMENTAL RESTORATION
#### § 2701. Environmental restoration program
###### (a) Environmental Restoration Program.

(1) In general .

The Secretary of Defense shall carry out a program of environmental restoration at facilities under the jurisdiction of the Secretary and at State-owned National Guard facilities. The program shall be known as the “Defense Environmental Restoration Program”.

(2) Application of section 120 of cercla .

Activities of the program described in subsection (b)(1) shall be carried out subject to, and in a manner consistent with, section 120 (relating to Federal facilities) of CERCLA ( 42 U.S.C. 9620 ).

(3) Consultation with epa .

The program shall be carried out in consultation with the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency.

(4) Administrative office within osd .

The Secretary shall identify an office within the Office of the Secretary which shall have responsibility for carrying out the program.
