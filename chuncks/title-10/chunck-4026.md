
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 134— MISCELLANEOUS ADMINISTRATIVE PROVISIONS
#### § 2246. Authorization of certain support for military service academy foundations
###### (a) Authority .

Subject to subsection (b) and pursuant to regulations prescribed by the Secretary of Defense, the Superintendent of a Service Academy may authorize a covered foundation to use, on an unreimbursed basis, facilities or equipment of such Service Academy.
