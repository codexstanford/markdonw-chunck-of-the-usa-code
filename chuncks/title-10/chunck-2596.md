
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 57— DECORATIONS AND AWARDS
#### § 1124a. Cyber operations-peculiar awards
###### (a) Authority .

The Secretary of Defense and the Secretaries of the military departments may authorize the payment of a cash award to, and incur necessary expense for the honorary recognition of, a member of the covered armed forces whose novel actions, invention, or technical achievement enables or ensures operational outcomes in or through cyberspace against threats to national security.
