
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle D— Air Force and Space Force
### CHAPTER 908— THE SPACE FORCE
#### § 9083. Office of the Chief of Space Operations: function; composition
###### (a) Function .

There is in the executive part of the Department of the Air Force an Office of the Chief of Space Operations to assist the Secretary of the Air Force in carrying out the responsibilities of the Secretary.
