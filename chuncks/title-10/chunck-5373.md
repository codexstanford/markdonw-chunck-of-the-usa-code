
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 203— GENERAL MATTERS
#### § 3064. Applicability of chapter 137 legacy provisions
###### (b) Applicability to Contracts for Installation or Alteration .

The provisions of this part that are chapter 137 legacy provisions that apply to the procurement of property apply also to contracts for its installation or alteration.
