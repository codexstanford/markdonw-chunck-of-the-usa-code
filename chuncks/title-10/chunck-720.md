
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 16— SECURITY COOPERATION
#### § 352. Naval Small Craft Instruction and Technical Training School
###### (d) Limitation on Personnel Eligible To Receive Education and Training .

The Secretary of Defense may not provide education or training at the School to any personnel of a country that is prohibited from receiving such education or training under any other provision of law.
