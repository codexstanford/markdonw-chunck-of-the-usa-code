
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle D— Air Force and Space Force
### CHAPTER 908— THE SPACE FORCE
#### § 9084. Office of the Chief of Space Operations: general duties
###### (a) Professional Assistance .

The Office of the Chief of Space Operations shall furnish professional assistance to the Secretary, the Under Secretary, and the Assistant Secretaries of the Air Force and to the Chief of Space Operations.
