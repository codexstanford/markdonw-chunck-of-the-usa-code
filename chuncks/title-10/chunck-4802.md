
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 160— ENVIRONMENTAL RESTORATION
#### § 2701. Environmental restoration program
###### (e) Response Action Contractors .

The provisions of section 119 of CERCLA ( 42 U.S.C. 9619 ) apply to response action contractors (as defined in that section) who carry out response actions under this section.
