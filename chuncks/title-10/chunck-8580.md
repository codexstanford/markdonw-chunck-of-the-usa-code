
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle E— Reserve Components
### CHAPTER 1411— ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS RELATING TO INVOLUNTARY SEPARATION
#### § 14907. Army National Guard of the United States and Air National Guard of the United States: discharge and withdrawal of Federal recognition of officers absent without leave
###### (b) Discharge From Reserve Appointment .

An officer of the Army National Guard of the United States or the Air National Guard of the United States whose Federal recognition as an officer of the National Guard is withdrawn under section 323(b) of title 32 shall be discharged from the officer’s appointment as a reserve officer of the Army or the Air Force, as the case may be.
