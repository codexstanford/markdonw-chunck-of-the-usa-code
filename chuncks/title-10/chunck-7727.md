
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle D— Air Force and Space Force
### CHAPTER 933— MISCELLANEOUS RIGHTS AND BENEFITS
#### § 9251. Presentation of United States flag upon retirement
###### (a) Presentation of Flag .

Upon the release of a member of the Air Force or the Space Force from active duty for retirement, the Secretary of the Air Force shall present a United States flag to the member.
