
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 387— SMALL BUSINESS PROGRAMS
#### § 4901. Department of Defense small business strategy
###### (e) Enhanced Outreach Under Procurement Technical Assistance Program Market .

The Secretary shall enable and promote activities to provide coordinated outreach to small business concerns through any procurement technical assistance program established under chapter 388 of this title to facilitate small business contracting with the Department of Defense.
