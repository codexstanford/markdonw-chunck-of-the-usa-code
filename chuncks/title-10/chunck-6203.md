
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 361— CONTRACT ADMINISTRATION
#### § 4603. Advance notification of contract performance outside the United States
###### (c) Availability of Notifications .

The Secretary of Defense shall ensure that the notifications (or copies) are maintained in compiled form for a period of 5 years after the date of submission and are available for use in the preparation of the national defense technology and industrial base assessment carried out under section 4816 of this title .
