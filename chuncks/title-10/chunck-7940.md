
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle D— Air Force and Space Force
### CHAPTER 961— CIVIL RESERVE AIR FLEET
#### § 9516. Airlift service
###### (d) Exception .

Subject to subsection (e), when the Secretary of Defense decides that no air carrier holding a certificate under section 41102 of title 49 is capable of providing, and willing to provide, the airlift service, the Secretary of Defense may make a contract to provide the service with an air carrier not having a certificate.
