
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 203— GENERAL MATTERS
#### § 3068. Inapplicability of certain laws
###### (a) Laws Inapplicable to Agencies Named in Section 3063.

Sections 6101 and 6304 of title 41 do not apply to the procurement or sale of property or services by the agencies named in section 3063 of this title .
