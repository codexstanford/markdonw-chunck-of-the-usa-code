
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 21— DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE INTELLIGENCE MATTERS
#### § 430a. Executive agent for management and oversight of alternative compensatory control measures
###### (a) Executive Agent .

The Secretary of Defense shall designate a senior official from among the personnel of the Department of Defense to act as the Department of Defense executive agent for the management and oversight of alternative compensatory control measures.
