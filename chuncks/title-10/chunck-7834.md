
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle D— Air Force and Space Force
### CHAPTER 953— UNITED STATES AIR FORCE ACADEMY
#### § 9442a. Cadets: nomination in event of death, resignation, or expulsion from office of Member of Congress otherwise authorized to nominate
###### (c) Rule of Construction .

The nomination of a cadet by a Member of Congress pursuant to this section shall not be construed to permanently reallocate nominations under section 9442 of this title .
