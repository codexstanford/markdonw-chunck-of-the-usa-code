
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 88— MILITARY FAMILY PROGRAMS AND MILITARY CHILD CARE
#### § 1789. Chaplain-led programs: authorized support
###### (b) Authorized Support Services .

The support services referred to in subsection (a) are costs of transportation, food, lodging, child care, supplies, fees, and training materials for a covered individual while participating in a program referred to in that subsection, including participation at a retreat or conference.
