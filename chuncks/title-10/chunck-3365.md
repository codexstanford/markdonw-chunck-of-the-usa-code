
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 83— CIVILIAN DEFENSE INTELLIGENCE EMPLOYEES
#### § 1602. Basic pay
###### (b) Prevailing Rate Systems .

The Secretary of Defense may, consistent with section 5341 of title 5 , adopt such provisions of that title as provide for prevailing rate systems of basic pay and may apply those provisions to positions for civilian employees in or under which the Department of Defense may employ individuals described by section 5342(a)(2)(A) of that title.
