
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle C— Navy and Marine Corps
### CHAPTER 863— NAVAL VESSELS
#### § 8678a. Limitation on decommissioning or inactivating a battle force ship before the end of expected service life
###### (a) Limitation .

The Secretary of the Navy may not decommission or inactivate a battle force ship before the end of the expected service life of the ship.
