
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle E— Reserve Components
### CHAPTER 1407— FAILURE OF SELECTION FOR PROMOTION AND INVOLUNTARY SEPARATION
#### § 14502a. Special selection review boards
###### (h) Promotion Board Defined .

In this section, the term “promotion board” means a selection board convened by the Secretary of a military department under section 14101(a) of this title .
