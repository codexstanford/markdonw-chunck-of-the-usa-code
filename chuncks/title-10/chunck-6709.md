
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle B— Army
### CHAPTER 753— UNITED STATES MILITARY ACADEMY
#### § 7457. Acceptance of guarantees with gifts for major projects
###### (d) Prohibition on Commingling of Funds .

The Secretary of the Army may not enter into any contract or other transaction involving the use of a qualified guarantee and appropriated funds in the same contract or transaction.
