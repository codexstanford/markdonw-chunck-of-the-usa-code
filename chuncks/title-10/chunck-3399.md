
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 83— CIVILIAN DEFENSE INTELLIGENCE EMPLOYEES
#### § 1612. Merit system principles and civil service protections: applicability
###### (a) Applicability of Merit System Principles .

Section 2301 of title 5 shall apply to the exercise of authority under this subchapter (other than sections 1605 and 1611).
