
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 113— DEFENSE CIVILIAN TRAINING CORPS
#### § 2200g. Establishment

(a) 1 The Secretary of Defense, acting through the Under Secretary for Defense for Acquisition and Sustainment, shall establish and maintain a Defense Civilian Training Corps program, organized into one or more units, at any accredited civilian educational institution authorized to grant baccalaureate degrees.
