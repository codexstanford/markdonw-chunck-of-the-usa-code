
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 303— RESEARCH AND ENGINEERING ACTIVITIES
#### § 4094. 1 Enhanced pay authority for certain research and technology positions in science and technology reinvention laboratories
###### (f) Science and Technology Reinvention Laboratories of the Department of Defense Defined .

In this section, the term “science and technology reinvention laboratories of the Department of Defense” means the laboratories designated as science and technology reinvention laboratories by section 4121(b) of this title .
