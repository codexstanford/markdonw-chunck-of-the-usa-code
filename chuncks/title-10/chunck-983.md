
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 24— NUCLEAR POSTURE
#### § 493a. Industrial base monitoring for B–21 and Sentinel programs
###### (c) Annual Report .

At the same time as the submission of the budget of the President pursuant to section 1105(a) of title 31 for a fiscal year, the Secretary shall submit to the congressional defense committees a report with respect to the status of the combined industrial base described in subsection (a).
