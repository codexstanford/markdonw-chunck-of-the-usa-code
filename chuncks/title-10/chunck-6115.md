
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 325— COST GROWTH—UNIT COST REPORTS (NUNN-MCCURDY)
#### § 4372. Unit cost reports: quarterly report from program manager to service acquisition executive
###### (a) Required Reports.

(1) Requirement .

The program manager for a major defense acquisition program (other than a program not required to be included in the Selected Acquisition Report for that quarter under section 4351(b)(3) of this title ) shall, on a quarterly basis, submit to the service acquisition executive designated by the Secretary concerned a written report on the unit costs of the program (or of each designated major subprogram under the program).

(2) Time for submittal .

Each report shall be submitted not more than 30 calendar days after the end of that quarter.
