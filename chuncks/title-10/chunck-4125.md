
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 135— SPACE PROGRAMS
#### § 2279b. Council on Oversight of the Department of Defense Positioning, Navigation, and Timing Enterprise
###### (h) Termination .

The Council shall terminate on the date that is 10 years after the date of the enactment of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2022.
