
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 301— RESEARCH AND ENGINEERING GENERALLY
#### § 4025. Prizes for advanced technology achievements
###### (b) Competition Requirements .

Each program under subsection (a) shall use a competitive process for the selection of recipients of cash prizes and for the selection of recipients of procurement contracts and other agreements. The process shall include the widely-advertised solicitation of submissions.
