
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 165— ACCOUNTABILITY AND RESPONSIBILITY
#### § 2789. Individual equipment: unauthorized disposition
###### (a) Prohibition .

No member of the armed forces may sell, lend, pledge, barter, or give any clothing, arms, or equipment furnished to such member by the United States to any person other than a member of the armed forces or an officer of the United States who is authorized to receive it.
