
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 3— GENERAL POWERS AND FUNCTIONS
#### § 127e. Support of special operations to combat terrorism
###### (a) Authority .

The Secretary of Defense may, with the concurrence of the relevant Chief of Mission, expend up to $100,000,000 during any fiscal year to provide support to foreign forces, irregular forces, groups, or individuals engaged in supporting or facilitating authorized ongoing military operations by United States special operations forces to combat terrorism.
