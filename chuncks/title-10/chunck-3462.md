
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 87— DEFENSE ACQUISITION WORKFORCE
#### § 1725. Senior Military Acquisition Advisors
###### (a) Position.

(1) In general .

The Secretary of Defense may establish in the acquisition workforce a position to be known as “Senior Military Acquisition Advisor”.

(2) Appointment .

A Senior Military Acquisition Advisor shall be appointed by the President, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate.

(3) Scope of position .

An officer who is appointed as a Senior Military Acquisition Advisor

(A) shall serve as an advisor to, and provide senior level acquisition expertise to, the service acquisition executive of that officer’s military department in accordance with this section; and

(B) shall be assigned as an adjunct professor at the Defense Acquisition University.
