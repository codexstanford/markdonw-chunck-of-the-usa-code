
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 155— ACCEPTANCE OF GIFTS AND SERVICES
#### § 2601a. Direct acceptance of gifts by members of the armed forces and Department of Defense and Coast Guard employees and their families
###### (c) Covered Employees .

This section applies to a civilian employee of the Department of Defense or Coast Guard who, while an employee on or after September 11, 2001 , incurred an injury or illness under a circumstance described in subsection (b).
