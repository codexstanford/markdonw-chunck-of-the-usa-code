
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 81— CIVILIAN EMPLOYEES
#### § 1599j. Restricted reports of incidents of adult sexual assault
###### (c) Duties Under Other Laws .

The receipt of a restricted report submitted under subsection (a) shall not be construed as imputing actual or constructive knowledge of an alleged incident of sexual assault to the Department of Defense for any purpose.
