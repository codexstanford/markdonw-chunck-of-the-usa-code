
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 160— ENVIRONMENTAL RESTORATION
#### § 2708. Contracts for handling hazardous waste from defense facilities
###### (c) Exception to Reimbursement Requirement .

Notwithstanding subsection (a), in the case of any contract to which this section applies, if the Secretary of Defense or the Secretary of the military department concerned determines that

(1) there is only one responsible offeror or there is no responsible offeror willing to provide the reimbursement required by subsection (a) for such contract; or

(2) failure to award the contract would place the facility concerned in violation of any requirement of the Solid Waste Disposal Act ( 42 U.S.C. 6901 et seq.), then the contract may be awarded without including the reimbursement provision required by subsection (a).
