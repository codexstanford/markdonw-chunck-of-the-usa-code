
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 301— RESEARCH AND ENGINEERING GENERALLY
#### § 4023. Procurement for experimental purposes
###### (b) Procedures .

Purchases under this section may be made inside or outside the United States and by contract or otherwise. Chapter 137  1 of this title applies only when such purchases are made in quantities greater than necessary for experimentation, technical evaluation, assessment of operational utility, or safety or to provide a residual operational capability.
