
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 69— RETIRED GRADE
#### § 1370. Regular commissioned officers
###### (g) Highest Permanent Grade Defined .

In this section, the term “highest permanent grade” means a grade at or below the grade of major general in the Army, Air Force, or Marine Corps, rear admiral in the Navy, or an equivalent grade in the Space Force.
