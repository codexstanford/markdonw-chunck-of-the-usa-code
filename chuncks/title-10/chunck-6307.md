
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 383— DEVELOPMENT, APPLICATION, AND SUPPORT OF DUAL-USE TECHNOLOGIES
#### § 4833. Federal Defense Laboratory Diversification Program
###### (a) Establishment of Program .

The Secretary of Defense shall conduct a program in accordance with this section for the purpose of promoting cooperation between Department of Defense laboratories and industry on research and development of dual-use technologies in order to further the national security objectives set forth in section 4811(a) of this title .
