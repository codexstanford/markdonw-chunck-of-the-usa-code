
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 222— INDEPENDENT COST ESTIMATION AND COST ANALYSIS
#### § 3227. Guidelines and collection method for acquisition of cost data
###### (b) Applicability to Acquisition Programs in Amount Greater Than Specified Threshold .

The program manager and contracting officer for each acquisition program in an amount greater than $100,000,000, in consultation with the cost estimating component of the relevant military department or Defense Agency, shall ensure that cost data are collected in accordance with the requirements of subsection (a).
