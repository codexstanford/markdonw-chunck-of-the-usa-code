
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 245— TASK AND DELIVERY ORDER CONTRACTS (MULTIPLE AWARD CONTRACTS)
#### § 3403. Task and delivery order contracts: general authority
###### (a) Authority To Award .

Subject to the requirements of this section, section 3406 of this title , and other applicable law, the head of an agency may enter into a task or delivery order contract (as defined in section 3401 of this title ) for procurement of services or property.
