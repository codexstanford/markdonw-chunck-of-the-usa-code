
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle E— Reserve Components
### CHAPTER 1209— ACTIVE DUTY
#### § 12304b. Selected Reserve: order to active duty for preplanned missions in support of the combatant commands
###### (a) Authority .

When the Secretary of a military department determines that it is necessary to augment the active forces for a preplanned mission in support of a combatant command, the Secretary may, subject to subsection (b), order any unit of the Selected Reserve (as defined in section 10143(a) of this title ), without the consent of the members, to active duty for not more than 365 consecutive days.
