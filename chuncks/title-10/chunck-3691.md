
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 102— JUNIOR RESERVE OFFICERS’ TRAINING CORPS
#### § 2035. Flexibility in authorities for management of programs and units
###### (d) Standardization of Program Data .

The Secretary of Defense shall take appropriate actions to standardize the data collected and maintained on the Junior Reserve Officers’ Training Corps programs in order to facilitate and enhance the collection and analysis of such data. Such actions shall include a requirement for the use of the National Center for Education Statistics (NCES) identification code for each school with a unit under a Junior Reserve Officers’ Training Corps program in order to facilitate identification of such schools and their units under the Junior Reserve Officers’ Training Corps programs.
