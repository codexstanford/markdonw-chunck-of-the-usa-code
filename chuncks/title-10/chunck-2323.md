
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 55— MEDICAL AND DENTAL CARE
#### § 1074n. Annual mental health assessments for members of the armed forces
###### (a) Mental Health Assessments .

Subject to subsection (c), not less frequently than once each calendar year (and before separation from active duty pursuant to section 1145(a)(5)(A) of this title ), the Secretary of Defense shall provide a person-to-person mental health assessment for

(1) each member of a regular component of the armed forces; and

(2) each member of the Selected Reserve of an armed force.
