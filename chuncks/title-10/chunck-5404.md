
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 205— DEFENSE ACQUISITION SYSTEM
#### § 3106. Elements of the defense acquisition system: performance goals
###### (b) Reporting Requirement .

The annual report prepared by the Secretary of Defense pursuant to section 1116 of title 31 shall address the Department’s success in achieving performance goals established pursuant to such section for elements of the defense acquisition system.
