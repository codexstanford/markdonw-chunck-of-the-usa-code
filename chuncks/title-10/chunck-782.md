
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 19— CYBER AND INFORMATION OPERATIONS MATTERS
#### § 394. Authorities concerning military cyber operations
###### (d) Congressional Oversight .

The Secretary shall brief the congressional defense committees about any military activities or operations in cyberspace, including clandestine military activities or operations in cyberspace, occurring during the previous quarter during the quarterly briefing required by section 484 of this title .
