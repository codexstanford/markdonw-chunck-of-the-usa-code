
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 322— MAJOR SYSTEMS AND MAJOR DEFENSE ACQUISITION PROGRAMS GENERALLY
#### § 4252. Major defense acquisition programs: certification required before Milestone B approval
###### (f) Nondelegation .

The milestone decision authority may not delegate the certification requirement under subsection (a) or the authority to waive any component of such requirement under subsection (d).
