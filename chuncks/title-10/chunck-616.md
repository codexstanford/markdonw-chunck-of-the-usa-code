
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 16— SECURITY COOPERATION
#### § 313. Bilateral or regional cooperation programs: awards and mementos to recognize superior noncombat achievements or performance
###### (c) Limitation .

Expenditures for the purchase or production of mementos for award under this section may not exceed the minimal value in effect under section 7342(a)(5) of title 5 .
