
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 49— MISCELLANEOUS PROHIBITIONS AND PENALTIES
#### § 985. Persons convicted of capital crimes; certain other persons: denial of specified burial-related benefits
###### (b) Disqualification From Burial in Military Cemeteries .

Except as provided in subsection (c), a person who is ineligible for interment in a national cemetery under the control of the National Cemetery Administration by reason of section 2411(b) of title 38 is not entitled to or eligible for, and may not be provided, burial in

(1) Arlington National Cemetery;

(2) the Soldiers’ and Airmen’s National Cemetery; or

(3) any other cemetery administered by the Secretary of a military department or the Secretary of Defense.
