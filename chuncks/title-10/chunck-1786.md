
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 47— UNIFORM CODE OF MILITARY JUSTICE
#### § 931a. Art. 131a. Subornation of perjury
###### (a) In General .

Any person subject to this chapter who induces and procures another person

(1) to take an oath; and

(2) to falsely testify, depose, or state upon such oath; shall, if the conditions specified in subsection (b) are satisfied, be punished as a court-martial may direct.
