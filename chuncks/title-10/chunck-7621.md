### CHAPTER 893— NATIONAL OCEANOGRAPHIC PARTNERSHIP PROGRAM
#### § 8933. Ocean Research Advisory Panel
###### (e) Termination .

Notwithstanding section 14 of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App.), the Advisory Panel shall terminate on January 1, 2040 .
