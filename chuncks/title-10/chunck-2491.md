
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 55— MEDICAL AND DENTAL CARE
#### § 1097a. TRICARE Prime: automatic enrollments
###### (b) Automatic Renewal of Enrollments of Covered Beneficiaries .

An enrollment of a covered beneficiary in TRICARE Prime shall be automatically renewed upon the expiration of the enrollment unless the renewal is declined.
