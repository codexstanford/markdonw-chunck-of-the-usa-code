
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle B— Army
### CHAPTER 773— DISPOSAL OF OBSOLETE OR SURPLUS MATERIAL
#### § 7687. Sale of excess, obsolete, or unserviceable ammunition and ammunition components
###### (b) Method of Sale .

The Secretary shall use competitive procedures to sell ammunition and ammunition components under this section, except that the Secretary may use procedures other than competitive procedures in any case in which the Secretary determines that there is only one potential buyer of the items being offered for sale.
