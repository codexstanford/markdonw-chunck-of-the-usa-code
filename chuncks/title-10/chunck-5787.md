
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 277— CONTRACT FINANCING
#### § 3808. Certain Navy contracts
###### (b) Authority to Advance Funds for Immediate Salvage Operations .

The Secretary of the Navy may advance to private salvage companies such funds as the Secretary considers necessary to provide for the immediate financing of salvage operations. Advances under this subsection shall be made on terms that the Secretary considers adequate for the protection of the United States.
