
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 222— INDEPENDENT COST ESTIMATION AND COST ANALYSIS
#### § 3227. Guidelines and collection method for acquisition of cost data
###### (a) Director of Cape to Develop Guidelines and Collection Method .

The Director of Cost Assessment and Program Evaluation shall, in consultation with the Under Secretary of Defense for Acquisition and Sustainment, develop policies, procedures, guidance, and a collection method to ensure that quality acquisition cost data are collected to facilitate cost estimation and comparison across acquisition programs.
