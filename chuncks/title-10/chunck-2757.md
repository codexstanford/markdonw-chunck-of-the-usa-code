
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 61— RETIREMENT OR SEPARATION FOR PHYSICAL DISABILITY
#### § 1203. Regulars and members on active duty for more than 30 days: separation
###### (a) Separation .

Upon a determination by the Secretary concerned that a member described in section 1201(c) of this title is unfit to perform the duties of the member’s office, grade, rank, or rating because of physical disability incurred while entitled to basic pay or while absent as described in section 1201(c)(3) of this title , the member may be separated from the member’s armed force, with severance pay computed under section 1212 of this title , if the Secretary also makes the determinations with respect to the member and that disability specified in subsection (b).
