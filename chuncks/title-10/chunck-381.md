
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 7— BOARDS, COUNCILS, AND COMMITTEES
#### § 183a. Military Aviation and Installation Assurance Clearinghouse for review of mission obstructions
###### (f) Authority to Accept Contributions of Funds .

The Secretary of Defense is authorized to request and accept a voluntary contribution of funds from an applicant for an energy project. Amounts so accepted shall remain available until expended for the purpose of offsetting the cost of measures undertaken by the Secretary of Defense to mitigate adverse impacts of such a project on military operations and readiness or to conduct studies of potential measures to mitigate such impacts.
