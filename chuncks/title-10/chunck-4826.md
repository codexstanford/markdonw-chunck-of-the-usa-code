
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 160— ENVIRONMENTAL RESTORATION
#### § 2704. Commonly found unregulated hazardous substances
###### (f) Functions of HHS To Be Carried Out Through ATSDR .

The functions of the Secretary of Health and Human Services under this section shall be carried out through the Administrator of the Agency for Toxic Substances and Disease Registry of the Department of Health and Human Services established under section 104(i) of CERCLA ( 42 U.S.C. 9604(i) ).
