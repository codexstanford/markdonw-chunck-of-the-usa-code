
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 205— DEFENSE ACQUISITION SYSTEM
#### § 3106. Elements of the defense acquisition system: performance goals
###### (a) Performance Goals Under Government Performance and Results Act of 1993.

The annual performance plan prepared by the Department of Defense pursuant to section 1115 of title 31 shall include appropriate performance goals for elements of the defense acquisition system.
