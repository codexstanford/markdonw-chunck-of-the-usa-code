
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 203— GENERAL MATTERS
#### § 3064. Applicability of chapter 137 legacy provisions
###### (a) General Applicability .

Any provision of this part that is a chapter 137 legacy provision applies to the procurement by any of the agencies named in section 3063 of this title , for its use or otherwise, of all property (other than land) and all services for which payment is to be made from appropriated funds.
