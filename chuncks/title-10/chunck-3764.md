
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 105— ARMED FORCES HEALTH PROFESSIONS FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE PROGRAMS
#### § 2128. Accession bonus for members of the program
###### (c) Repayment .

A person who receives an accession bonus under this section, but fails to comply with the agreement under section 2122(a)(2) of this title or to commence or complete the active duty obligation imposed by section 2123 of this title , shall be subject to the repayment provisions of section 303a(e) or 373 of title 37.
