
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 6— COMBATANT COMMANDS
#### § 164. Commanders of combatant commands: assignment; powers and duties
###### (h) Support to Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff .

The commander of a combatant command shall provide such information to the Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff as may be necessary for the Chairman to perform the duties of the Chairman under section 153 of this title .
