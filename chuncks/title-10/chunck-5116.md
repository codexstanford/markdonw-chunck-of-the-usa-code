
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 169— MILITARY CONSTRUCTION AND MILITARY FAMILY HOUSING
#### § 2861. Military construction projects in connection with industrial facility investment program
###### (a) Authority .

The Secretary of Defense may carry out a military construction project, not previously authorized, for the purpose of carrying out activities under section 2474(a)(2) of this title , using funds appropriated or otherwise made available for that purpose in military construction accounts.
