
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 157— TRANSPORTATION
#### § 2645. Indemnification of Department of Transportation for losses covered by vessel war risk insurance
###### (c) Deposit of Funds .

Any amount transferred to the Secretary of Transportation under this section shall be deposited in, and merged with amounts in, the Vessel War Risk Insurance Fund as provided in section 53909(b) of title 46 .
