
# Title 10— ARMED FORCES
## Subtitle A— General Military Law
### CHAPTER 203— GENERAL MATTERS
#### § 3072. Comptroller General assessment of acquisition programs and efforts
###### (a) Assessment Required .

The Comptroller General of the United States shall submit to the congressional defense committees an annual assessment of selected acquisition programs and efforts of the Department of Defense by March 30th of each year from 2020 through 2026.
