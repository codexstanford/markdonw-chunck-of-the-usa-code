
# Title 20— EDUCATION
### CHAPTER 70— STRENGTHENING AND IMPROVEMENT OF ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY SCHOOLS
#### § 6337. Education finance incentive grant program
##### (c) Use of funds; eligibility of local educational agencies

All funds awarded to each State under this section shall be allocated to local educational agencies under the following provisions. Within local educational agencies, funds allocated under this section shall be distributed to schools on a basis consistent with section 6313 of this title , and may only be used to carry out activities under this part. A local educational agency in a State is eligible to receive a targeted grant under this section for any fiscal year if

(1) the number of children in the local educational agency counted under section 6333(c) of this title , before application of the weighted child count described in paragraph (3), 1 is at least 10; and

(2) if the number of children counted for grants under section 6333(c) of this title , before application of the weighted child count described in paragraph (3), 1 is at least 5 percent of the total number of children aged 5 to 17 years, inclusive, in the school district of the local educational agency. For any fiscal year for which the Secretary allocates funds under this section on the basis of counties, funds made available as a result of applying this subsection shall be reallocated by the State educational agency to other eligible local educational agencies in the State in proportion to the distribution of other funds under this section.
