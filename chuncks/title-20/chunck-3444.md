
# Title 20— EDUCATION
### CHAPTER 59— BARRY GOLDWATER SCHOLARSHIP AND EXCELLENCE IN EDUCATION PROGRAM
#### § 4704. Barry Goldwater scholarship and excellence in education awards
##### (b) Barry Goldwater Scholars and Research Interns

Recipients of scholarships and fellowships under this chapter shall be known as “Barry Goldwater Scholars”. Recipients of research internships under this chapter shall be known as “Barry Goldwater Interns”.
