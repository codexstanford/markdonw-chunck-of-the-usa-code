
# Title 20— EDUCATION
### CHAPTER 28— HIGHER EDUCATION RESOURCES AND STUDENT ASSISTANCE
#### § 1071. Statement of purpose; nondiscrimination; and appropriations authorized
##### (d) Termination of authority to make or insure new loans

Notwithstanding paragraphs (1) through (6) of subsection (b) or any other provision of law

(1) no new loans (including consolidation loans) may be made or insured under this part after June 30, 2010 ; and

(2) no funds are authorized to be appropriated, or may be expended, under this chapter or any other Act to make or insure loans under this part (including consolidation loans) for which the first disbursement is after June 30, 2010 , except as expressly authorized by an Act of Congress enacted after March 30, 2010 .
