
# Title 20— EDUCATION
### CHAPTER 28— HIGHER EDUCATION RESOURCES AND STUDENT ASSISTANCE
#### § 1092a. Combined payment plan
##### (a) Eligibility for plan

Upon the request of the borrower, a lender described in subparagraph (A), (B), or (C) of section 1078–3(a)(1) of this title , or an eligible lender as defined in section 719 of the Public Health Service Act ( 42 U.S.C. 292 o ) may, with respect to a consolidation loan made under section 1078–3 of this title (and section 1087–2( o ) of this title as in effect prior to the enactment of section 1078–3 of this title ) and loans guaranteed under part A of title VII of the Public Health Service Act ( 42 U.S.C. 292 et seq.), offer a combined payment plan under which the lender shall submit one bill to the borrower for the repayment of all such loans for the monthly or other similar period of repayment.
