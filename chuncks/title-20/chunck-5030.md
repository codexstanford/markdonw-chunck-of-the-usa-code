
# Title 20— EDUCATION
### CHAPTER 80— STATE FISCAL STABILIZATION FUND
#### § 10003. Uses of funds by local educational agencies
##### (a) In general

A local educational agency that receives funds under this chapter may use the funds for any activity authorized by the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965 ( 20 U.S.C. 6301 et seq.) (“ESEA”), the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act ( 20 U.S.C. 1400 et seq.) (“IDEA”), the Adult Education and Family Literacy Act ( 20 U.S.C. 9201 et seq.), 1 or the Carl D. Perkins Career and Technical Education Act of 2006 ( 20 U.S.C. 2301 et seq.) (“the Perkins Act”) or for modernization, renovation, or repair of public school facilities, including modernization, renovation, and repairs that are consistent with a recognized green building rating system.
