
# Title 20— EDUCATION
### CHAPTER 48— DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION
#### § 3503. Agency terminations
##### (b) Positions authorized to be compensated at rate prescribed for level IV or V of the Executive Schedule

Each position which was expressly authorized by law, or the incumbent of which was authorized to receive compensation at the rate prescribed for level IV or V of the Executive Schedule ( 5 U.S.C. 5315–531 6), in an office terminated pursuant to this chapter shall also terminate.
