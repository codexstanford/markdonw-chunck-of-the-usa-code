
# Title 20— EDUCATION
### CHAPTER 28— HIGHER EDUCATION RESOURCES AND STUDENT ASSISTANCE
#### § 1140m. Model demonstration programs to support improved access to postsecondary instructional materials for students with print disabilities
##### (c) Program authorized

From amounts appropriated under section 1140 o of this title, the Secretary shall award grants or contracts, on a competitive basis, to not less than one eligible partnership to enable the eligible partnership to support the activities described in subsection (f) and, as applicable, subsection (g).
