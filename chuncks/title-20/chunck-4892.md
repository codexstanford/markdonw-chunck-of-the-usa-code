
# Title 20— EDUCATION
### CHAPTER 76— EDUCATION RESEARCH, STATISTICS, EVALUATION, INFORMATION, AND DISSEMINATION
#### § 9601. Definitions

In this subchapter:

(1) In general

The terms “local educational agency” and “State educational agency” have the meanings given those terms in section 7801 of this title .

(2) Secretary

The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Education.
