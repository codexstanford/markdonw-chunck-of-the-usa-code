
# Title 37— PAY AND ALLOWANCES OF THE UNIFORMED SERVICES
### CHAPTER 7— ALLOWANCES OTHER THAN TRAVEL AND TRANSPORTATION ALLOWANCES
#### § 402b. Basic needs allowance for members on active service in the Armed Forces
###### (j) Effective Period.

(1) Implementation period .

The allowance under subsection (a) is payable for months beginning on or after the date that is one year after the date of the enactment of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2022.

(2) Termination .

The allowance under subsection (a) may not be paid for any month beginning after December 31, 2027 .
