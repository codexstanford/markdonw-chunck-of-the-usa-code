
# Title 37— PAY AND ALLOWANCES OF THE UNIFORMED SERVICES
### CHAPTER 5— SPECIAL AND INCENTIVE PAYS
#### § 356. Continuation pay: full TSP members with 8 to 12 years of service
###### (g) Repayment .

A full TSP member who receives continuation pay under this section (a)  1 and fails to complete the obligated service required under such subsection shall be subject to the repayment provisions of section 373 of this title .
