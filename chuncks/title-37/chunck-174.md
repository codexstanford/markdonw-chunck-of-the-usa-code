
# Title 37— PAY AND ALLOWANCES OF THE UNIFORMED SERVICES
### CHAPTER 5— SPECIAL AND INCENTIVE PAYS
#### § 308c. Special pay: bonus for affiliation or enlistment in the Selected Reserve
###### (i) Termination of Bonus Authority .

No bonus may be paid under this section with respect to any agreement entered into under subsection (a) or (c) after December 31, 2018 .
