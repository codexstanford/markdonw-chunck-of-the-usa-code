
# Title 37— PAY AND ALLOWANCES OF THE UNIFORMED SERVICES
### CHAPTER 5— SPECIAL AND INCENTIVE PAYS
#### § 336. Contracting bonus for cadets and midshipmen enrolled in the Senior Reserve Officers’ Training Corps
###### (a) Contracting Bonus Authorized .

The Secretary concerned may pay a bonus under this section to a cadet or midshipman enrolled in the Senior Reserve Officers’ Training Corps who executes a written agreement described in subsection (c).
