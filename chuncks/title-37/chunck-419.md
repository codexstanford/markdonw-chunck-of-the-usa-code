
# Title 37— PAY AND ALLOWANCES OF THE UNIFORMED SERVICES
### CHAPTER 5— SPECIAL AND INCENTIVE PAYS
#### § 355. Special pay: retention incentives for members qualified in critical military skills or assigned to high priority units
###### (h) Termination of Bonus Authority .

No bonus may be paid under this section with respect to any reenlistment, or voluntary extension of an enlistment, in the armed forces entered into after December 31, 2023 , and no agreement under this section may be entered into after that date.
