
# Title 23— HIGHWAYS
### CHAPTER 1— FEDERAL-AID HIGHWAYS
#### § 169. Development of programmatic mitigation plans
###### (f) Consideration in Project Development and Permitting .

If a programmatic mitigation plan has been developed pursuant to this section, any Federal agency responsible for environmental reviews, permits, or approvals for a transportation project shall give substantial weight to the recommendations in a programmatic mitigation plan when carrying out the responsibilities under the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 ( 42 U.S.C. 4321 et seq.) or other Federal environmental law.
