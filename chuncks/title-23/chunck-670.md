
# Title 23— HIGHWAYS
### CHAPTER 4— HIGHWAY SAFETY
#### § 406. General requirements for Federal assistance
###### (d) Grant Application and Deadline.

(1) Applications .

To be eligible to receive a grant under this chapter, a State shall submit to the Secretary an application at such time, in such manner, and containing such information as the Secretary may require.

(2) Deadline .

The Secretary shall establish a single deadline for the submission of applications under paragraph (1) to enable the provision of grants under this chapter early in each applicable fiscal year beginning after the date of submission.
