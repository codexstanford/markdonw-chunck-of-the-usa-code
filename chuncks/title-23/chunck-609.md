
# Title 23— HIGHWAYS
### CHAPTER 3— GENERAL PROVISIONS
#### § 330. Program for eliminating duplication of environmental reviews
###### (h) Relationship to Locally Administered Projects.

(1) In general .

A State with an approved program under this section, at the request of a local government, may exercise authority under that program on behalf of up to 25 local governments for locally administered projects.

(2) Scope .

For up to 25 local governments selected by a State with an approved program under this section, the State shall be responsible for ensuring that any environmental review, consultation, or other action required under the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 ( 42 U.S.C. 4321 et seq.) or the State program, or both, meets the requirements of such Act or program.
