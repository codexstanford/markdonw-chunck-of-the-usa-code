
# Title 23— HIGHWAYS
### CHAPTER 1— FEDERAL-AID HIGHWAYS
#### § 166. HOV facilities
###### (a) In General.

(1) Authority of public authorities .

A public authority that has jurisdiction over the operation of a HOV facility shall establish the occupancy requirements of vehicles operating on the facility.

(2) Occupancy requirement .

Except as otherwise provided by this section, no fewer than two occupants per vehicle may be required for use of a HOV facility.
