
# Title 23— HIGHWAYS
### CHAPTER 4— HIGHWAY SAFETY
#### § 406. General requirements for Federal assistance
###### (e) Distribution of Funds to States .

Not later than 60 days after the later of the start of a fiscal year or the date of enactment of any appropriations Act making funds available to carry out this chapter for that fiscal year, the Secretary shall distribute to each State the portion of those funds to which the State is entitled for the applicable fiscal year.
