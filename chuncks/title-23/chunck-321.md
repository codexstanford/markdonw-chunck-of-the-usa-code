
# Title 23— HIGHWAYS
### CHAPTER 1— FEDERAL-AID HIGHWAYS
#### § 151. National electric vehicle charging and hydrogen, propane, and natural gas fueling corridors
###### (d) Redesignation.

(1) Initial redesignation .

Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of the Surface Transportation Reauthorization Act of 2021, the Secretary shall update and redesignate the corridors under subsection (a).

(2) Subsequent redesignation .

The Secretary shall establish a recurring process to regularly update and redesignate the corridors under subsection (a).
