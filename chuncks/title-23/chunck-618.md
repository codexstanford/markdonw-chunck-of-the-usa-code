
# Title 23— HIGHWAYS
### CHAPTER 3— GENERAL PROVISIONS
#### § 332. Pollinator-friendly practices on roadsides and highway rights-of-way
###### (c) Application .

To be eligible to receive a grant under this section, an eligible entity shall submit to the Secretary an application at such time, in such manner, and containing such information as the Secretary may require, including a pollinator-friendly practices plan described in subsection (d).
