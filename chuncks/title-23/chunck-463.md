
# Title 23— HIGHWAYS
### CHAPTER 1— FEDERAL-AID HIGHWAYS
#### § 178. Environmental review implementation funds
###### (b) Cost Share.

(1) In general .

The Federal share of the cost of an activity carried out under this section by an eligible entity shall be not more than 80 percent.

(2) Source of funds .

The non-Federal share of the cost of an activity carried out under this section by an eligible entity may be satisfied using funds made available to the eligible entity under any other Federal, State, or local grant program.
