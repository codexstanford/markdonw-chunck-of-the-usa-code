
# Title 23— HIGHWAYS
### CHAPTER 5— RESEARCH, TECHNOLOGY, AND EDUCATION
#### § 505. State planning and research
###### (c) Implementation of Future Strategic Highway Research Program Findings and Results .

(1) Funds .

A State shall make available to the Secretary to carry out section 503(c)(2)(C) a percentage of funds subject to subsection (a) that are apportioned to that State, that is agreed to by ¾ of States for each of fiscal years 2013 and 2014.

(2) Treatment of funds .

Funds expended under paragraph (1) shall not be considered to be part of the extramural budget of the agency for the purpose of section 9 of the Small Business Act ( 15 U.S.C. 638 ).
