
# Title 51— NATIONAL AND COMMERCIAL SPACE PROGRAMS
## Subtitle V— Programs Targeting Commercial Opportunities
### CHAPTER 509— COMMERCIAL SPACE LAUNCH ACTIVITIES
#### § 50909. Prohibition, suspension, and end of launches, operation of launch sites and reentry sites, and reentries
###### (b) Effective Periods of Orders .

An order under this section takes effect immediately and remains in effect during a review under section 50912 of this title .
