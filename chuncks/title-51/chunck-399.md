
# Title 51— NATIONAL AND COMMERCIAL SPACE PROGRAMS
## Subtitle V— Programs Targeting Commercial Opportunities
### CHAPTER 509— COMMERCIAL SPACE LAUNCH ACTIVITIES
#### § 50910. Preemption of scheduled launches or reentries
###### (b) Imperative National Need Decisions .

In consultation with the Secretary of Transportation, the Secretary of Defense or the Administrator shall decide when an imperative national need requires preemption under subsection (a) of this section. That decision may not be delegated.
