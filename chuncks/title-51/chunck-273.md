
# Title 51— NATIONAL AND COMMERCIAL SPACE PROGRAMS
## Subtitle IV— Aeronautics and Space Research and Education
### CHAPTER 403— NATIONAL SPACE GRANT COLLEGE AND FELLOWSHIP PROGRAM
#### § 40308. Space grant review panel
###### (a) Establishment .

The Administrator shall establish an independent committee known as the space grant review panel, which shall not be subject to the provisions of chapter 10 of title 5.
