
# Title 51— NATIONAL AND COMMERCIAL SPACE PROGRAMS
## Subtitle VI— Earth Observations
### CHAPTER 601— LAND REMOTE SENSING POLICY
#### § 60132. Availability of federally gathered unenhanced data
###### (a) In General .

All unenhanced land remote sensing data gathered and owned by the United States Government, including unenhanced data gathered under the technology demonstration program carried out pursuant to section 60133 of this title , shall be made available to users in a timely fashion.
