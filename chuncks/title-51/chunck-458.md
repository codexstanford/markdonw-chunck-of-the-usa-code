
# Title 51— NATIONAL AND COMMERCIAL SPACE PROGRAMS
## Subtitle V— Programs Targeting Commercial Opportunities
### CHAPTER 513— SPACE RESOURCE COMMERCIAL EXPLORATION AND UTILIZATION
#### § 51301. Definitions

In this chapter:

(1) Asteroid resource .

The term “asteroid resource” means a space resource found on or within a single asteroid.

(2) Space resource.

(A) In general .

The term “space resource” means an abiotic resource in situ in outer space.

(B) Inclusions .

The term “space resource” includes water and minerals.

(3) United states citizen .

The term “United States citizen” has the meaning given the term “citizen of the United States” in section 50902.
